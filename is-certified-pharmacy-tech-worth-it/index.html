Is Certified Pharmacy Tech Worth It

Written by Petter May 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering a career as a pharmacy technician, you might be wondering whether getting certified is worth it. With so many training programs and certification options available, it can be tough to know where to start. In this blog post, we'll take a closer look at the benefits of becoming a certified pharmacy technician, and help you decide whether it's the right choice for you.

The Pain Points of Not Being Certified

Without certification, you may find it harder to get hired, receive promotions or salary increases, and have fewer job opportunities available to you. Additionally, certification can give you a competitive edge over other applicants and demonstrate your commitment to the profession.

The Answer

Yes, becoming a certified pharmacy technician is absolutely worth it. Certification can lead to higher pay, more job opportunities, and greater job security. Additionally, it can give you the skills and knowledge you need to provide better patient care and make a positive impact on the healthcare industry.

Summary of Main Points

Becoming a certified pharmacy technician can lead to higher pay, more job opportunities, greater job security, and better patient care. Certification demonstrates your commitment to the profession and gives you a competitive edge over other applicants.

What is Certified Pharmacy Tech?

A certified pharmacy technician is someone who has completed a training program and passed a certification exam. Certification programs are offered by a number of organizations, including the Pharmacy Technician Certification Board (PTCB) and the National Healthcareer Association (NHA).

Personally, I found that getting certified as a pharmacy technician not only helped me get a better job, but also gave me the confidence and knowledge to provide better care for patients. It's definitely worth considering if you're looking to advance your career in pharmacy.

How to Get Certified

To become a certified pharmacy technician, you'll need to complete a training program and pass a certification exam. Training programs can be found at community colleges, vocational schools, and online programs. Once you've completed your training, you can register for the certification exam through the PTCB or NHA.

Benefits of Certification

There are many benefits to becoming a certified pharmacy technician, including:

  • Higher pay
  • More job opportunities
  • Greater job security
  • Improved patient care
  • Professional recognition

How to Maintain Certification

Once you've become certified, you'll need to maintain your certification by completing continuing education courses and renewing your certification every two years. This ensures that you stay up-to-date with the latest developments in the field and maintain your professional status.

Personal Experience with Certification

When I first started working as a pharmacy technician, I wasn't certified. I quickly realized that certification could help me advance my career and provide better care for patients, so I enrolled in a training program and took the certification exam. Since then, I've noticed a significant improvement in my job prospects and salary, and I feel much more confident in my abilities as a pharmacy technician.

Questions and Answers

Q: What is the difference between a certified and non-certified pharmacy technician?

A: A certified pharmacy technician has completed a training program and passed a certification exam, while a non-certified technician has not. Certification can lead to higher pay, more job opportunities, and greater job security.

Q: How long does it take to become a certified pharmacy technician?

A: The length of time it takes to become certified can vary depending on the training program you choose. Most programs take between 6 months and 2 years to complete.

Q: How much does it cost to become a certified pharmacy technician?

A: The cost of certification can vary depending on the training program and exam fees. On average, you can expect to spend between $500 and $1,500 to become certified.

Q: Is certification required to work as a pharmacy technician?

A: Certification is not required to work as a pharmacy technician, but it can lead to higher pay, more job opportunities, and greater job security.

Conclusion of Is Certified Pharmacy Tech Worth It

If you're considering a career as a pharmacy technician, getting certified is definitely worth it. Certification can lead to higher pay, more job opportunities, greater job security, and better patient care. By investing in your education and professional development, you can position yourself for a successful and fulfilling career in the healthcare industry.
