How To Choose A Mechanical Keyboard For Your Interior Design Workspace

Written by William Jun 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of using a regular keyboard for your interior design projects? Do you want to improve your typing speed and accuracy? If yes, then it's time to upgrade to a mechanical keyboard. Mechanical keyboards are known for their tactile feedback and durability, making them a popular choice among gamers, coders, and designers.

However, with so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right mechanical keyboard for your needs. It's important to consider factors such as the type of switches, layout, and customization options before making a decision.

To choose the perfect mechanical keyboard, follow these simple steps:

Determine Your Typing Needs and Preferences

Before buying a mechanical keyboard, it's important to consider your typing needs and preferences. Do you prefer a soft or firm typing experience? Are you a fast or slow typist? Do you need a keyboard with programmable keys? These questions will help you determine the type of switches and layout that would suit your needs best.

For instance, if you prefer a soft and quiet typing experience, you may want to choose a keyboard with Cherry MX Brown switches. On the other hand, if you prefer a firm and tactile typing experience, you may want to choose a keyboard with Cherry MX Blue switches.

Consider the Size and Layout

Another important factor to consider is the size and layout of the keyboard. Do you need a full-sized keyboard with a number pad or a compact keyboard without one? Do you need a keyboard with a wrist rest? These factors will help you determine the size and layout of the keyboard that would fit your workspace best.

For instance, if you have limited desk space, you may want to choose a compact keyboard without a number pad. On the other hand, if you type for long hours, you may want to choose a keyboard with a wrist rest for added comfort.

Customization Options

Many mechanical keyboards come with customization options such as programmable keys, RGB lighting, and detachable cables. These options can enhance your typing experience and allow you to personalize your keyboard according to your preferences.

For instance, if you use specific shortcuts for your design projects, you can program them onto the keyboard for easy access. Similarly, if you prefer a specific color scheme for your workspace, you can choose a keyboard with RGB lighting to match it.

Maintenance and Durability

Finally, it's important to consider the maintenance and durability of the keyboard. Mechanical keyboards are known for their longevity and can last for several years with proper care. However, it's important to keep the keyboard clean and free from dust and debris to ensure its longevity.

Additionally, it's important to choose a keyboard with a durable frame and keycaps that can withstand heavy use. This will ensure that your keyboard lasts for a long time and provides a comfortable typing experience.

Personal Experience

As an interior designer, I spend a lot of time typing and designing on my computer. I recently upgraded to a mechanical keyboard with Cherry MX Brown switches and a compact layout. This has improved my typing speed and accuracy, and the soft typing experience has reduced the strain on my fingers. Additionally, the keyboard's RGB lighting adds a pop of color to my workspace and enhances my overall design aesthetic.

FAQs

What are the best switches for a mechanical keyboard?

It depends on your typing needs and preferences. Cherry MX Brown switches are suitable for soft and quiet typing, while Cherry MX Blue switches are suitable for firm and tactile typing. Other popular switches include Cherry MX Red, Black, and Speed.

What is the difference between a full-sized and compact keyboard?

A full-sized keyboard includes a number pad, while a compact keyboard does not. Compact keyboards are smaller in size and take up less desk space. They are also more travel-friendly and easier to carry around.

What are programmable keys?

Programmable keys allow you to assign specific functions or shortcuts to individual keys on the keyboard. This can save time and increase productivity, especially for tasks that require repeated use of certain commands.

How do I clean my mechanical keyboard?

You can clean your mechanical keyboard by using a soft-bristled brush or compressed air to remove dust and debris from the switches and keycaps. You can also use a cleaning solution and a microfiber cloth to wipe down the keyboard's surface.

Conclusion of How to Choose a Mechanical Keyboard

Choosing a mechanical keyboard can enhance your typing experience and improve your productivity as an interior designer. By considering factors such as switches, layout, customization options, and durability, you can choose a keyboard that suits your needs and preferences. With proper care and maintenance, your mechanical keyboard can last for several years and provide a comfortable and enjoyable typing experience.
