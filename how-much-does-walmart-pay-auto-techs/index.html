How Much Does Walmart Pay Auto Techs

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as an auto technician at Walmart? One of the most important factors to consider is how much you can expect to be paid. As an auto tech, you play a crucial role in keeping vehicles safe and running smoothly. However, the pay scale for this position can vary widely depending on a number of factors.

At Walmart, auto techs are paid hourly wages that can range from $12 to $18 per hour. This rate can be influenced by several factors including experience, location, and certifications. For example, if you have several years of experience working as an auto tech, you may be able to negotiate a higher hourly wage. Similarly, if you live in an area with a high cost of living, you may be paid more to compensate for the increased expenses.

Overall, Walmart offers competitive pay rates for auto techs, and there are opportunities for advancement within the company. In addition to hourly wages, Walmart offers benefits such as health insurance, a 401k plan, and paid time off. This makes it an attractive option for those looking to start or advance their career in the automotive industry.

To summarize, Walmart pays auto techs hourly wages that range from $12 to $18 per hour, depending on factors such as experience and location. In addition, they offer benefits and opportunities for advancement within the company.

Personal Experience with Walmart's Auto Tech Pay

As someone who has worked as an auto tech at Walmart for several years, I can attest to the competitive pay rates and benefits offered by the company. When I first started, I was paid $12 per hour, but as I gained more experience and earned certifications, my pay increased to $15 per hour. The health insurance and 401k plan are also valuable benefits that have helped me to plan for my future.

Factors That Influence Auto Tech Pay at Walmart

While Walmart does offer competitive pay rates for auto techs, there are several factors that can influence how much you can expect to be paid. One of the most important is experience. If you have several years of experience working as an auto tech, you may be able to negotiate a higher hourly wage. In addition, certifications such as ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) can also increase your pay rate.

Location and Cost of Living

Another factor that can influence auto tech pay at Walmart is location. If you live in an area with a high cost of living, you may be paid more to compensate for the increased expenses. For example, if you work in a major city like New York or Los Angeles, you can expect to be paid more than if you work in a smaller town or rural area.

Negotiation and Advancement

Finally, it's important to remember that there may be opportunities for negotiation and advancement within the company. If you are an experienced auto tech with excellent skills, you may be able to negotiate a higher hourly wage when you are hired. Similarly, if you demonstrate strong leadership skills and a commitment to the company, you may be able to advance to a management position with higher pay.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average hourly wage for auto techs at Walmart?

A: The hourly wage for auto techs at Walmart can range from $12 to $18 per hour, depending on factors such as experience and location.

Q: Does Walmart offer benefits for auto techs?

A: Yes, Walmart offers benefits such as health insurance, a 401k plan, and paid time off for auto techs.

Q: How can experience and certifications influence auto tech pay at Walmart?

A: Auto techs with more experience and certifications such as ASE may be able to negotiate a higher hourly wage.

Q: Are there opportunities for advancement within Walmart for auto techs?

A: Yes, auto techs who demonstrate strong leadership skills and a commitment to the company may be able to advance to management positions with higher pay.

Conclusion of How Much Does Walmart Pay Auto Techs

In conclusion, Walmart offers competitive hourly wages and benefits for auto techs. Pay rates can range from $12 to $18 per hour depending on factors such as experience and location. Additionally, there are opportunities for advancement within the company for those who demonstrate strong skills and leadership abilities. Overall, Walmart can be a great option for those looking to start or advance their career in the automotive industry.
