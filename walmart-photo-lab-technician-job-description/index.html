The Daily Responsibilities of a Walmart Photo Lab Technician

The daily responsibilities of a Walmart Photo Lab Technician can vary, but some of the most common tasks include:

- Developing film
- Printing photos
- Operating the photo kiosk
- Assisting customers with any questions they may have about the photo lab or their photos
- Maintaining the cleanliness and organization of the photo lab
- Troubleshooting any issues that may arise with the photo lab equipment

The Requirements for a Walmart Photo Lab Technician

To become a Walmart Photo Lab Technician, you'll need to meet certain requirements. These requirements may vary depending on your location, but some of the most common ones include:

- A high school diploma or equivalent
- Experience working with photo lab equipment
- Knowledge of photo editing software
- Excellent customer service skills
- Attention to detail
- The ability to work in a fast-paced environment
- The ability to work a flexible schedule, including nights and weekends
