Factors Affecting Average Mechanical Engineer Salary in Saudi Arabia

Experience, qualifications, job location, and industry are all factors that can affect the average mechanical engineer salary in Saudi Arabia. Mechanical engineers with more experience and higher qualifications can typically command higher salaries. Additionally, working in a larger city such as Riyadh or Jeddah can often result in higher salaries due to the higher cost of living in these areas. Finally, working in industries such as oil and gas or manufacturing can often result in higher salaries as these industries are highly lucrative in Saudi Arabia.
