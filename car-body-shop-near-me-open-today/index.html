Question and Answer

Q: How long does it take to repair a car?

A: The time it takes to repair a car depends on the extent of the damage. Minor repairs can take a few days, while more extensive repairs can take several weeks.

Q: Will my insurance cover the cost of repairs?

A: If you have insurance that covers collision damage, your insurance company may cover the cost of repairs. However, you'll need to check your policy to see what's covered.

Q: Can I still drive my car if it's been damaged?

A: It depends on the extent of the damage. If your car is safe to drive and doesn't require immediate repairs, you can still drive it. However, if the damage is significant, it's best to get it repaired as soon as possible.

Q: How much does it cost to repair a car?

A: The cost of repairing a car depends on the extent of the damage and the type of repairs needed. Minor repairs can cost a few hundred dollars, while more extensive repairs can cost several thousand dollars.
