The good news is that there is one university in Bangladesh that offers a Bachelor of Science in Automotive Engineering program, and that is the Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET). CUET is a prestigious public university that was established in 1968 and is located in Chittagong, Bangladesh. The university offers a four-year undergraduate program in Automotive Engineering that covers a wide range of topics such as automotive design, engine technology, vehicle dynamics, and more.
