Why is Relaxed English Language Important in a Call Center?

I once worked in a call center where the representatives were required to use complex technical jargon and industry-specific terminology. Implementing relaxed English language in a call center can be achieved through training and coaching. Additionally, coaching sessions can be conducted to provide feedback on communication skills and offer support for improvement.
