The Top Highest Paying Mechanic Jobs In Canada

Written by Aprilia Nov 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a career in the mechanic industry but unsure of which jobs pay the most? Look no further because we have gathered a list of the top highest paying mechanic jobs in Canada.

It's no secret that the mechanic industry can be physically demanding, and it requires a great deal of skill, knowledge, and experience. However, with the right qualifications and experience, a career in the mechanic industry can be highly rewarding.

When it comes to the highest paying mechanic jobs in Canada, the target is to find jobs that offer both financial stability and personal satisfaction.

In this article, we will discuss the top highest paying mechanic jobs in Canada and their salary range, the skills and qualifications required, and the job outlook. Let's dive in!

Automotive Service Technician

As an automotive service technician, you will be responsible for diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining vehicles. You may also be required to provide advice on the best course of action to take when a vehicle needs repairs. The average salary for an automotive service technician in Canada is $56,000 to $80,000 per year. To become a licensed automotive service technician in Canada, you must complete an apprenticeship program and pass a certification exam.

Heavy Duty Mechanic

Heavy duty mechanics are responsible for diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining large vehicles and equipment, such as trucks, buses, and bulldozers. The average salary for a heavy duty mechanic in Canada is $70,000 to $110,000 per year. To become a licensed heavy duty mechanic in Canada, you must complete an apprenticeship program and pass a certification exam.

Aircraft Maintenance Engineer

As an aircraft maintenance engineer, you will be responsible for maintaining and repairing aircraft. You may also be required to inspect aircraft to ensure they meet safety regulations. The average salary for an aircraft maintenance engineer in Canada is $60,000 to $120,000 per year. To become a licensed aircraft maintenance engineer in Canada, you must complete an approved training program and pass a certification exam.

Marine Mechanic

Marine mechanics are responsible for diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining boats and other watercraft. The average salary for a marine mechanic in Canada is $40,000 to $80,000 per year. To become a licensed marine mechanic in Canada, you must complete an apprenticeship program and pass a certification exam.

Conclusion of Highest Paying Mechanic Jobs in Canada

In conclusion, the mechanic industry offers a range of rewarding career opportunities for those who are willing to put in the hard work and dedication. By pursuing a career in one of the highest paying mechanic jobs in Canada, you can ensure that you will have a financially stable future while doing something you love. So, what are you waiting for? Start your journey towards a successful career in the mechanic industry today!

A: To become a licensed heavy duty mechanic in Canada, you must have strong analytical and problem-solving skills, as well as excellent manual dexterity.

Q: What is the job outlook for aircraft maintenance engineers in Canada?

A: The job outlook for aircraft maintenance engineers in Canada is positive, with an expected growth rate of 5% over the next 10 years.

Q: How long does it take to become a licensed automotive service technician in Canada?

A: It typically takes 4 years to complete an automotive service technician apprenticeship program in Canada.
