Are Microsoft Certifications Worth It In 2021

Written by Robby Oct 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you want to boost your career in the tech industry? Are you considering getting a Microsoft certification? You're not alone. Microsoft certifications have been gaining popularity among IT professionals, but the question is, are they worth it?

Many people are hesitant to get Microsoft certifications because they think it's too expensive and time-consuming. They also think that the skills they learn will become outdated quickly. However, in reality, getting a Microsoft certification can be a game-changer for your career.

Microsoft certifications are recognized worldwide and are highly respected in the industry. They demonstrate that you have a deep understanding of Microsoft technologies and are capable of handling complex IT problems. Microsoft certifications can also increase your earning potential and provide you with more job opportunities.

In summary, Microsoft certifications are definitely worth it in 2021. They can help you advance your career, increase your salary, and demonstrate your expertise to potential employers.

Why Should You Get Microsoft Certifications?

I remember when I first started my career in IT. I had a basic understanding of Microsoft technologies, but I knew that wasn't enough to take my career to the next level. I decided to get a Microsoft certification, and it changed everything.

Getting a Microsoft certification gave me the confidence to take on more complex projects and responsibilities. It also opened up more job opportunities for me. Employers were impressed by my certification and knew that I had the skills they were looking for.

How Can You Get Microsoft Certifications?

There are several ways to get Microsoft certifications. You can take Microsoft Official Courses (MOC) at a Microsoft Learning Partner, which provides you with hands-on experience and prepares you for the certification exam. You can also take online training courses, study on your own, and then take the certification exam.

Types of Microsoft Certifications

Microsoft offers a wide range of certifications, including:

- Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate

- Microsoft Certified: Azure Developer Associate

- Microsoft Certified: Azure Security Engineer Associate

- Microsoft Certified: Azure AI Engineer Associate

- Microsoft Certified: Azure Data Scientist Associate

Tips for Passing Microsoft Certification Exams

Passing a Microsoft certification exam can be a challenging task. Here are some tips to help you:

- Study the exam objectives carefully

- Take practice exams to get familiar with the format

- Use Microsoft's official study materials

- Join study groups or forums to get support from other candidates

My Personal Experience with Microsoft Certifications

Getting a Microsoft certification was one of the best decisions I made for my career. It helped me to stand out from the competition and gave me the skills and knowledge I needed to succeed in my job. I highly recommend getting a Microsoft certification if you want to advance your career in the tech industry.

Question and Answer

Q: Are Microsoft certifications expensive?

A: The cost of Microsoft certifications varies depending on the exam. However, they are generally affordable and can provide a great return on investment.

Q: How long does it take to get a Microsoft certification?

A: The time it takes to get a Microsoft certification depends on your experience and knowledge of the technology. On average, it takes about 3-6 months to prepare for a certification exam.

Q: Will my Microsoft certification become outdated?

A: Microsoft technologies are constantly evolving, but Microsoft certifications are updated regularly to reflect these changes. Microsoft certification holders are also required to renew their certification every few years to ensure they are up-to-date with the latest technology.

Q: Are Microsoft certifications recognized worldwide?

A: Yes, Microsoft certifications are recognized worldwide and are highly respected in the IT industry.

Conclusion of Are Microsoft Certifications Worth It

In conclusion, Microsoft certifications are definitely worth it in 2021. They can help you advance your career, increase your salary, and demonstrate your expertise to potential employers. With the right preparation and dedication, you can pass a Microsoft certification exam and take your career to the next level.
