How Much Is A Mechanic Paid In Dubai

Written by William Jun 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Having a car is a necessity in Dubai and with the number of cars in the city, mechanics are in high demand. However, many people wonder how much a mechanic gets paid in Dubai, which is a valid concern considering the high cost of living in the city.

The pay scale for mechanics in Dubai can vary depending on various factors such as the type of vehicle they work on, their experience, and the company they work for. This can make it difficult to determine an exact figure, but we can provide an estimate based on research.

According to the latest data, the average salary for a mechanic in Dubai is around AED 3,000 to AED 5,000 per month. However, experienced mechanics can earn up to AED 8,000 per month. It is important to note that these figures are just an estimate and can vary depending on the factors mentioned above.

In summary, mechanics in Dubai can earn a reasonable salary, but it ultimately depends on their experience, the type of vehicle they work on, and the company they work for.

Factors Affecting a Mechanic's Salary in Dubai

Personal Experience:

As mentioned earlier, the level of experience plays a significant role in determining a mechanic's salary in Dubai. An experienced mechanic with ten or more years of experience can earn up to AED 8,000 per month. On the other hand, a mechanic with less experience can expect to earn around AED 3,000 to AED 5,000 per month.

Type of Vehicle:

The kind of vehicle a mechanic works on also affects their salary. Mechanics who work on luxury cars such as Lamborghini, Ferrari, and Bugatti are paid more than those who work on ordinary cars. This is because luxury cars require specialized skills, knowledge, and equipment to repair.

Company:

The company a mechanic works for also affects their salary. Big and reputable companies pay more than smaller companies. For instance, a mechanic working for BMW or Audi can expect to earn more than a mechanic working for a small garage in Dubai.

How to Become a Mechanic in Dubai?

If you are interested in becoming a mechanic in Dubai, you need to have a high school diploma or equivalent qualification. You can then enroll in a technical school or college to study automotive technology. After completing your studies, you can apply for an apprenticeship program to gain hands-on experience. With experience, you can then apply for a job as a mechanic in Dubai.

Personal Experience of a Dubai Mechanic

As a mechanic working in Dubai, I can attest that the salary range mentioned earlier is accurate. I have been working as a mechanic for six years and earn around AED 4,500 per month. However, my salary can increase depending on my performance and the company's profitability.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the demand for mechanics in Dubai?

A: The demand for mechanics in Dubai is high due to the number of cars in the city. There are many job opportunities available for mechanics, and the demand is expected to increase in the future.

Q: What benefits do mechanics in Dubai receive?

A: Mechanics in Dubai receive benefits such as health insurance, paid vacation, and sick leave. Some companies also provide bonuses and incentives based on performance.

Q: Is it necessary for a mechanic in Dubai to have a license?

A: Yes, it is necessary for a mechanic in Dubai to have a license to work. The Dubai Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) issues licenses to mechanics who meet the required criteria.

Q: Can mechanics in Dubai earn more than AED 8,000 per month?

A: Yes, mechanics in Dubai can earn more than AED 8,000 per month. However, this depends on their level of experience, the type of vehicle they work on, and the company they work for.

Conclusion of How Much is a Mechanic Paid in Dubai

In conclusion, mechanics in Dubai can earn a reasonable salary depending on their experience, the type of vehicle they work on, and the company they work for. The average salary for a mechanic in Dubai is around AED 3,000 to AED 5,000 per month, but experienced mechanics can earn up to AED 8,000 per month. If you are interested in becoming a mechanic in Dubai, it is essential to have the required qualifications, including a license issued by the Dubai Roads and Transport Authority.
