When Should You Get Your Car Brakes Serviced?

You should get your car brakes serviced every 12,000 miles or at least once a year, whichever comes first. However, if you notice any of the following signs, you should get your brakes checked immediately:

  • Noise when applying the brakes
  • Pulling to one side when applying the brakes
  • Brake pedal feels spongy or goes all the way to the floor
  • Vibration when applying the brakes
  • Dashboard warning light is on

Q: What is the difference between brake pads and brake shoes?

A: Brake pads and brake shoes are the two parts of the braking system that apply friction to the wheels to stop the car. However, brake pads are used in disc brakes, while brake shoes are used in drum brakes.
