Best Book to Learn Mechanical Engineering: An Introduction

The best book to learn mechanical engineering in relaxed English language is "Mechanical Engineering for Beginners" by Mark Freedom. This book is specifically designed for people who are new to mechanical engineering and want to learn the basics in a language that is easy to understand.

The book covers a wide range of topics, including mechanics, thermodynamics, materials science, and robotics. It explains each concept in detail, with practical examples and illustrations to help you visualize the material. The book also includes exercises and quizzes at the end of each chapter to test your understanding and reinforce the concepts you have learned.
