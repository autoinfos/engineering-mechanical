How Much Do Refrigeration Mechanics Make In Australia

Written by Robby Aug 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Refrigeration mechanics play a crucial role in keeping our food, drinks and other perishable items fresh by ensuring the smooth functioning of refrigeration and air conditioning systems. However, many people may wonder how much do refrigeration mechanics make in Australia and if it's a lucrative career choice.

It's no secret that salary is an important factor to consider when choosing a career path, and refrigeration mechanics are no exception. The job can be physically demanding, and often requires working in harsh conditions, which may not be everyone's cup of tea. On top of that, the level of education and training required to become a refrigeration mechanic can be quite intensive, which makes it important for potential mechanics to understand the earning potential of this profession.

The average salary of a refrigeration mechanic in Australia is around AUD $73,000 per year. However, this figure may vary depending on factors such as experience, location, industry and employer. For instance, those working in metropolitan areas may earn more than those in rural areas due to the higher cost of living. Similarly, those employed in the commercial or industrial sector may earn more than those working in residential settings.

Overall, refrigeration mechanics can earn a good living in Australia, with opportunities for growth and advancement within the field. By obtaining additional certifications or specialisations, mechanics can increase their earning potential and take on more challenging roles.

Personal Experience with Refrigeration Mechanic Salary

My cousin works as a refrigeration mechanic in Sydney, and he has been in the field for over a decade. According to him, the starting salary for a refrigeration mechanic can be around AUD $50,000, which may seem low, but as you gain more experience, you can expect to earn significantly more.

Factors Affecting Refrigeration Mechanic Salary

As mentioned earlier, several factors can affect the salary of a refrigeration mechanic. For instance, those working in the commercial sector may earn more than those working in the residential sector due to the complexity of the systems they work with. Similarly, those with additional certifications or specialisations may earn more than those without.

Educational Requirements

To become a refrigeration mechanic in Australia, you need to complete a Certificate III in Air Conditioning and Refrigeration. This course covers topics such as installing, maintaining and repairing refrigeration and air conditioning systems. Once you have completed the course, you can apply for a refrigeration mechanic license with the Australian Refrigeration Council (ARC).

Career Prospects

The demand for refrigeration mechanics in Australia is expected to grow in the coming years, with the rise of industries such as food processing, logistics and healthcare. This means that there will be plenty of job opportunities for those looking to enter the field, with the potential for growth and advancement.

Personal Growth in the Field

My cousin has been able to increase his earnings by taking on more challenging roles and obtaining additional certifications, such as the Certificate IV in Engineering (Refrigeration and Air Conditioning). This has allowed him to work on more complex systems and take on supervisory roles, which come with a higher salary.

Question and Answer

Q: How long does it take to become a refrigeration mechanic in Australia?

A: It typically takes around 4 years to complete a Certificate III in Air Conditioning and Refrigeration and obtain a refrigeration mechanic license through the Australian Refrigeration Council (ARC).

Q: Is the demand for refrigeration mechanics increasing in Australia?

A: Yes, the demand for refrigeration mechanics is expected to grow in the coming years due to the rise of industries such as food processing, logistics and healthcare.

Q: Can refrigeration mechanics earn more by obtaining additional certifications or specialisations?

A: Yes, refrigeration mechanics can increase their earning potential by obtaining additional certifications or specialisations, such as the Certificate IV in Engineering (Refrigeration and Air Conditioning).

Q: What is the average salary of a refrigeration mechanic in Australia?

A: The average salary of a refrigeration mechanic in Australia is around AUD $73,000 per year.

Conclusion of How Much Do Refrigeration Mechanics Make in Australia

Refrigeration mechanics play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth functioning of refrigeration and air conditioning systems. While the job can be physically demanding, it can also be quite rewarding, with opportunities for growth and advancement within the field. By obtaining additional certifications or specialisations, refrigeration mechanics can increase their earning potential and take on more challenging roles, making it a lucrative career choice in Australia.
