How To Become A Certified Rv Technician

Written by Daniel Oct 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you love the outdoors and have a passion for fixing things, becoming a certified RV technician could be the perfect career for you. However, the journey to becoming a certified RV technician can be overwhelming, especially if you don't know where to start.

Many aspiring RV technicians face several challenges, such as finding the right training program, understanding the certification process, and gaining hands-on experience in the field.

To become a certified RV technician, you need to follow a few essential steps:

First, you need to find an accredited RV technician training program that meets the requirements outlined by the Recreation Vehicle Industry Association (RVIA). You can either choose to attend a traditional college or opt for an online training program.

Second, you need to gain hands-on experience by working as an apprentice under a seasoned RV technician. This will give you the opportunity to learn from the best and gain practical experience working on real RVs.

Third, you need to pass the RVIA certification exam. This exam covers various aspects of RV repair and maintenance, including electrical systems, plumbing, appliances, and HVAC systems.

In summary, becoming a certified RV technician requires a combination of education, hands-on experience, and certification. By following these essential steps, you can start your journey towards a fulfilling and rewarding career as a certified RV technician.

Why Become a Certified RV Technician?

As a certified RV technician, you can enjoy several benefits, including:

1. Job Security: The RV industry is booming, and there is a high demand for qualified RV technicians. By becoming certified, you can increase your chances of landing a stable job with a reputable RV dealership or repair shop.

2. Competitive Salary: Certified RV technicians typically earn more than non-certified technicians. This is because certification demonstrates your expertise and commitment to the field.

3. Flexibility: As an RV technician, you can work in a variety of settings, including RV dealerships, repair shops, and even RV parks. This gives you the flexibility to choose a work environment that suits your lifestyle.

My Experience Becoming a Certified RV Technician

When I decided to become a certified RV technician, I knew that I had a long road ahead of me. I started by researching accredited RV technician training programs and found one that was both affordable and flexible enough to fit my busy schedule.

After completing my training, I reached out to local RV dealerships and repair shops to inquire about apprenticeship opportunities. Fortunately, I was able to secure an apprenticeship with a seasoned RV technician who taught me everything I needed to know about RV repair and maintenance.

Finally, I took the RVIA certification exam and passed with flying colors. Today, I am a certified RV technician with a stable job and a fulfilling career.

FAQs About Becoming a Certified RV Technician

Q: How long does it take to become a certified RV technician?

A: The length of time it takes to become a certified RV technician depends on the training program you choose and the amount of hands-on experience you gain. Typically, RV technician training programs take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years to complete.

Q: Do I need a high school diploma to become a certified RV technician?

A: Yes, most accredited RV technician training programs require applicants to have a high school diploma or GED equivalent.

Q: How much does RV technician training cost?

A: The cost of RV technician training varies depending on the program and the school. However, you can expect to pay anywhere from $3,000 to $20,000 for an accredited RV technician training program.

Q: What is the job outlook for certified RV technicians?

A: The job outlook for certified RV technicians is excellent, as the RV industry is experiencing rapid growth. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of RV service technicians is projected to grow 18 percent from 2018 to 2028, much faster than the average for all occupations.

Conclusion of How to Become a Certified RV Technician

Becoming a certified RV technician can be a fulfilling and rewarding career choice for those who love the outdoors and have a passion for fixing things. By following the essential steps outlined in this post, you can start your journey towards becoming a certified RV technician and enjoy the benefits of job security, competitive salary, and flexibility.
