How Much Does A Mechanical Engineer Make In Dubai

Written by Daniel Jun 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a mechanical engineer looking to work in Dubai but unsure about the salary range? You're not alone. Many engineers are interested in working in Dubai, but are curious about how much they can expect to earn. Understanding the salary range for mechanical engineers in Dubai can help you make informed decisions about your career.

In Dubai, the salary range for a mechanical engineer varies depending on the level of experience and qualifications. Generally, the salary for mechanical engineers in Dubai ranges from AED 8,000 to AED 30,000 per month. However, these salaries can go up to AED 40,000 per month for senior-level engineers.

So, what factors affect the salary range for mechanical engineers in Dubai? Firstly, the level of experience and qualifications play a significant role. The more experienced and qualified an engineer is, the higher their salary will be. Secondly, the industry and company they work for can also affect their salary range. For example, mechanical engineers working in the oil and gas sector may earn higher salaries than those working in construction.

In summary, the salary range for mechanical engineers in Dubai varies depending on the level of experience, qualifications, industry, and company. It's essential to research and understand these factors before accepting a job offer.

My Experience as a Mechanical Engineer in Dubai

As a mechanical engineer with five years of experience, I moved to Dubai to work in the oil and gas sector. My starting salary was AED 18,000 per month, which was higher than my previous job in my home country. Over the years, my salary increased as I gained more experience and took on more responsibility. I now earn AED 28,000 per month, which is a comfortable salary for me.

Tips for Negotiating Your Salary as a Mechanical Engineer in Dubai

1. Research the salary range for your level of experience and qualifications before negotiating.
2. Highlight your experience and qualifications during the negotiation process.
3. Consider negotiating other benefits such as housing allowance or health insurance.
4. Be confident and assertive during the negotiation process.

The Benefits of Working as a Mechanical Engineer in Dubai

Working as a mechanical engineer in Dubai has several benefits, including:

1. Tax-free income
2. Opportunities to work in various industries
3. High salaries for experienced engineers
4. Opportunities for career growth

How to Find Mechanical Engineering Jobs in Dubai

There are several ways to find mechanical engineering jobs in Dubai, including:

1. Searching online job portals such as Indeed, GulfTalent, and Bayt
2. Networking with professionals in your industry
3. Attending job fairs and career events
4. Applying directly to companies in your industry

My Final Thoughts

Overall, the salary range for mechanical engineers in Dubai is competitive and offers many opportunities for career growth. However, it's essential to research and understand the factors that affect the salary range before accepting a job offer. With the right experience, qualifications, and negotiation skills, mechanical engineers in Dubai can earn a comfortable salary and enjoy a high quality of life.
