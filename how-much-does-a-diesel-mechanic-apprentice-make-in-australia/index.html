How Much Does A Diesel Mechanic Apprentice Make In Australia

Written by Luffy Sep 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a diesel mechanic apprentice in Australia? Do you want to know how much money you can expect to make? If so, you're in the right place! In this post, we'll explore the topic of how much does a diesel mechanic apprentice make in Australia and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

As a diesel mechanic apprentice, you'll be responsible for maintaining, repairing, and servicing diesel engines. While this is a rewarding and challenging career, it's important to note that it can also be physically demanding and require long hours. Additionally, the pay for diesel mechanic apprentices in Australia can vary depending on a range of factors, including location, experience, and employer.

So, how much does a diesel mechanic apprentice make in Australia? According to Payscale, the average hourly rate for a diesel mechanic apprentice in Australia is $19.19. This equates to an annual salary of around $40,000 - $50,000, depending on your level of experience and location.

In summary, diesel mechanic apprentices in Australia can expect to earn a decent salary, with the potential to earn more as they gain experience and progress in their careers. It's important to note that the pay can vary depending on a range of factors, so it's always a good idea to do your research and speak to potential employers before making any career decisions.

What Factors Affect the Salary of a Diesel Mechanic Apprentice in Australia?

There are several factors that can impact the salary of a diesel mechanic apprentice in Australia. These include:

Location

The location of your employer can have a significant impact on your salary. For example, diesel mechanic apprentices working in major cities such as Sydney and Melbourne may earn more than those in regional areas.

Experience

As with most careers, the more experience you have, the higher your salary is likely to be. Diesel mechanic apprentices who have been in the industry for several years may be able to command a higher salary than those who are just starting out.

Personal Experience

When I first started as a diesel mechanic apprentice, I was earning around $35,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and progressed in my career, my salary increased to around $50,000 per year. I found that location was also a factor, with higher salaries available in major cities.

How Can You Increase Your Salary as a Diesel Mechanic Apprentice in Australia?

If you want to increase your salary as a diesel mechanic apprentice in Australia, there are several things you can do. These include:

Gain Experience

The more experience you have, the higher your salary is likely to be. Look for opportunities to gain additional experience, such as working on more complex engines or taking on additional responsibilities.

Get Certified

Obtaining additional certifications and qualifications can also increase your earning potential. Consider obtaining certifications in areas such as engine diagnostics or air conditioning servicing.
