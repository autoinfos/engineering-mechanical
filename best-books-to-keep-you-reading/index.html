Best Books To Keep You Reading A Guide To Relaxing Reads

Written by William Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you love to read but struggle to find the time to do so? Are you looking for books that will help you unwind and relax? Look no further than the best books to keep you reading! These books are perfect for those who want to escape from the stresses of everyday life and get lost in a good story.

Many people find it difficult to set aside time to read, especially when they have busy schedules or are dealing with stress. However, reading can be an excellent way to relax and unwind, and it can even help to reduce stress levels. The best books to keep you reading are those that are engaging, easy to read, and offer an escape from reality.

So, what are the best books to keep you reading? The answer to this question will depend on your personal preferences, but there are a few genres that are particularly well-suited for relaxation and escape. These genres include romance, mystery, and fantasy, as well as non-fiction books on topics like travel and self-help.

Romance Novels: A Classic Choice for Relaxation

When it comes to relaxing books, romance novels are a classic choice. These books are typically light and easy to read, with engaging characters and heartwarming stories. Whether you prefer historical romance, contemporary romance, or something in between, there are plenty of great romance novels to choose from.

Personally, I love curling up with a good romance novel after a long day. There's something about the happy endings and feel-good stories that always helps me to unwind and relax. Some of my favorite romance authors include Nora Roberts, Susan Mallery, and Debbie Macomber.

Mystery Novels: The Perfect Escape

If you're looking for a book that will keep you on the edge of your seat, a mystery novel might be just what you need. These books are perfect for getting lost in a complex plot and trying to solve the puzzle along with the characters.

One of my favorite mystery series is the "In Death" series by J.D. Robb. These books follow the adventures of homicide detective Eve Dallas as she solves crimes in a futuristic New York City. The stories are engaging and fast-paced, with plenty of twists and turns to keep you guessing.

Fantasy Novels: A World of Imagination

For those who want to completely escape reality, fantasy novels are the perfect choice. These books transport you to a world of imagination, where anything is possible. Whether you prefer epic fantasies like "The Lord of the Rings" or more lighthearted fare like "Harry Potter," there's a fantasy novel out there for everyone.

Personally, I love the "A Song of Ice and Fire" series by George R.R. Martin. These books are epic in every sense of the word, with complex characters, intricate plotlines, and a richly detailed world. They're the perfect choice for those who want to get lost in a fully realized fantasy world.

Non-Fiction Books: Learning and Relaxation Combined

If you prefer non-fiction books, there are still plenty of great options for relaxation and escape. Travel books, in particular, can be a great choice for those who want to explore the world without leaving home.

One of my favorite travel books is "Eat, Pray, Love" by Elizabeth Gilbert. This book follows the author as she travels to Italy, India, and Indonesia in search of self-discovery and happiness. It's a great read for those who want to escape into someone else's adventure.

Conclusion of Best Books to Keep You Reading

Whether you prefer romance, mystery, fantasy, or non-fiction, there are plenty of great books out there to help you relax and escape. So, the next time you're feeling stressed or overwhelmed, pick up one of these books and let yourself get lost in a good story.

Question and Answer

Q: Are there any other genres that are good for relaxation?

A: Yes! Cozy mysteries, chick lit, and even some science fiction can be great choices for relaxation.

Q: What if I don't like any of these genres?

A: That's okay! The most important thing is to find a book that you enjoy and that helps you relax. Experiment with different genres and authors until you find something that works for you.

Q: Can audiobooks be a good choice for relaxation?

A: Absolutely! Audiobooks are a great way to enjoy a good story while doing other activities, like driving or exercising.

Q: Where can I find recommendations for good relaxation books?

A: There are plenty of websites and blogs that offer book recommendations, as well as social media groups and book clubs. You can also ask friends and family members for their favorite relaxation reads.
