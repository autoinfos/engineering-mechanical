Certified Automotive Technician Salary What You Need To Know

Written by Daniel Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in becoming a certified automotive technician? If so, you may be wondering about the salary and compensation that comes with this career. In this post, we will dive into the details of certified automotive technician salary and what you can expect.

As with any career, there are both pros and cons when it comes to certified automotive technician salary. On the one hand, this career can provide a decent salary and job stability. On the other hand, it can also come with long hours and physical demands.

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for automotive service technicians and mechanics was $44,050 as of May 2020. However, this can vary based on factors such as location, years of experience, and certifications held. It is also important to note that some technicians may earn commission on top of their base salary, which can significantly increase their overall earnings.

How I Got Started in the Automotive Industry

My journey in the automotive industry began when I was just 18 years old. I had always been interested in cars and knew that I wanted to pursue a career in this field. I started out as a technician's assistant, learning the ropes and gaining hands-on experience.

Eventually, I decided to pursue certification as an automotive technician. This involved completing a comprehensive training program and passing a series of exams. After becoming certified, I was able to secure a full-time position as a technician at a local dealership.

The Benefits of Certification

One of the biggest benefits of becoming a certified automotive technician is that it can lead to higher pay and more job opportunities. Certification shows that you have the skills and knowledge needed to perform the job at a high level, which can make you a more valuable asset to employers.

In addition, certification can also help you stand out in a crowded job market. Many employers prefer to hire technicians who are certified, as this indicates a level of professionalism and dedication to the industry.

How to Increase Your Earnings as a Certified Automotive Technician

If you are looking to increase your earnings as a certified automotive technician, there are a few steps you can take. First and foremost, consider pursuing additional certifications or specializations. This can make you more valuable to employers and increase your earning potential.

Another option is to seek out job opportunities in areas where the demand for technicians is high. For example, if you live in an area with a lot of car dealerships and repair shops, you may be able to find a job with a higher salary.

How to Negotiate Your Salary as a Certified Automotive Technician

When it comes to negotiating your salary as a certified automotive technician, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, do your research to determine what the average salary is for someone with your level of experience and certifications.

Next, be prepared to make a case for why you deserve a higher salary. This might include highlighting your skills and experience, as well as any unique certifications or specializations you hold.

Conclusion of Certified Automotive Technician Salary

Overall, certified automotive technician salary can provide a decent living for those who are willing to put in the time and effort required. By pursuing certification, seeking out job opportunities in high-demand areas, and negotiating your salary effectively, you can increase your earning potential and build a successful career in this field.

Question and Answer

Q. What is the average starting salary for a certified automotive technician?

A. The average starting salary for a certified automotive technician varies based on location and experience, but is typically around $30,000 to $40,000 per year.

Q. How much does certification impact automotive technician salary?

A. Certification can have a significant impact on automotive technician salary, as it shows that the technician has the skills and knowledge needed to perform the job at a high level. Certified technicians may earn more than non-certified technicians, and may also have access to more job opportunities.

Q. What are some of the highest-paying jobs in the automotive industry?

A. Some of the highest-paying jobs in the automotive industry include automotive engineers, automotive designers, and automotive executives.

Q. Is it possible to earn a six-figure salary as a certified automotive technician?

A. While it is possible to earn a six-figure salary as a certified automotive technician, this typically requires years of experience and advanced certifications or specializations.
