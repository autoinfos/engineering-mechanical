How Much Does A Helicopter Mechanic Make In Canada

Written by Eveline Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you curious about the salary of a helicopter mechanic in Canada? If you're considering a career in aviation, it's important to know how much you can expect to earn. In this blog post, we will explore the average salary of a helicopter mechanic in Canada and factors that can affect their earnings.

Working as a helicopter mechanic can be a challenging and rewarding career. However, it's no secret that the job can also come with its own set of challenges. One of the biggest concerns for many is the salary. Many aspiring helicopter mechanics want to know how much they can earn and if it's enough to support themselves and their families.

According to the Government of Canada Job Bank, the average hourly wage for helicopter mechanics in Canada is around $34 an hour. This translates to an annual salary range of $50,000 to $100,000, depending on experience, location, and other factors. However, it's important to note that entry-level positions may pay less, while senior-level mechanics with years of experience can earn significantly more.

In summary, the salary of a helicopter mechanic in Canada can vary widely depending on several factors such as location, years of experience, and type of employer. However, on average, a helicopter mechanic in Canada can expect to earn around $50,000 to $100,000 per year.

Factors That Affect a Helicopter Mechanic's Salary in Canada

Location: Salaries can vary depending on where you work in Canada. For example, helicopter mechanics in larger cities like Toronto or Vancouver may earn more than those working in smaller towns or rural areas.

Years of Experience: As with many jobs, the more experience you have, the more you can expect to earn. Entry-level helicopter mechanics may start at a lower salary, while those with years of experience can earn significantly more.

My Personal Experience as a Helicopter Mechanic in Canada

As a former helicopter mechanic in Canada, I can attest that the salary can be quite good, especially for those with experience. When I started out, I was making around $40,000 per year. However, after a few years of working and gaining experience, my salary increased to around $80,000 per year.

How to Become a Helicopter Mechanic in Canada

To become a helicopter mechanic in Canada, you will need to complete a Transport Canada-approved training program. This typically involves a combination of classroom learning and hands-on training. Once you have completed your training, you will need to pass an examination to obtain your Aircraft Maintenance Engineer (AME) license.

Job Outlook for Helicopter Mechanics in Canada

The job outlook for helicopter mechanics in Canada is positive. According to the Government of Canada Job Bank, there are currently more job openings than there are qualified applicants. This means that there is a high demand for skilled helicopter mechanics, which can lead to more job opportunities and potentially higher salaries.

Tips for Increasing Your Salary as a Helicopter Mechanic in Canada

If you're looking to increase your salary as a helicopter mechanic in Canada, there are a few things you can do:

  • Gain more experience: The more experience you have, the more you can expect to earn.
  • Specialize in a certain area: Specializing in a certain area of helicopter mechanics, such as avionics or engine repair, can make you more valuable to employers and potentially lead to higher salaries.
  • Work for a larger company: Larger companies may offer higher salaries and better benefits than smaller companies.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average salary for an entry-level helicopter mechanic in Canada?

A: The average salary for an entry-level helicopter mechanic in Canada is around $40,000 per year.

Q: How much can a senior-level helicopter mechanic earn in Canada?

A: Senior-level helicopter mechanics with years of experience can earn up to $100,000 per year or more.

Q: What type of training is required to become a helicopter mechanic in Canada?

A: To become a helicopter mechanic in Canada, you will need to complete a Transport Canada-approved training program and obtain an Aircraft Maintenance Engineer (AME) license.

Q: Is there a high demand for helicopter mechanics in Canada?

A: Yes, there is currently a high demand for skilled helicopter mechanics in Canada, which can lead to more job opportunities and potentially higher salaries.

Conclusion of How Much Does a Helicopter Mechanic Make in Canada

Overall, the salary of a helicopter mechanic in Canada can vary depending on factors such as location, years of experience, and type of employer. However, on average, a helicopter mechanic in Canada can expect to earn around $50,000 to $100,000 per year. With a positive job outlook and opportunities for growth, becoming a helicopter mechanic in Canada can be a rewarding and lucrative career choice.
