Questions and Answers About Care Worker Job Description PDF

What is a care worker?

A care worker is a healthcare professional who provides personal care and support to patients in a variety of settings, including hospitals, nursing homes, and private homes.

The primary role of a care worker is to provide personal care and support to patients, including assisting with daily living activities, administering medication, and monitoring vital signs.
