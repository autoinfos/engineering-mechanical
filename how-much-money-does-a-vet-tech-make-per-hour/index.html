How Much Money Does A Vet Tech Make Per Hour

Written by Luffy Nov 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you considering becoming a veterinary technician? One of the most common questions asked by people considering this career path is how much money does a vet tech make per hour? It's important to understand the financial aspects of your chosen profession, as it can impact your quality of life and overall job satisfaction.

Many people who are interested in becoming a vet tech are concerned about the salary. It's understandable because no one wants to work hard and not be paid fairly. The good news is that vet techs are compensated well for their work.

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median hourly wage for veterinary technicians in the United States is $16.87 per hour. However, this can vary depending on different factors such as experience, location, and type of employer.

In general, vet techs who work for private veterinary clinics tend to make more money than those who work for animal shelters. The more experience you have, the higher your salary will be. Additionally, if you live in a city with a high cost of living, you can expect to make more money than those who live in rural areas.

In conclusion, the median hourly wage for veterinary technicians is $16.87 per hour. However, this can vary depending on factors such as experience, location, and type of employer. It's important to keep in mind that this career can be very rewarding, both emotionally and financially.

Personal Experience with Vet Tech Salary

When I first started working as a vet tech, I was making around $14 per hour. However, as I gained more experience and became more valuable to my employer, my salary increased. After a few years of working in the field, I was making around $20 per hour.

It's important to remember that while the salary isn't the highest, there are other benefits to working as a veterinary technician. For example, you get to help animals every day and work in a field that you are passionate about.

Factors That Affect Vet Tech Salary

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can impact the salary of a vet tech. One of the biggest factors is experience. The more experience you have, the higher your salary will be. Additionally, if you work for a larger veterinary clinic, you can expect to make more money than if you work for a smaller clinic.

Another factor that can impact your salary is location. If you live in a city with a high cost of living, you can expect to make more money than if you live in a rural area. Additionally, if you work in a state with a high demand for vet techs, you can expect to make more money than if you work in a state with a lower demand.
