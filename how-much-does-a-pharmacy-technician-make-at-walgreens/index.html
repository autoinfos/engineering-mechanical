How Much Does A Pharmacy Technician Make At Walgreens

Written by Petter Sep 15, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you considering a career as a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens but unsure about the salary? It's important to know the average pay for this position before making a decision.

A Pharmacy Technician's salary can be a significant pain point for those interested in the position. While the job may offer a variety of benefits such as flexible hours, it's crucial to ensure the salary matches your expectations.

According to Glassdoor, the average hourly pay for a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens is $14. Hourly rates range from $10 to $24, depending on experience and location. Additionally, Pharmacy Technicians at Walgreens may receive bonuses and profit-sharing opportunities.

In summary, a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens can expect to make an average of $14 per hour. However, the salary may vary based on experience and location.

What Factors Affect a Pharmacy Technician's Salary at Walgreens?

While the average salary for a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens is $14 per hour, several factors can affect the pay rate. One of the primary factors is experience. Pharmacy Technicians with more experience may receive a higher hourly wage.

Location can also affect a Pharmacy Technician's salary at Walgreens. Cities with a higher cost of living may offer higher pay rates, while rural areas may pay less.

My Experience as a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens

As a former Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens, I can attest to the job's rewarding nature. The flexible hours allowed me to balance work with school, and the fast-paced environment kept me engaged.

Regarding salary, I found the pay to be competitive compared to other entry-level positions. However, I did notice a difference in pay between locations, with larger cities offering higher hourly rates.

The Benefits of Being a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens

Aside from the competitive salary, being a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens offers several benefits. The job provides a fantastic opportunity to work in the healthcare industry without the extensive education required for other positions.

Pharmacy Technicians also have the opportunity to work alongside pharmacists, gaining valuable experience and knowledge. Additionally, Walgreens offers various benefits such as health insurance and a 401(k) plan.

How to Become a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens

To become a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens, you must have a high school diploma or GED equivalent. Additionally, you must complete a Pharmacy Technician training program and obtain certification in some states.

Once hired, Walgreens provides on-the-job training to prepare Pharmacy Technicians for their role. This training includes learning about medication dispensing, customer service, and pharmacy regulations.

FAQs About Pharmacy Technician Salaries at Walgreens

Q: Is the hourly pay for Pharmacy Technicians at Walgreens negotiable?
A: No, the hourly pay for Pharmacy Technicians at Walgreens is not negotiable.

Q: Do Pharmacy Technicians at Walgreens receive bonuses?
A: Yes, Pharmacy Technicians at Walgreens may receive bonuses and profit-sharing opportunities.

Q: How does Walgreens compare to other pharmacies in terms of Pharmacy Technician salaries?
A: According to Glassdoor, Walgreens offers competitive pay rates for Pharmacy Technicians compared to other pharmacies.

Q: Are there opportunities for advancement as a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens?
A: Yes, Walgreens offers several opportunities for growth as a Pharmacy Technician, including the ability to become a Certified Pharmacy Technician or even a Pharmacy Manager.

Conclusion of How Much Does a Pharmacy Technician Make at Walgreens

Overall, a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens can expect to make an average of $14 per hour. However, the salary may vary based on location and experience. While salary is essential to consider, being a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens also offers numerous benefits and growth opportunities in the healthcare industry.
