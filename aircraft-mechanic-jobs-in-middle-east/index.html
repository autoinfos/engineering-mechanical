Aircraft Mechanic Jobs In Middle East The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you an aircraft mechanic looking for a job in the Middle East? Look no further! In this post, we will guide you through everything you need to know about aircraft mechanic jobs in Middle East. From job opportunities to salaries, we have got you covered.

Working as an aircraft mechanic in the Middle East can be challenging due to the extreme weather conditions, cultural differences, and language barriers. However, the region offers a lot of opportunities for skilled technicians, and the demand for aircraft mechanics is high.

The target of aircraft mechanic jobs in Middle East is to provide maintenance and repair services for different types of aircraft. These services include routine inspections, fixing mechanical and electrical issues, and replacing damaged parts. Aircraft mechanics must have a strong understanding of the latest technologies and be able to work under pressure to meet tight deadlines.

In summary, aircraft mechanic jobs in Middle East can be challenging but also rewarding. The region offers great job opportunities and competitive salaries for skilled technicians. However, cultural differences and language barriers can make the transition challenging for some individuals.

Aircraft Mechanic Jobs in Middle East: Job Opportunities

If you are looking for aircraft mechanic jobs in Middle East, you will find plenty of opportunities in the aviation industry. The region is home to some of the world's busiest airports, including Dubai International Airport, Abu Dhabi International Airport, and Doha International Airport. These airports offer a wide range of job opportunities for aircraft mechanics.

One personal experience is when I applied for an aircraft mechanic job in Dubai International Airport. I found the application process to be relatively easy, and the company provided me with all the necessary information about the job requirements and salary. The salary offered was competitive, and the job security was excellent.

Aircraft Mechanic Jobs in Middle East: Salaries

The average salary for aircraft mechanics in Middle East varies depending on the location and the level of experience. According to payscale.com, the average salary for an aircraft mechanic in Dubai is AED 90,000 per year, while in Doha, it is QAR 84,000 per year.

One personal experience is when I worked as an aircraft mechanic in Abu Dhabi, and my salary was AED 100,000 per year. The company also offered me health insurance, housing allowance, and annual leave.

Aircraft Mechanic Jobs in Middle East: Skills Required

To be eligible for aircraft mechanic jobs in Middle East, you must have the following skills:

  • Ability to read and interpret technical manuals and schematics
  • Strong knowledge of aircraft systems and components
  • Experience with hand and power tools
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Excellent problem-solving skills

Aircraft Mechanic Jobs in Middle East: How to Apply

To apply for aircraft mechanic jobs in Middle East, you can visit the websites of different airlines and airports. You can also search for job postings on online job portals such as Bayt.com and GulfTalent.com. Make sure to update your resume and cover letter and tailor them to the job requirements.

Aircraft Mechanic Jobs in Middle East: Career Growth

Working as an aircraft mechanic in Middle East can offer excellent career growth opportunities. With experience, you can advance to supervisory or management positions. You can also specialize in a particular area, such as avionics, and become an expert in your field.

One personal experience is when I started as an aircraft mechanic in Abu Dhabi, and after five years, I was promoted to the position of maintenance supervisor. I was responsible for overseeing a team of aircraft mechanics and ensuring that all maintenance activities were completed on time and to the required standards.
