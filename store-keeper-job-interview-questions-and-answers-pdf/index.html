Sample Store Keeper Job Interview Questions and Answers

Here are some sample store keeper job interview questions and answers:

Question 1: What experience do you have in storekeeping?

Answer: I have several years of experience in storekeeping. In my previous role, I was responsible for managing inventory, ordering supplies, and ensuring that the store was clean and organized. I also have experience using inventory management software and working with a team.

Question 2: How do you handle inventory discrepancies?

Answer: When I encounter an inventory discrepancy, I first try to identify the cause of the issue. I then work with my team to reconcile the inventory and ensure that all items are accounted for. In some cases, I may need to order additional supplies or adjust the inventory levels to prevent future discrepancies.

Question 3: How do you prioritize your tasks as a store keeper?

Answer: I prioritize my tasks based on their urgency and importance. For example, if a customer has a specific request, I'll prioritize that task over other tasks that can wait. I also make sure to delegate tasks to my team members as needed to ensure that everything is completed on time.

Question 4: How do you handle difficult customers?

Answer: When dealing with difficult customers, I try to remain calm and professional. I listen to their concerns and try to find a solution that meets their needs while also upholding the store's policies. If necessary, I'll escalate the issue to a manager or supervisor for further assistance.
