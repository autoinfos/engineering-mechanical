Ford Service Advisor Job Description What You Need To Know

Written by Eveline Nov 17, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you interested in a career as a Ford service advisor? Do you want to know more about the job description and what it entails? Look no further, because in this post we will be discussing everything you need to know about the Ford service advisor job description.

As a Ford service advisor, you will be responsible for providing excellent customer service to Ford vehicle owners who are in need of repairs or maintenance. You will act as a liaison between the customer, the service technicians, and the dealership. This can be a demanding job, as you will be dealing with customers who may be upset or frustrated about their vehicle issues.

The main target of the Ford service advisor job description is to ensure that the customer is satisfied with the service they receive and that their vehicle is repaired or maintained to the highest possible standard. In order to achieve this, the Ford service advisor must have strong communication skills, be knowledgeable about Ford vehicles and their maintenance needs, and be able to work well under pressure.

In summary, the Ford service advisor job description involves providing excellent customer service, acting as a liaison between the customer and the dealership, and ensuring that the customer's vehicle is repaired or maintained to the highest possible standard.

What Does the Ford Service Advisor Job Entail?

When I first started working as a Ford service advisor, I was nervous about dealing with upset customers and making sure that their vehicles were repaired correctly. However, with time and experience, I have come to love my job and the challenges that it presents.

As a Ford service advisor, my main responsibilities include:

- Greeting customers and listening to their vehicle concerns
- Scheduling appointments for repairs or maintenance
- Communicating with the service technicians to ensure that they have all the information they need to repair the vehicle
- Keeping the customer updated on the status of their vehicle
- Answering any questions that the customer may have about their vehicle or the repair process
- Collecting payment for services rendered
- Following up with the customer after the repair to ensure that they are satisfied with the service they received.

What Skills Do You Need to Be a Successful Ford Service Advisor?

To be a successful Ford service advisor, you must have strong communication skills, both verbal and written. You must be able to listen to the customer's concerns and convey that information to the service technicians in a clear and concise manner. Additionally, you must be able to explain the repair process and any necessary repairs to the customer in a way that they can understand.

You must also be knowledgeable about Ford vehicles and their maintenance needs. This includes knowing the recommended maintenance schedule for each make and model of Ford vehicle, as well as any common issues that may arise with those vehicles.

Finally, you must be able to work well under pressure. Customers may be upset or frustrated about their vehicle issues, and it is up to you to remain calm and professional while addressing their concerns.

How Do You Become a Ford Service Advisor?

In order to become a Ford service advisor, you will need a high school diploma or equivalent. Some dealerships may require additional education or certification in automotive technology or a related field. Additionally, prior customer service experience is highly valued.

To apply for a Ford service advisor position, you should contact your local Ford dealership and inquire about any open positions. You can also search for job openings on Ford's website or on popular job search websites such as Indeed or Glassdoor.

How Can You Excel as a Ford Service Advisor?

To excel as a Ford service advisor, you should focus on providing excellent customer service and building strong relationships with your customers. This includes listening to their concerns, keeping them updated on the status of their vehicle, and following up with them after their repair is complete.

You should also strive to stay up-to-date on the latest Ford vehicles and their maintenance needs. Attend training sessions and workshops offered by Ford to expand your knowledge and skills.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the salary range for a Ford service advisor?
A: The salary range for a Ford service advisor varies depending on location and experience. On average, a Ford service advisor can expect to make between $40,000 and $60,000 per year.

Q: Is there room for growth as a Ford service advisor?
A: Yes, there is room for growth as a Ford service advisor. With experience, you may be able to move up to a management position or become a senior service advisor.

Q: What is the work environment like for a Ford service advisor?
A: The work environment for a Ford service advisor is typically fast-paced and can be stressful at times. However, the job can also be rewarding, as you will be helping customers solve their vehicle issues.

Q: What hours do Ford service advisors typically work?
A: Ford service advisors typically work full-time hours, which may include evenings and weekends.

Conclusion of Ford Service Advisor Job Description

In conclusion, the Ford service advisor job description involves providing excellent customer service, acting as a liaison between the customer and the dealership, and ensuring that the customer's vehicle is repaired or maintained to the highest possible standard. To be successful in this role, you must have strong communication skills, be knowledgeable about Ford vehicles and their maintenance needs, and be able to work well under pressure. If you are interested in a career as a Ford service advisor, contact your local Ford dealership to inquire about open positions.
