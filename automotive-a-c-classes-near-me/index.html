What Will You Learn in Automotive A/C Classes Near Me?

In automotive A/C classes near you, you'll learn the following:

1. The Basics of A/C Systems:

You'll learn how A/C systems work, their components, and their functions. You'll also learn about the different types of refrigerants and their properties, as well as the environmental regulations regarding their use and disposal.

2. Diagnosis and Troubleshooting:

You'll learn how to diagnose A/C problems, such as leaks, clogs, and electrical faults. You'll also learn how to use diagnostic tools and equipment, such as gauges, thermometers, and multimeters.

3. Repair and Maintenance:

You'll learn how to repair and replace A/C components, such as compressors, condensers, evaporators, and hoses. You'll also learn how to recharge refrigerant, perform vacuum tests, and check for leaks. Moreover, you'll learn about best practices in A/C repair and maintenance, such as safety precautions, proper handling of refrigerants, and environmental regulations.
