As with any job, there are both benefits and challenges to working as an automotive technician. So what exactly does an automotive technician do? In short, an automotive technician is responsible for inspecting, maintaining, and repairing vehicles of all kinds. The Target of Automotive Technician Jobs Tifton GA: Education and Certification

When it comes to pursuing a career in automotive technology, education and certification are key. Most employers will require that you have completed a formal training program in automotive technology, which typically takes anywhere from six months to two years to complete. During this time, you will learn about the various systems and components of a vehicle, as well as how to diagnose and repair common problems.

After completing your training, you will then need to obtain certification from a recognized organization such as ASE. This involves passing a series of exams that demonstrate your knowledge and skills in various areas of automotive technology. Once you are certified, you will have a much better chance of landing a job as an automotive technician, and you may also be eligible for higher pay and better benefits.
