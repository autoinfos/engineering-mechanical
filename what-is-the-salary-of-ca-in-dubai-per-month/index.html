What Is The Salary Of A Ca In Dubai Per Month

Written by Luffy Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering becoming a Chartered Accountant (CA) in Dubai? Or are you already a CA looking to move to Dubai? If so, you might be wondering what the salary of a CA in Dubai is per month. The good news is that Dubai is known for offering competitive salaries and benefits to professionals, including CAs.

However, the cost of living in Dubai can also be high, so it's important to have a clear understanding of the salary range for CAs in Dubai to make an informed decision about your career and lifestyle.

The salary of a CA in Dubai can vary depending on several factors, including the level of experience, industry, and company size. According to research, the average salary for a CA in Dubai ranges from AED 16,000 to AED 50,000 per month.

On the lower end of the scale, newly qualified CAs can expect to earn around AED 16,000 to AED 20,000 per month. However, with more experience and expertise, CAs can earn much higher salaries, up to AED 50,000 per month or more.

My Personal Experience as a CA in Dubai

As a CA who has worked in Dubai for several years, I can attest to the fact that the salary range for CAs in Dubai is quite competitive. When I first started my career in Dubai, I was earning around AED 18,000 per month. However, as I gained more experience and moved up the ranks, my salary increased significantly.

Today, I earn a salary of over AED 40,000 per month, which is a testament to the lucrative opportunities available to CAs in Dubai. Of course, the cost of living in Dubai can be high, but I have found that the benefits and lifestyle make it worthwhile.

Factors That Affect the Salary of a CA in Dubai

As mentioned, several factors can impact the salary of a CA in Dubai. One of the most significant factors is the level of experience. CAs with more experience and expertise are generally paid higher salaries than those who are just starting out in their career.

The industry and company size can also play a role in determining the salary of a CA in Dubai. For example, CAs working in the finance or banking industry may earn higher salaries than those working in other industries. Similarly, CAs working for large multinational companies may earn more than those working for smaller local companies.

Opportunities for CAs in Dubai

Overall, Dubai offers plenty of opportunities for CAs to grow their careers and earn competitive salaries. With a booming economy and a thriving business environment, Dubai is a great place for CAs to gain valuable experience and build strong networks.

How to Find a Job as a CA in Dubai

If you're interested in pursuing a career as a CA in Dubai, there are several steps you can take to increase your chances of finding a job. One of the best ways to find job openings is to network with other professionals in your industry and attend job fairs and networking events.

You can also search for job openings on online job boards and company websites. Be sure to tailor your resume and cover letter to the specific job you're applying for and highlight your relevant skills and experience.

Conclusion of What is the Salary of a CA in Dubai Per Month

In conclusion, the salary of a CA in Dubai can range from AED 16,000 to AED 50,000 per month, depending on various factors such as experience, industry, and company size. However, Dubai is known for offering competitive salaries and benefits to professionals, making it a great place for CAs to advance their careers.

Question and Answer

A: No, newly qualified CAs can expect to earn around AED 16,000 to AED 20,000 per month, which is on the lower end of the salary range.

Q: Are CAs in the finance industry paid higher salaries in Dubai?

A: Yes, CAs working in the finance or banking industry may earn higher salaries than those working in other industries.

Q: How can I increase my chances of finding a job as a CA in Dubai?

A: You can increase your chances by networking with other professionals, attending job fairs and networking events, and tailoring your resume and cover letter to the specific job you're applying for.
