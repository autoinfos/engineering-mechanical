How To Become An Xray Tech In Utah A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you interested in becoming an xray tech but don't know where to start? Are you overwhelmed by the process and wondering what it takes to become one in Utah? Look no further! In this article, we'll break down the steps you need to take to become an xray tech in Utah.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to becoming an xray tech in Utah is understanding the requirements and certifications. It can be confusing to know which certifications are necessary and what classes you need to take. Additionally, finding the right school or program can be a challenge.

The first step to becoming an xray tech in Utah is to complete a program that is accredited by the Joint Review Committee on Education in Radiologic Technology (JRCERT). This ensures that you are receiving a quality education that meets the standards set by the profession. After completing the program, you will need to take and pass the American Registry of Radiologic Technologists (ARRT) exam to become certified.

Overall, there are several steps you need to take to become an xray tech in Utah. First, research and find an accredited program that meets your needs. Next, complete the program and gain the necessary experience. Finally, take and pass the ARRT exam to become certified. Once you have become certified, you can begin your career as an xray tech in Utah.

What Are the Steps to Becoming an Xray Tech in Utah?

When I decided to become an xray tech in Utah, I was overwhelmed by the process. However, I found that breaking it down into steps made it more manageable. First, I researched different programs and schools and found one that met my needs. I made sure it was accredited by JRCERT and offered the courses I needed to take.

Next, I completed the program and gained hands-on experience through internships and clinical rotations. During this time, I also made sure to study and prepare for the ARRT exam. Once I felt confident in my knowledge, I took and passed the exam, becoming certified as an xray tech in Utah.

What Are the Job Prospects for Xray Techs in Utah?

As with any career, job prospects for xray techs in Utah can vary. However, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of radiologic and MRI technologists is projected to grow 7 percent from 2019 to 2029, faster than the average for all occupations. Additionally, Utah is home to several large hospitals and medical centers, which can provide job opportunities for xray techs.

What Are the Different Xray Tech Specializations?

While the title "xray tech" is often used broadly, there are actually several different specializations within the field. Some of these include computed tomography (CT), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), mammography, and bone densitometry. Each specialization requires additional training and certification beyond the basic ARRT certification.

What Skills Do You Need to Become an Xray Tech in Utah?

In addition to the technical skills required to operate the equipment and perform the necessary procedures, xray techs in Utah also need to have strong communication skills. They work closely with patients, explaining procedures and answering questions. Additionally, they often work as part of a team with other healthcare professionals, so strong teamwork skills are essential.

Is Becoming an Xray Tech in Utah Worth It?

As with any career, the decision to become an xray tech in Utah is a personal one. However, the field offers several benefits, including job security, competitive salaries, and the ability to help others. Additionally, the field is constantly evolving, meaning there is always room for growth and learning.

Conclusion of How to Become an Xray Tech in Utah

Overall, becoming an xray tech in Utah requires dedication and hard work. However, by following the steps outlined in this article and gaining the necessary certifications, you can start a rewarding career in healthcare. Remember to research different programs and schools, gain hands-on experience, and prepare for the ARRT exam. Good luck on your journey to becoming an xray tech in Utah!

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average salary for an xray tech in Utah?

A: According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for radiologic and MRI technologists in Utah was $62,920 in May 2020.

Q: How long does it take to become an xray tech in Utah?

A: The length of time it takes to become an xray tech in Utah can vary depending on the program you choose. Typically, it takes around two years to complete an accredited program and gain the necessary experience.

Q: Can you become an xray tech in Utah with a criminal record?

A: It depends on the nature of the offense. Some offenses may prevent you from becoming certified by the ARRT, while others may not. It is important to be honest about your criminal record when applying for programs and certifications.

Q: What are the working hours like for xray techs in Utah?

A: The working hours for xray techs in Utah can vary depending on the facility and shift. Some xray techs may work a traditional 9-5 schedule, while others may work evenings or weekends.
