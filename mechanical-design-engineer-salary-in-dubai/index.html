Are you considering a career as a mechanical design engineer in Dubai? If so, you may be wondering about the salary prospects in this field. As a mechanical design engineer in Dubai, there are some pain points to consider when it comes to salary. One of the biggest challenges is the cost of living, which can be quite high in this city. Additionally, competition for jobs in the engineering field can be fierce, which can drive down salaries somewhat. So, what can you expect in terms of mechanical design engineer salary in Dubai? Generally, entry-level mechanical design engineers can expect to earn around 8,000 - 10,000 AED per month, while more experienced engineers can earn upwards of 20,000 AED per month.

In summary, mechanical design engineer salary in Dubai can be competitive, but it's important to consider the cost of living and competition in the job market. With the right qualifications and experience, you can earn a comfortable living as a mechanical design engineer in Dubai.
