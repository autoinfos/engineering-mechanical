Discover The Most Popular Audible Books Of 2020

Written by Aprilia Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect audiobook to relax and unwind with? Look no further than the most popular audible books of 2020. These titles have captured the hearts and minds of readers around the world, offering hours of entertainment and escape.

As we all know, 2020 was a year unlike any other. With so many of us stuck at home, audiobooks became a much-needed escape from the stresses of the world. Whether you're looking for a gripping mystery or a heartwarming romance, there's an audiobook out there for you.

So, what are the most popular audible books of 2020? According to Audible, the top titles of the year include a mix of fiction and non-fiction, ranging from thrillers to memoirs.

Among the most popular audible books of 2020 are "Where the Crawdads Sing" by Delia Owens, "The Guest List" by Lucy Foley, "Untamed" by Glennon Doyle, and "The Silent Patient" by Alex Michaelides. These titles have all received critical acclaim and captured the hearts of readers around the world.

The Silent Patient

One of the most popular audible books of 2020 is "The Silent Patient" by Alex Michaelides. This gripping thriller follows the story of Alicia Berenson, a famous painter who murders her husband and then stops speaking altogether. The book has been praised for its suspenseful plot and unexpected twists and turns.

Personally, I found "The Silent Patient" to be a truly captivating listen. The narrator's voice was perfectly suited to the story, and I found myself completely engrossed in the plot. If you're a fan of psychological thrillers, this book is definitely a must-listen.

Untamed

"Untamed" by Glennon Doyle is another one of the most popular audible books of 2020. This memoir is a powerful exploration of what it means to be a woman in today's world, and has been praised for its honesty and vulnerability.

As someone who has struggled with issues of identity and self-acceptance, I found "Untamed" to be an incredibly moving listen. Glennon Doyle's story is one that many women will resonate with, and her message of self-love and acceptance is one that I think is incredibly important.

Where the Crawdads Sing

"Where the Crawdads Sing" by Delia Owens is another one of the most popular audible books of 2020. This novel follows the story of Kya Clark, a young woman who has spent her entire life living in the marshes of North Carolina. When a murder occurs in the town, Kya becomes a prime suspect and must fight to clear her name.

I absolutely loved "Where the Crawdads Sing". The narrator did an incredible job of bringing the story to life, and I found myself completely engrossed in Kya's world. If you're looking for a gripping story with a strong female protagonist, this is definitely a book you won't want to miss.

The Guest List

"The Guest List" by Lucy Foley is a thrilling mystery that has become one of the most popular audible books of 2020. The book follows the story of a wedding on a remote island off the coast of Ireland, where things quickly take a dark turn.

I was completely hooked on "The Guest List" from the very first chapter. The setting was incredibly atmospheric, and the mystery kept me guessing until the very end. If you're a fan of mysteries or thrillers, this book is definitely one you won't want to miss.

Question and Answer:

Q: What makes an audiobook popular?

A: Popular audiobooks typically have engaging plots, relatable characters, and skilled narrators who bring the story to life. They often appeal to a wide range of readers and are recommended by word of mouth.

Q: Can audiobooks help reduce stress?

A: Yes! Audiobooks can be a great way to relax and unwind after a long day. They allow you to escape into a different world and can help take your mind off your worries.

Q: Are audiobooks better than physical books?

A: It really depends on personal preference. Some people prefer physical books because they enjoy the tactile experience of reading. Others prefer audiobooks because they can listen while doing other activities, like exercising or driving.

Q: How can I find the best audiobooks to listen to?

A: There are many ways to discover new audiobooks, including recommendations from friends, book clubs, and online reviews. You can also browse the most popular titles on platforms like Audible and Spotify.

Conclusion of Most Popular Audible Books of 2020

Whether you're a long-time audiobook fan or just getting started, the most popular audible books of 2020 are definitely worth checking out. From gripping thrillers to heartwarming memoirs, there's something for everyone on this list.

So why not pour yourself a cup of tea, curl up with your headphones, and escape into a different world for a while? Trust us, your mind (and your soul) will thank you.
