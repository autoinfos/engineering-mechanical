Factors That Affect Truck Driver Salaries in New Zealand

The amount of money you can earn as a truck driver in New Zealand will depend on several factors, including your experience, the type of truck you drive, and the company you work for. As a general rule, the more experience you have, the more money you can expect to earn. How Much Do Truck Drivers Make in New Zealand?
