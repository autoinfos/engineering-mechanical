How Much Do Automotive General Managers Make

Written by Eveline Oct 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you considering a career in the automotive industry as a general manager? If so, you may be wondering how much money you can expect to make in this position. Many factors can affect a general manager's salary, such as years of experience, location, and the size of the dealership they work for. In this blog post, we will explore how much automotive general managers make and what factors can impact their earnings.

As a general manager in the automotive industry, you may face some challenges related to your earning potential. For example, the industry is highly competitive, and some dealerships may not be willing to pay their general managers as much as they should. Additionally, the job can be stressful, and you may need to work long hours, which can impact your work-life balance.

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the average annual salary for automotive service managers, which includes general managers, was $123,150 as of May 2020. However, this figure can vary widely based on factors such as location, experience, and the size of the dealership. In some areas, general managers can earn salaries well above the average, while in other areas, they may earn less.

In summary, automotive general managers can expect to earn a decent salary, but this can vary widely based on several factors. Factors such as experience, location, and the size of the dealership can all impact your earnings potential.

What is the Starting Salary for an Automotive General Manager?

When I started my career as an automotive general manager, I was making around $60,000 per year. However, this can vary based on your location and the size of the dealership. In some areas, starting salaries can be higher, while in others, they may be lower.

What is the Average Salary for an Automotive General Manager?

The average salary for an automotive general manager is around $123,150 per year, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. However, this figure can vary widely based on factors such as location, experience, and the size of the dealership.

How Can I Increase My Earnings as an Automotive General Manager?

There are several things you can do to increase your earnings as an automotive general manager. First, consider pursuing additional education or certifications to improve your skills and knowledge. This can make you a more valuable employee and potentially lead to higher pay. Additionally, consider seeking out opportunities for advancement within your dealership or seeking employment at a larger dealership with more earning potential.

What Benefits Do Automotive General Managers Receive?

Automotive general managers typically receive benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and paid vacation time. Additionally, some dealerships may offer bonuses or other incentives to their general managers to reward them for their hard work and dedication.

Conclusion of How Much Do Automotive General Managers Make

In conclusion, automotive general managers can expect to earn a decent salary, but this can vary widely based on several factors. Factors such as experience, location, and the size of the dealership can all impact your earnings potential. However, with hard work, dedication, and a willingness to pursue opportunities for advancement, you can increase your earning potential and enjoy a successful career in the automotive industry.
