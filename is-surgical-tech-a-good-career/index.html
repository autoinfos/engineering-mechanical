Is Surgical Tech A Good Career

Written by Bardi Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a surgical tech? If so, you may be wondering if it is a good career choice. Well, the short answer is yes! In this blog post, we will explore the ins and outs of the surgical tech profession and why it may be a great career option for you.

Pain Points of Choosing a Career

When it comes to choosing a career, there are many factors to consider. Some of the pain points that people often face include job security, salary, and job satisfaction. It can be challenging to find a career that meets all of these criteria, but the good news is that surgical techs often do!

Answering the Target Question

So, is surgical tech a good career? Absolutely. Not only is job growth in this field projected to be much faster than average, but the median annual salary is also higher than the national average. Additionally, surgical techs often report high job satisfaction due to the excitement and fast-paced nature of the job.

Summary of Main Points

In summary, choosing a career as a surgical tech can provide job security, a good salary, and job satisfaction. This profession is projected to grow rapidly, making it an excellent option for those who want a stable career. But let's dive into more detail about why surgical tech is a good career choice.

The Exciting World of Surgical Tech

Being a surgical tech can be incredibly exciting. Every day is different, and you never know what kind of surgery you will be assisting with. The job requires excellent attention to detail, as you will be responsible for ensuring that all surgical instruments and equipment are sterilized and ready for use.

One of the most exciting aspects of being a surgical tech is the opportunity to work in a variety of settings. You could work in a hospital, outpatient surgery center, or even a dentist's office. The job also allows for advancement, with the opportunity to become a surgical first assistant or even a surgical technologist instructor.

Job Security and Salary

As mentioned earlier, job growth for surgical techs is projected to be much faster than average. This is due in part to the aging population, who require more surgeries as they get older. Additionally, surgical techs are in high demand, meaning you will likely have no trouble finding a job after completing your training.

The median annual salary for surgical techs is also higher than the national average, with many professionals earning over $50,000 per year. This is an excellent salary for a career that typically requires only an associate's degree.

The Importance of Training

While becoming a surgical tech is a great career choice, it's important to note that proper training is crucial. Most employers require surgical techs to have completed an accredited program and to have obtained certification. This ensures that you have the knowledge and skills necessary to be successful in your job.

Advancement Opportunities

As mentioned earlier, there are many opportunities for advancement in the surgical tech field. One of the most common paths is to become a surgical first assistant, which involves working more closely with the surgeon during surgery. This role requires additional training and certification but can lead to higher pay and more job satisfaction.

Question and Answer Section

Q: How long does it take to become a surgical tech?

A: Most surgical tech programs take between 9 and 24 months to complete.

Q: What kind of person makes a good surgical tech?

A: Successful surgical techs typically have excellent attention to detail, are comfortable working in a fast-paced environment, and have a desire to help others.

Q: Is there room for growth in the surgical tech profession?

A: Yes! As mentioned earlier, there are many opportunities for advancement in the surgical tech field, including becoming a surgical first assistant or a surgical technologist instructor.

Q: What is the job outlook for surgical techs?

A: The job outlook for surgical techs is excellent, with job growth projected to be much faster than average.

Conclusion of Is Surgical Tech a Good Career

In conclusion, choosing a career as a surgical tech can provide job security, a good salary, and job satisfaction. This profession is projected to grow rapidly, making it an excellent option for those who want a stable career. With proper training and certification, you can be on your way to an exciting and fulfilling career as a surgical tech.
