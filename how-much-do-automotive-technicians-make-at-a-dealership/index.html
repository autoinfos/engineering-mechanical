How Much Do Automotive Technicians Make At A Dealership

Written by Aprilia May 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in becoming an automotive technician but wondering how much you can earn at a dealership? The answer is not straightforward as it depends on several factors such as experience, skills, and location. However, there are some general pain points related to how much do automotive technicians make at a dealership that we'll touch upon in this article.

On average, automotive technicians at a dealership make around $45,000 to $60,000 per year. However, this can vary based on various factors such as geographic location, level of experience, certifications, and the dealership's size. For instance, technicians in big cities or high-end dealerships can earn more than those in rural areas or smaller dealerships.

In summary, automotive technicians at a dealership can earn a decent income, but it depends on several factors. Let's dive into more detail about how much do automotive technicians make at a dealership and related keywords.

What Affects How Much Automotive Technicians Make at a Dealership?

I used to work as an automotive technician at a dealership, and I can tell you that several factors affect how much technicians can earn. For instance, if you have certifications such as ASE or manufacturer-specific certifications, you can earn more than someone without them. Additionally, if you specialize in a particular area such as electrical or transmission, you can also earn more.

Moreover, your experience level can also impact your earnings. Technicians with more years of experience typically earn more than those with less experience. However, this may not always be the case as some dealerships may prioritize hiring entry-level technicians to save on costs.

What Are Some Tips to Increase Your Earnings as an Automotive Technician at a Dealership?

One way to increase your earnings as an automotive technician at a dealership is to specialize in a particular area such as electrical or transmission. This can make you more valuable to the dealership, and they may be willing to pay you more. Additionally, getting certified in manufacturer-specific training can also increase your earnings.

Another way to increase your earnings is to negotiate your salary when you get hired or during a performance review. Don't be afraid to ask for a raise if you feel that you deserve it based on your experience, skills, and performance.

What Are Some Challenges of Working as an Automotive Technician at a Dealership?

Working as an automotive technician at a dealership can be challenging due to the high-pressure environment and long working hours. Technicians are often expected to meet tight deadlines and deal with difficult customers, which can be stressful. Additionally, the work can be physically demanding, requiring technicians to stand or crouch for long periods.

How Can You Prepare for a Career as an Automotive Technician at a Dealership?

If you're interested in becoming an automotive technician at a dealership, it's essential to get the right training and certifications. Look for vocational schools or community colleges that offer automotive technology programs. Additionally, getting certified in ASE or manufacturer-specific training can make you more valuable to potential employers.

Conclusion of How Much Do Automotive Technicians Make at a Dealership

In conclusion, automotive technicians at a dealership can earn a decent income, but it depends on several factors such as experience, skills, and location. Getting certified, specializing in a particular area, or negotiating your salary can help increase your earnings. However, working as an automotive technician at a dealership can be challenging due to the high-pressure environment and long working hours.

Question and Answer

Q: How much do entry-level automotive technicians make at a dealership?
A: Entry-level automotive technicians at a dealership can make around $30,000 to $40,000 per year.

Q: How much do experienced automotive technicians make at a dealership?
A: Experienced automotive technicians at a dealership can make around $60,000 to $80,000 per year.

Q: What is the job outlook for automotive technicians at a dealership?
A: According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the employment of automotive service technicians and mechanics is projected to grow 4 percent from 2019 to 2029, which is about as fast as the average for all occupations.

Q: What are some other career options for automotive technicians?
A: Some other career options for automotive technicians include working in independent repair shops, automotive manufacturing, or as fleet maintenance technicians.
