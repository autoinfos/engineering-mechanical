What Is The Starting Salary For Mechanical Engineers In Uae

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a mechanical engineer looking to start your career in the UAE? Are you unsure of what salary to expect? Look no further, as we delve into the world of mechanical engineering salaries in the UAE.

Mechanical engineering is a highly sought-after profession in the UAE, with many opportunities for growth and development. However, with the high demand for mechanical engineers comes a competitive job market, making it essential to know your worth and what you can expect in terms of salary.

The starting salary for mechanical engineers in the UAE varies depending on the level of experience and qualifications. On average, a fresh graduate can expect a starting salary of AED 5,000 to AED 8,000 per month. However, with experience and expertise, this figure can increase significantly, with senior mechanical engineers earning up to AED 40,000 per month.

Personal Experience with Mechanical Engineering Salary in UAE

As a mechanical engineer with two years of experience in the UAE, I can attest that the starting salary can be a bit lower than expected. However, with hard work and dedication, I was able to increase my salary by 30% within a year. It is essential to negotiate your salary and understand your worth in the industry.

Factors Affecting Mechanical Engineering Salary in UAE

Several factors can affect the salary of a mechanical engineer in the UAE. One of the most significant factors is the level of experience, with senior engineers earning significantly more than fresh graduates. Other factors include the industry, company size, location, and qualifications.

Industry

The industry you work in can significantly affect your salary as a mechanical engineer. For example, the oil and gas industry typically pays higher salaries than the construction industry.

Qualifications

Pursuing higher education and obtaining additional certifications can increase your salary as a mechanical engineer. For instance, holding a master's degree in mechanical engineering can increase your salary by up to 20%.

How to Negotiate Your Mechanical Engineering Salary in UAE

When negotiating your salary, it is essential to know your worth and the industry's standards. Research the average salary for a mechanical engineer in your industry and location to have a better understanding of what to expect. Be confident in your skills and expertise and do not be afraid to ask for a higher salary. Remember, negotiating your salary can have a significant impact on your future earnings and career growth.
