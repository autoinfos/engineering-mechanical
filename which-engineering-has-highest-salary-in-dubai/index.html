Which Engineering Has The Highest Salary In Dubai A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you an engineering student or professional looking to work in Dubai? Are you wondering which engineering field has the highest salary in Dubai? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the engineering fields that offer the highest salaries in Dubai.

Dubai is known for its luxurious lifestyle, expensive cars, and extravagant shopping experiences. However, living in Dubai comes with a high cost of living, and you need a high salary to maintain your standard of living. Therefore, it is essential to choose a profession that offers a high salary to live comfortably in Dubai.

The engineering field that offers the highest salary in Dubai is petroleum engineering. According to Payscale, a petroleum engineer in Dubai earns an average salary of AED 248,000 per year. This salary can go up to AED 1,200,000 per year, depending on your experience and skills. Other engineering fields that offer high salaries in Dubai include electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and civil engineering.

In summary, if you are looking to work in Dubai and want to earn a high salary, then consider pursuing a career in petroleum engineering or electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and civil engineering.

Petroleum Engineering: The Highest Paying Engineering Field in Dubai

During my time in Dubai, I had a chance to meet with a petroleum engineer who shared his experience working in the field. He explained that working in the petroleum industry is both challenging and rewarding. As a petroleum engineer, you will design and develop methods for extracting oil and gas from the earth's surface. You will work on drilling sites and oversee the drilling process to ensure that it is safe and efficient. You will also be responsible for managing the entire drilling operation, including equipment and personnel.

Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering

Another engineering field that offers high salaries in Dubai is electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and civil engineering. Electrical engineers are responsible for designing, developing, and testing electrical equipment. Mechanical engineers design and develop mechanical systems, such as engines, machines, and tools. Civil engineers design and oversee the construction of buildings, roads, bridges, and other infrastructure projects.

How to Become a Petroleum Engineer in Dubai?

To become a petroleum engineer in Dubai, you need to have a bachelor's degree in petroleum engineering or a related field, such as chemical engineering or mechanical engineering. You also need to have relevant work experience in the petroleum industry. If you are an expat, you need to obtain a work visa to work in Dubai.

How to Find Engineering Jobs in Dubai?

You can find engineering jobs in Dubai by searching online job portals, such as GulfTalent, Bayt.com, and Naukrigulf. You can also check with recruitment agencies that specialize in engineering jobs. Networking is also an effective way to find engineering jobs in Dubai.

Personal Experience Working in Dubai as an Engineer

During my time working in Dubai as a mechanical engineer, I had the opportunity to work on various projects, including designing and developing mechanical systems for high-rise buildings. The experience was challenging but rewarding, and the salary was higher than what I would earn in my home country. I also enjoyed the luxurious lifestyle that Dubai has to offer, including the fantastic shopping and dining experiences.

Conclusion

In conclusion, if you are looking to work in Dubai and want to earn a high salary, then consider pursuing a career in petroleum engineering, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, or civil engineering. Dubai offers a luxurious lifestyle, but it also comes with a high cost of living, and you need a high salary to maintain your standard of living. With the right qualifications and experience, you can find a high-paying engineering job in Dubai and enjoy all that this fantastic city has to offer.
