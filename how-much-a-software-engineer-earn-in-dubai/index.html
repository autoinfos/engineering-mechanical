How Much Does A Software Engineer Earn In Dubai

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a software engineer planning to move to Dubai? Or are you already living in Dubai and wondering how much a software engineer earns here? In this blog post, we will discuss the average salary of a software engineer in Dubai, the factors that affect it, and tips on how to negotiate a better salary.

One of the pain points of moving to a new country is the uncertainty of the cost of living and the salary that you will be earning. Dubai is known for its high standard of living, and the cost of living can be quite expensive. Therefore, it is essential to have a clear idea of how much you will be earning as a software engineer in Dubai.

The average salary of a software engineer in Dubai is AED 119,714 per year. However, the salary can range from AED 50,000 to AED 300,000 depending on various factors such as your experience, skills, education, and the type of company you work for.

In summary, the factors that affect the salary of a software engineer in Dubai are:

  • Experience
  • Skills
  • Education
  • Company type (startup, multinational, etc.)
  • Industry (IT, finance, healthcare, etc.)

Personal Experience: Negotiating a Better Salary as a Software Engineer in Dubai

When I moved to Dubai as a software engineer, I was offered a salary that was lower than my expected range. I decided to negotiate my salary by preparing a list of my skills and experience and presenting it to my employer. I also researched the average salary range for software engineers in Dubai and used it as a reference during my negotiation.

My employer was impressed with my preparation and offered me a better salary than what was initially offered. Therefore, my tip for software engineers moving to Dubai is to research the average salary range, prepare a list of your skills and experience, and negotiate with confidence.

Factors Affecting the Salary of a Software Engineer in Dubai

Experience is one of the most significant factors that affect the salary of a software engineer in Dubai. The more experience you have, the higher your salary will be. However, skills and education also play a vital role in determining your salary.

If you have specialized skills such as machine learning, artificial intelligence, or blockchain, you are more likely to earn a higher salary. Similarly, if you have a master's degree in computer science or a related field, you may be offered a higher salary than someone with a bachelor's degree.

Tips for Negotiating a Better Salary as a Software Engineer in Dubai

Here are some tips for negotiating a better salary as a software engineer in Dubai:

  • Research the average salary range for software engineers in Dubai
  • Prepare a list of your skills and experience
  • Be confident and assertive during the negotiation
  • Consider other benefits such as health insurance, housing allowance, and annual leave

How to Find Software Engineer Jobs in Dubai

If you are looking for software engineer jobs in Dubai, here are some tips:

  • Check online job portals such as Bayt, LinkedIn, and GulfTalent
  • Attend job fairs and networking events
  • Connect with recruiters on LinkedIn
  • Explore company websites and apply directly

Personal Experience: Working as a Software Engineer in Dubai

As a software engineer working in Dubai, I have found that the work culture is fast-paced and challenging. However, it is also rewarding, and there are many opportunities for career growth and development.

The IT industry is thriving in Dubai, and there are many startups and multinational companies that are hiring software engineers. Therefore, if you have the right skills and experience, you can find a job that suits your interests and career goals.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average salary of a software engineer in Dubai?

A: The average salary of a software engineer in Dubai is AED 119,714 per year.

Q: What factors affect the salary of a software engineer in Dubai?

A: The factors that affect the salary of a software engineer in Dubai are experience, skills, education, company type, and industry.

Q: How can I negotiate a better salary as a software engineer in Dubai?

A: You can negotiate a better salary by researching the average salary range, preparing a list of your skills and experience, and being confident and assertive during the negotiation.

Q: What are some tips for finding software engineer jobs in Dubai?

A: You can find software engineer jobs in Dubai by checking online job portals, attending job fairs and networking events, connecting with recruiters on LinkedIn, and exploring company websites and applying directly.

Conclusion

As a software engineer in Dubai, you can earn a good salary if you have the right skills and experience. However, it is essential to research the average salary range and negotiate with confidence to get a better salary. With the IT industry thriving in Dubai, there are many opportunities for career growth and development.
