FAQs About Car Shipping Companies in UAE

Q: How long does it take to ship a car in UAE?

A: The time it takes to ship a car in UAE depends on the distance between the pickup and delivery locations, as well as the type of shipping service you choose. Generally, it takes between 1 to 7 days for local transport and up to 45 days for international transport.

Q: How much does it cost to ship a car in UAE?

A: The cost of shipping a car in UAE depends on various factors, such as the distance between the pickup and delivery locations, the type of shipping service you choose, and the size and weight of your vehicle. Generally, it can cost between AED 500 to AED 5,000 for local transport and up to AED 20,000 for international transport.

Q: Can I ship my car internationally from UAE?

A: Yes, you can ship your car internationally from UAE. However, you will need to comply with the regulations and requirements of the destination country, such as customs clearance, import taxes, and vehicle registration.
