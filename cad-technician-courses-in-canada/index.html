Types of CAD Technician Courses in Canada

There are several types of CAD technician courses available in Canada, including certificate programs, diploma programs, and degree programs. Certificate programs are typically the shortest and most focused, while degree programs are the longest and most comprehensive.

Certificate programs typically take 6-12 months to complete and focus on developing specific skills related to CAD software. Diploma programs take 1-2 years to complete and offer a more comprehensive education in CAD technology. Degree programs take 3-4 years to complete and offer the most comprehensive education in CAD technology, as well as other related fields such as engineering and architecture.
