Is Aws Developer Certification Worth It

Written by Frank Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting an AWS developer certification? If so, you may be wondering if it's worth the time and effort. In this blog post, we'll explore the benefits of becoming an AWS certified developer and help you decide if this certification is right for you.

As technology becomes more advanced, the demand for skilled developers continues to grow. However, competition in the job market is high, and it can be challenging to stand out from the crowd. That's where certifications come in. They demonstrate to potential employers that you have the skills and knowledge needed to excel in your role.

The short answer is yes; AWS developer certification is worth it. Not only does it show that you have expertise in AWS development, but it can also lead to better job opportunities and higher salaries. According to Payscale, the average salary for an AWS-certified developer is $113,000 per year.

Personal Experience with AWS Developer Certification

When I decided to pursue an AWS developer certification, I was a bit skeptical. I wasn't sure if it would make a significant impact on my career. However, after completing the certification, I noticed that more job opportunities became available to me. Employers were impressed by my certification and were more likely to consider me for roles that required AWS development experience.

Benefits of AWS Developer Certification

There are many benefits to becoming an AWS certified developer. Firstly, it demonstrates your expertise in AWS development, which is a highly sought-after skill in today's job market. Additionally, having this certification can lead to better job opportunities and higher salaries.

Furthermore, AWS is a cloud computing platform that is used by many organizations worldwide. By becoming certified, you'll gain a deep understanding of how AWS works and become an expert in using its services. This knowledge can be applied to various job roles, including software development, IT operations, and cloud architecture.

Cost and Time Commitment

One of the drawbacks of becoming an AWS certified developer is the cost and time commitment. The certification exam costs $150, and you'll need to study and prepare for the exam thoroughly. However, the benefits of becoming certified far outweigh the costs.

How to Prepare for the AWS Developer Certification Exam

If you're interested in becoming an AWS certified developer, there are several steps you can take to prepare for the exam. Firstly, you'll need to have a solid understanding of AWS services and how they work together. You can do this by taking online courses, reading documentation, or attending AWS events.

Next, you'll need to practice using AWS services in real-world scenarios. This will help you understand how to apply your knowledge to practical situations. Finally, you'll need to take practice exams to test your knowledge and identify areas where you need to improve.

FAQs: Is AWS Developer Certification Worth It?

Q: What are the benefits of becoming an AWS certified developer?

A: There are many benefits to becoming an AWS certified developer, including demonstrating your expertise in AWS development, gaining a deep understanding of how AWS works, and having better job opportunities and higher salaries.

Q: How much does the AWS developer certification exam cost?

A: The AWS developer certification exam costs $150.

Q: How long does it take to prepare for the AWS developer certification exam?

A: The amount of time it takes to prepare for the AWS developer certification exam varies depending on your experience and knowledge. However, it typically takes several weeks to several months to prepare thoroughly.

Q: Is it worth the cost and time commitment to become an AWS certified developer?

A: Yes, the benefits of becoming an AWS certified developer far outweigh the cost and time commitment. The certification demonstrates your expertise in AWS development, which can lead to better job opportunities and higher salaries.

Conclusion of Is AWS Developer Certification Worth It

Overall, AWS developer certification is undoubtedly worth it. It demonstrates your expertise in AWS development, which is a highly sought-after skill in today's job market. Additionally, having this certification can lead to better job opportunities and higher salaries. While the cost and time commitment may seem daunting, the benefits of becoming certified far outweigh any drawbacks. If you're looking to take your career to the next level, AWS developer certification is an excellent investment.
