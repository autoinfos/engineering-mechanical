Sales Jobs In London With No Experience How To Get Started

Written by Christine May 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for an exciting career in sales but don't have any experience? Maybe you've just graduated from university or are looking for a career change. Whatever your situation, finding the right sales job in London can be challenging, especially if you don't have any experience.

Many job postings require years of experience in sales, which can be discouraging for those just starting. But don't worry, there are plenty of sales jobs in London available for those with no experience. In fact, many companies are looking for fresh talent to mold into the perfect salesperson.

So, how can you break into the world of sales in London without any experience? Here are some tips to get you started:

Targeting Entry-Level Sales Jobs

The best way to start your sales career in London is by targeting entry-level sales jobs. These positions are designed for those with little to no experience in sales and offer on-the-job training to help you develop your skills. Some common entry-level sales jobs in London include sales assistant, telesales representative, and customer service representative.

When I was starting my sales career in London, I targeted sales assistant positions. These roles allowed me to learn the basics of sales, such as customer service, product knowledge, and communication skills. From there, I was able to move up the ladder and eventually become a top-performing salesperson.

Networking and Building Relationships

Networking is crucial in any industry, and sales is no exception. Building relationships with people in your industry can help you learn about new job opportunities, get references for future employers, and even secure a job offer. Attend industry events, join sales groups on LinkedIn, and connect with other salespeople in your community to build your network.

When I was starting out, I attended as many sales events and conferences as possible. I made sure to introduce myself to as many people as I could and exchange contact information. These relationships helped me secure my first sales job in London.

Developing Your Skills

Even if you don't have any experience in sales, there are skills you can develop that will make you a more attractive candidate. For example, communication skills, problem-solving abilities, and a positive attitude are all essential in sales. Take courses or attend workshops that can help you develop these skills and add them to your resume.

When I was starting my sales career in London, I took a public speaking course to help me improve my communication skills. This course helped me become more confident when speaking with clients, which ultimately helped me close more deals.

Staying Motivated

Starting a career in sales can be challenging, especially if you don't have any experience. It's important to stay motivated and positive, even when facing rejection or setbacks. Remember that every rejection is an opportunity to learn and improve your sales skills.

When I was starting my sales career in London, I faced a lot of rejection. But I used each rejection as an opportunity to improve my sales pitch and communication skills. Eventually, my hard work paid off, and I secured my first sales job in London.

A: Remember that every rejection is an opportunity to learn and improve your sales skills. Stay positive and keep working hard, and eventually, your hard work will pay off.

Conclusion of Sales Jobs in London with No Experience

Breaking into the world of sales in London without any experience can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By targeting entry-level sales jobs, networking and building relationships, developing your skills, and staying motivated, you can start your sales career in London and eventually become a top-performing salesperson.
