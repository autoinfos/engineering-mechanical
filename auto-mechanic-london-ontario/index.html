Question and Answer

Q: How often should I have my car serviced?

A: It's recommended to have your car serviced every six months to a year, depending on the make and model of your vehicle. It's also essential to have your car serviced before any long road trips.

Q: What should I do if my car breaks down on the highway?

A: If your car breaks down on the highway, pull over to the side of the road as far as possible and turn on your hazard lights. Call Auto Mechanic London Ontario's emergency service for assistance and wait for them to arrive.

Q: How can I tell if my brakes need to be replaced?

A: Signs that your brakes need to be replaced include squeaking or grinding noises, a spongy brake pedal, or a vibration when you apply the brakes.

Q: How often should I rotate my tires?

A: It's recommended to rotate your tires every 6,000 to 8,000 miles to ensure even wear and extend the life of your tires.
