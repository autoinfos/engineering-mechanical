What Does An Automotive General Manager Do

Written by Bardi Jun 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Managing an automotive dealership can be a challenging job. Automotive general managers are responsible for overseeing all aspects of dealership operations, from sales and marketing to service and financing. They must ensure that the dealership is profitable and that customers are satisfied with their experience.

One of the biggest pain points related to the automotive general manager job description is the need to balance the dealership's financial goals with the needs of customers. This can be a delicate balancing act, as the dealership must make a profit while also providing excellent service and support.

The target of the automotive general manager job description is someone who is experienced in automotive sales and customer service. They must be able to lead a team of salespeople, service technicians, and other staff members, while also managing inventory, finances, and other aspects of the dealership.

In summary, the automotive general manager job description requires a person who can manage all aspects of an automotive dealership, from sales and marketing to service and financing. They must balance the dealership's financial goals with the needs of customers and ensure that the dealership is profitable while also providing excellent service and support.

Automotive General Manager Job Description and Target

When I was working as an automotive general manager, my main focus was on ensuring that the dealership was running smoothly and that customers were satisfied with their experience. This meant that I had to be skilled in both sales and customer service, as well as finance and inventory management.

One of the key responsibilities of an automotive general manager is to oversee the sales team and ensure that they are meeting their targets. This involves setting sales goals, monitoring performance, and providing coaching and feedback to help salespeople improve their skills.

Another important aspect of the job is managing the dealership's inventory. This means ordering new vehicles and ensuring that the dealership has a variety of options available to customers. It also involves managing the dealership's used car inventory, including pricing and marketing vehicles to potential buyers.

Skills and Qualifications for an Automotive General Manager

To be successful as an automotive general manager, there are several key skills and qualifications that are necessary. These include:

1. Sales and Customer Service Skills

The automotive general manager must be skilled in sales and customer service. They must be able to lead the sales team and ensure that customers are satisfied with their experience.

2. Financial Management Skills

The automotive general manager must be able to manage finances, balance the dealership's budget, and ensure that the dealership is profitable.

3. Inventory Management Skills

The automotive general manager must be able to manage inventory, order new vehicles, and ensure that the dealership has a variety of options available to customers.

4. Leadership Skills

The automotive general manager must be able to lead a team of salespeople, service technicians, and other staff members, and ensure that they are working together effectively to achieve the dealership's goals.

How to Become an Automotive General Manager

To become an automotive general manager, you typically need several years of experience in automotive sales and customer service. You may also need a degree in business administration or a related field. Some dealerships may require certification from the National Automobile Dealers Association (NADA) or other industry organizations.

Conclusion of Automotive General Manager Job Description

The automotive general manager job description is a challenging and rewarding role that requires a range of skills and qualifications. From sales and customer service to financial and inventory management, the automotive general manager must be able to manage all aspects of the dealership to ensure that it is profitable and that customers are satisfied with their experience.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some of the biggest challenges facing automotive general managers?

A: One of the biggest challenges is balancing the dealership's financial goals with the needs of customers. This requires a delicate balancing act to ensure that the dealership is profitable while also providing excellent service and support.

Q: What skills are necessary to be successful as an automotive general manager?

A: Sales and customer service skills, financial management skills, inventory management skills, and leadership skills are all necessary to be successful as an automotive general manager.

Q: What qualifications are required to become an automotive general manager?

A: Several years of experience in automotive sales and customer service are typically required, as well as a degree in business administration or a related field. Certification from the National Automobile Dealers Association (NADA) or other industry organizations may also be required.

Q: What is the job outlook for automotive general managers?

A: The job outlook for automotive general managers is positive, with steady demand for experienced professionals in the field.
