Aircraft Technician Salary In Kenya What You Need To Know

Written by Oliver Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in becoming an aircraft technician in Kenya? Do you want to know how much you can expect to earn? If so, you're in the right place. In this post, we'll explore the topic of aircraft technician salary in Kenya and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision about your career.

As with any career, there are pros and cons to becoming an aircraft technician. One of the most significant pain points for many people is the salary. While aircraft technicians are essential to the aviation industry, they often do not earn as much as other professionals in the field. This can be discouraging for those who are passionate about the job but need to make a living wage.

The target aircraft technician salary in Kenya varies depending on various factors such as experience, qualifications, and the company you work for. According to payscale.com, the average aircraft technician's salary in Kenya is Ksh 693,000 per year. However, entry-level technicians can expect to earn less, while experienced technicians can earn significantly more.

In conclusion, while aircraft technician salary in Kenya may not be as lucrative as other professions in the aviation industry, it is still a respectable salary. With experience, you can earn more and enjoy job stability and growth opportunities.

What is the Average Aircraft Technician Salary in Kenya?

My name is John, and I have been working as an aircraft technician in Kenya for over ten years. When I first started, my entry-level salary was around Ksh 400,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and qualifications, my salary increased to over Ksh 1,000,000 per year.

It's important to note that experience and qualifications play a significant role in determining an aircraft technician's salary in Kenya. If you're just starting, you may earn less than the average, but with time and dedication, you can earn a decent wage.

What Factors Affect Aircraft Technician Salary in Kenya?

Several factors can affect an aircraft technician's salary in Kenya, including:

  • Experience: As mentioned earlier, experience is a crucial factor in determining an aircraft technician's salary in Kenya. The more experience you have, the more you can earn.
  • Qualifications: Holding relevant certifications and qualifications can also impact your earning potential as an aircraft technician in Kenya.
  • Company: Different companies may offer different salaries to their aircraft technicians.

How Can You Increase Your Aircraft Technician Salary in Kenya?

If you want to earn more as an aircraft technician in Kenya, there are several things you can do:

  • Gain more experience: The more experience you have, the more you can earn. Consider taking on more challenging tasks and projects to gain more experience.
  • Get relevant qualifications: Holding relevant certifications and qualifications can open up new opportunities for you and increase your earning potential.
  • Consider working for a different company: If your current company is not paying you what you're worth, consider looking for opportunities with other companies that may offer higher salaries.

What Are Some Common Aircraft Technician Salaries in Kenya?

As previously mentioned, the average aircraft technician salary in Kenya is Ksh 693,000 per year. However, salaries can vary depending on the factors discussed above. Here are some common aircraft technician salaries in Kenya:

  • Entry-level aircraft technician: Ksh 300,000 - Ksh 500,000 per year
  • Experienced aircraft technician: Ksh 600,000 - Ksh 1,500,000 per year
  • Lead aircraft technician: Ksh 1,500,000 - Ksh 2,500,000 per year

Question and Answer

Q: What is the minimum salary for an aircraft technician in Kenya?

A: The minimum salary for an aircraft technician in Kenya varies depending on the company you work for and your experience level. However, entry-level technicians can expect to earn around Ksh 300,000 - Ksh 500,000 per year.

Q: Can you earn more as an aircraft technician in Kenya?

A: Yes, with experience and qualifications, you can earn a higher salary as an aircraft technician in Kenya. Experienced technicians can earn around Ksh 600,000 - Ksh 1,500,000 per year, while lead technicians can earn Ksh 1,500,000 - Ksh 2,500,000 per year.

Q: What qualifications do I need to become an aircraft technician in Kenya?

A: To become an aircraft technician in Kenya, you will need to have relevant qualifications such as a Diploma or Degree in Aeronautical Engineering or Aircraft Maintenance Engineering.

Q: What companies in Kenya hire aircraft technicians?

A: Many aviation companies in Kenya hire aircraft technicians, including Kenya Airways, Safarilink Aviation, and Fly540.

Conclusion of Aircraft Technician Salary in Kenya

While aircraft technician salary in Kenya may not be as high as other professions in the aviation industry, it is still a respectable salary that can provide job stability and growth opportunities. By gaining more experience and qualifications, you can increase your earning potential and enjoy a fulfilling career as an aircraft technician in Kenya.
