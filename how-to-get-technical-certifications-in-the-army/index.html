How To Get Technical Certifications In The Army A Complete Guide

Written by William Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to further your career in the Army and wondering how to get technical certifications? Technical certifications can open up a world of opportunities, from promotions to new job prospects. In this blog post, we'll provide you with a complete guide on how to get technical certifications in the Army.

One of the most significant pain points when it comes to getting technical certifications in the Army is the amount of time and effort it takes. It can be challenging to balance your work and personal life with studying for a technical certification exam. Additionally, finding the right resources and support can be a struggle.

First and foremost, to get technical certifications in the Army, you need to have a solid understanding of the subject matter. This means taking the time to study and prepare for the exams thoroughly. You can do this by attending courses or training sessions offered by the Army, utilizing online resources or finding a mentor who can guide you through the process.

To summarize, the key to getting technical certifications in the Army is to dedicate the time and effort required to prepare for the exams. This includes finding the right resources and support, such as attending courses or training sessions, utilizing online resources, and finding a mentor.

Utilize Your Resources

When it comes to getting technical certifications in the Army, it's essential to utilize all the resources available to you. One of the best resources is the Army Credentialing Assistance (CA) program, which provides funding for soldiers to earn technical certifications. I personally utilized this program and was able to earn several technical certifications, which helped me advance in my career.

Another resource is the Army e-Learning platform, which offers courses and training sessions for various technical certifications. Additionally, you can find study materials and practice exams through online resources such as CompTIA or Cisco.

Find a Mentor

Having a mentor who has already earned technical certifications can be incredibly helpful in navigating the process. They can provide guidance, support, and advice on the best study materials and resources to use. I was fortunate enough to have a mentor who helped me prepare for my technical certification exams, which greatly benefited my success.

Take Advantage of Tuition Assistance

Another resource available to soldiers looking to earn technical certifications is Tuition Assistance (TA). This program provides funding for soldiers to attend college courses or earn technical certifications. Utilizing TA can help you achieve your career goals while also furthering your education.

Study and Prepare Diligently

Lastly, it's crucial to study and prepare diligently for your technical certification exams. This means dedicating the necessary time and effort to studying and utilizing all the resources available to you. It's also essential to take practice exams and quizzes to ensure you're fully prepared for the actual exam.

Question and Answer

Q: What technical certifications are available in the Army?

A: There are a variety of technical certifications available in the Army, including CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, and Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA).

Q: How much does it cost to get a technical certification in the Army?

A: The cost of earning a technical certification in the Army varies depending on the certification and the resources utilized. However, the Army Credentialing Assistance program provides funding for soldiers to earn technical certifications, which can greatly reduce the cost.

Q: How long does it take to prepare for a technical certification exam?

A: The amount of time it takes to prepare for a technical certification exam varies depending on the individual and the certification. However, it's recommended to dedicate at least 3-6 months of consistent studying to ensure you're fully prepared.

Q: Can I earn technical certifications while on active duty?

A: Yes, soldiers can earn technical certifications while on active duty through various resources such as the Army Credentialing Assistance program and Tuition Assistance.

Conclusion of How to Get Technical Certifications in the Army

Earning technical certifications in the Army can be a challenging but rewarding process. By utilizing the resources available to you, finding a mentor, taking advantage of tuition assistance, and studying diligently, you can achieve your career goals and advance in your field. Remember, the key is to dedicate the necessary time and effort to prepare for the exams thoroughly.
