Additionally, maintenance mechanic helpers may be responsible for ordering parts and supplies, as well as keeping detailed records of all repairs and maintenance performed.

The Skills You Need to Succeed as a Maintenance Mechanic Helper

While there are many skills that can be useful in a career as a maintenance mechanic helper, there are a few that are particularly important. These include:

Technical Skills

As a maintenance mechanic helper, you'll need to have a solid understanding of how machinery and equipment works. This can involve knowledge of electrical systems, hydraulic systems, and mechanical systems, among other things. Additionally, you'll need to be comfortable using a variety of tools and equipment, such as wrenches, pliers, and screwdrivers.

Problem-Solving Skills

When machinery or equipment breaks down, it's up to the maintenance mechanic helper to figure out what went wrong and how to fix it. This requires strong problem-solving skills, as well as the ability to think on your feet and come up with creative solutions to unexpected problems.

The Job Outlook for Maintenance Mechanic Helpers

The job outlook for maintenance mechanic helpers is generally positive. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of maintenance and repair workers is projected to grow 4 percent from 2019 to 2029, about as fast as the average for all occupations. This growth is due to an increased emphasis on preventative maintenance, as well as the need for workers to maintain and repair new types of equipment and machinery.

Question and Answer

Q. What kind of education and training do I need to become a maintenance mechanic helper?

A. While there are no specific educational requirements to become a maintenance mechanic helper, it can be helpful to have a high school diploma or equivalent. Additionally, many maintenance mechanic helpers receive on-the-job training from experienced mechanics.

Q. What kind of salary can I expect as a maintenance mechanic helper?

A. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for maintenance and repair workers, including maintenance mechanic helpers, was $41,420 in May 2020.

Q. Is there room for advancement in a career as a maintenance mechanic helper?

A. Yes, there is room for advancement in a career as a maintenance mechanic helper. With experience and additional training, you may be able to advance to a maintenance mechanic position, which typically comes with a higher salary and more responsibilities.

Q. What kind of working conditions can I expect as a maintenance mechanic helper?

A. Maintenance mechanic helpers may work in a variety of settings, from factories to hospitals to schools.
