What Are the Key Responsibilities of an Automotive Service Manager in Cape Town?

The key responsibilities of an automotive service manager in Cape Town include:

1. Managing a Team of Technicians:

One of the most important responsibilities of an automotive service manager is managing a team of technicians. This includes hiring, training, and supervising technicians to ensure they are providing high-quality service to customers.

2. Ensuring Customer Satisfaction:

Another key responsibility of an automotive service manager is ensuring customer satisfaction. This involves communicating with customers to understand their needs and concerns, and working with technicians to address any issues that arise.

3. Maintaining Financial Health:

An automotive service manager is also responsible for maintaining the financial health of the business. This includes managing budgets, tracking expenses, and identifying opportunities to increase revenue.
