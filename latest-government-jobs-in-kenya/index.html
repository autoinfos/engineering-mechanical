What are the Latest Government Jobs in Kenya?

The latest government jobs in Kenya are available in different sectors such as health, education, security, and finance, among others. Some of the latest government jobs in Kenya include:

1. Teachers Service Commission (TSC) Jobs

If you have a degree in education, then you can apply for the latest TSC jobs. These jobs are available in different counties across Kenya, and the requirements vary depending on the position applied for. To apply for these jobs, you need to have a TSC number and meet the minimum qualifications set by the commission.

2. Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) Jobs

KRA is responsible for collecting revenue on behalf of the government of Kenya. The latest KRA jobs are available in different departments such as human resources, finance, and IT, among others. To apply for these jobs, you need to have a degree in a relevant field, meet the minimum qualifications set by KRA, and have relevant work experience.

3. Kenya Defence Forces (KDF) Jobs

KDF is responsible for defending Kenya from external aggression. The latest KDF jobs are available in different departments such as the army, navy, and air force, among others. To apply for these jobs, you need to meet the minimum qualifications set by KDF, pass the medical and physical fitness tests, and be of good conduct.

4. Kenya Police Service (KPS) Jobs

KPS is responsible for maintaining law and order in Kenya. The latest KPS jobs are available in different departments such as administration, criminal investigations, and traffic, among others. To apply for these jobs, you need to meet the minimum qualifications set by KPS, pass the medical and physical fitness tests, and be of good conduct.

5. Ministry of Health Jobs

The Ministry of Health is responsible for ensuring the health and well-being of Kenyans. The latest Ministry of Health jobs are available in different departments such as nursing, pharmacy, and laboratory, among others. To apply for these jobs, you need to have a degree in a relevant field, meet the minimum qualifications set by the ministry, and have relevant work experience.

How to Apply for the Latest Government Jobs in Kenya

To apply for the latest government jobs in Kenya, you need to follow the following steps:

1. Visit the Relevant Government Website

Each government institution has its website where they advertise the latest job vacancies. Visit the website to check for the latest job vacancies and their requirements.

2. Prepare Your Application Documents

Once you have identified the job vacancy that suits your qualifications, prepare your application documents. These documents include your CV, cover letter, and academic certificates.

3. Apply for the Job

Submit your application documents through the relevant portal provided on the government website.

4. Wait for Feedback

After submitting your application, wait for feedback from the relevant government institution. If you are shortlisted, you will be invited for an interview.
