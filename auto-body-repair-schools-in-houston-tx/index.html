Choosing the Right Auto Body Repair School in Houston, TX

When it comes to choosing the right auto body repair school in Houston, TX, there are a few key factors to consider. One of the most important is accreditation. You want to make sure that the school you choose is accredited by a reputable agency, such as the National Automotive Technicians Education Foundation (NATEF).

Another factor to consider is the curriculum. You want to make sure that the school you choose covers all of the essential topics in auto body repair, such as welding, painting, and panel replacement. Additionally, you'll want to look for a school that offers hands-on training opportunities, such as internships or co-op programs.
