How Much Do Engineers Make A Year In Australia

Written by Christine Jun 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career in engineering in Australia, but unsure of how much you can expect to earn? The salary of an engineer can vary depending on various factors, such as the type of engineering, location, and experience. In this post, we will explore how much engineers make a year in Australia and what factors can impact their salary.

Engineering is a highly-skilled profession that requires years of education and training. Therefore, it's natural to wonder whether the salary is commensurate with the investment in education and training. Many engineers in Australia feel that they are not being compensated adequately for their work, which can cause frustration and dissatisfaction in the workplace.

The average salary for an engineer in Australia is around AUD 85,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on the type of engineering. For example, a civil engineer can expect to earn around AUD 80,000 per year, while a software engineer can earn around AUD 100,000 per year. Engineers with more experience and expertise can earn significantly more.

In summary, the salary of an engineer in Australia can vary depending on various factors, such as the type of engineering, location, and experience. However, the average salary for an engineer in Australia is around AUD 85,000 per year.

Factors that Affect an Engineer's Salary in Australia

When it comes to determining an engineer's salary in Australia, several factors can come into play. One of the most significant factors is the type of engineering. Some engineering fields, such as software engineering, tend to pay higher salaries than others, such as civil engineering.

Location can also impact an engineer's salary. Engineers who work in large cities such as Sydney and Melbourne tend to earn more than those who work in smaller towns or rural areas.

Experience is another key factor that can impact an engineer's salary. Engineers with more experience and expertise tend to earn more than those who are just starting their careers.

Types of Engineering and Their Salaries in Australia

As mentioned earlier, the type of engineering can impact an engineer's salary. Here are some of the most popular types of engineering and their expected salaries in Australia:

Civil Engineering

Civil engineers in Australia can expect to earn an average salary of around AUD 80,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on experience, location, and other factors.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineers in Australia can expect to earn an average salary of around AUD 85,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on experience, location, and other factors.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineers in Australia can expect to earn an average salary of around AUD 90,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on experience, location, and other factors.

Personal Experience of an Engineer in Australia

As an engineer working in Australia, I can attest that the salary can be somewhat underwhelming, especially for those just starting their careers. However, with experience and expertise, it is possible to earn a comfortable salary in the engineering field. For me, the satisfaction of working on challenging projects and making a positive impact on society outweighs any concerns about salary.

Is Becoming an Engineer in Australia Worth it?

If you're considering becoming an engineer in Australia, it's natural to wonder whether it's worth the investment in education and training. Ultimately, the answer to this question depends on your passions and goals. If you're passionate about making a difference in the world through engineering and are willing to put in the effort to develop your skills and expertise, then becoming an engineer can be a rewarding career choice.
