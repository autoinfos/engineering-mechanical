The Types of Auto Tech Jobs Available

There are many different types of auto tech jobs available, each with its own set of requirements and responsibilities. Some of the most common types of auto tech jobs include:

Automotive Service Technicians and Mechanics

These professionals are responsible for diagnosing and repairing various types of vehicles, including cars, trucks, and SUVs. They may work in a dealership, independent repair shop, or other automotive service facility.

How to Become an Automotive Service Technician or Mechanic

To become an automotive service technician or mechanic, you will need to complete a postsecondary education program in automotive technology. You may also need to obtain certification from the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE).

Auto Body and Glass Repairers

These professionals are responsible for repairing and replacing damaged body parts and glass on vehicles. They may work in a collision repair shop or other automotive repair facility.

How to Become an Auto Body or Glass Repairer

To become an auto body or glass repairer, you will need to complete a postsecondary education program in automotive body repair. You may also need to obtain certification from ASE.
