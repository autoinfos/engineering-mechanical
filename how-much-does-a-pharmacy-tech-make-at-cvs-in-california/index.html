What is a Pharmacy Tech?

A Pharmacy Tech is a healthcare professional who works in a pharmacy to assist licensed pharmacists in dispensing medications and providing customer service. Pharmacy Techs also have administrative responsibilities, such as managing inventory and processing insurance claims.
