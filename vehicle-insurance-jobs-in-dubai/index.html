Types of Vehicle Insurance Coverage in Dubai

In Dubai, there are several types of vehicle insurance coverage, including third-party liability insurance, comprehensive insurance, and personal accident insurance. Third-party liability insurance is mandatory for all vehicle owners in Dubai, while comprehensive insurance provides additional coverage for damage to your own vehicle. Personal accident insurance provides coverage for injuries sustained in an accident.

How to Make a Claim for Vehicle Insurance in Dubai

If you need to make a claim for vehicle insurance in Dubai, the process can vary depending on the type of coverage you have. For third-party liability insurance, you should contact your insurance company immediately after an accident and provide them with all relevant information, including the details of the other driver involved. For comprehensive insurance, you should also contact your insurance company as soon as possible and provide them with all relevant information. They may require a police report and an assessment of the damage to your vehicle.
