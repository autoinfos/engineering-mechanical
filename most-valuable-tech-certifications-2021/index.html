Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate

One of the most valuable tech certifications 2021 is the Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate. This certification is designed for IT professionals who manage cloud services that span storage, security, networking, and compute cloud capabilities. It demonstrates your ability to manage Azure subscriptions, configure virtual networking, secure identities, and more. Personally, I found this certification to be very valuable in my career because it allowed me to specialize in cloud computing and demonstrate my expertise to potential employers.

Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)

The Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) certification is another valuable tech certification in 2021. It is designed for professionals who want to build and maintain networks in small to medium-sized businesses. The certification covers topics such as routing and switching, network security, and wireless networking. Personally, I found this certification to be valuable because it allowed me to specialize in networking and demonstrate my knowledge to potential employers.

Google Certified Professional Cloud Architect

The Google Certified Professional Cloud Architect certification is designed for IT professionals who design, develop, and manage secure, scalable, and highly available solutions in the Google Cloud Platform. It demonstrates your ability to design and plan cloud solutions, manage and provision the cloud infrastructure, and optimize business processes. This certification is valuable because it allows you to specialize in cloud computing and demonstrate your skills to potential employers.

How to Get Certified

If you're interested in getting certified in a specific skillset, there are a few things you need to do. First, research the certifications that are available in your field and determine which ones are the most valuable. Next, study for the exams by using study materials provided by the certification provider or attending certification courses. Finally, take the exam and become certified. It's important to note that some certifications require you to renew them periodically to ensure that you stay up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements.

CompTIA A+

The CompTIA A+ certification is designed for IT professionals who support and maintain PCs, mobile devices, and laptops. It demonstrates your ability to install, configure, and troubleshoot computer systems and networks. Personally, I found this certification to be valuable because it allowed me to specialize in computer systems and demonstrate my expertise to potential employers.
