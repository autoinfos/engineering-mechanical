How Much Do Automotive Service Technicians Make

Written by Robby Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career as an automotive service technician? One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a career is the salary. In this article, we will discuss how much do automotive service technicians make and what factors can affect their salary.

Many people believe that automotive service technicians do not make a lot of money. However, this is not true. While the salary of automotive service technicians can vary depending on their location, experience, and the type of employer, they can earn a good living wage.

The average salary for an automotive service technician in the United States is around $42,000 per year. However, experienced technicians with specialized skills can earn up to $70,000 per year or more. Technicians who work for dealerships and other large automotive companies usually earn more than those who work for smaller independent repair shops.

In conclusion, automotive service technicians can make a good living wage. Their salary can vary depending on their location, experience, and the type of employer. Experienced technicians with specialized skills can earn up to $70,000 per year or more.

What Factors Affect the Salary of Automotive Service Technicians?

Several factors can affect the salary of an automotive service technician. Here are some of the most important factors:

Location

The location of the technician's workplace can have a significant impact on their salary. Technicians who work in large cities or high-income areas usually earn more than those who work in rural areas or low-income areas.

Experience

The more experience a technician has, the higher their salary will be. Experienced technicians have developed specialized skills and knowledge that make them more valuable to employers.

My Personal Experience

As an automotive service technician with over ten years of experience, I can attest that experience is the most important factor that affects salary. When I started working as a technician, I earned around $30,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and developed specialized skills, my salary increased to over $60,000 per year.

Specialized Skills

Automotive service technicians who have specialized skills can earn more than those who do not. For example, technicians who are certified in hybrid and electric vehicle repair can earn significantly higher salaries than those who are not certified. Technicians who have experience working on luxury and high-performance vehicles can also earn higher salaries.

How to Develop Specialized Skills

To develop specialized skills, automotive service technicians can attend training courses and seminars. They can also obtain certifications from organizations such as the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE). Specialized skills can make technicians more valuable to employers and increase their earning potential.

Job Outlook

The job outlook for automotive service technicians is positive. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of automotive service technicians is projected to grow 6 percent from 2020 to 2030, faster than the average for all occupations. As the number of vehicles on the road continues to increase, demand for automotive service technicians will also increase.

Question and Answer

Q: Can automotive service technicians earn overtime pay?

A: Yes, automotive service technicians are eligible for overtime pay if they work more than 40 hours per week.

Q: Do automotive service technicians receive benefits?

A: Many automotive service technicians receive benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off.

Q: Is it necessary to have a college degree to become an automotive service technician?

A: No, it is not necessary to have a college degree to become an automotive service technician. However, formal training at a technical school or community college can be beneficial.

Q: Can automotive service technicians work part-time?

A: Yes, some automotive service technicians work part-time. However, full-time employment is more common.

Conclusion of How Much Do Automotive Service Technicians Make

Automotive service technicians can make a good living wage. The salary of an automotive service technician can vary depending on their location, experience, and the type of employer. However, experienced technicians with specialized skills can earn up to $70,000 per year or more. As the job outlook for automotive service technicians is positive, it can be a rewarding and lucrative career choice.
