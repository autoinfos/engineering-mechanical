The Different Types of Microsoft Certifications

Microsoft offers a wide range of certifications for professionals at all levels. Some of the most popular certifications include:

Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate

This certification is ideal for professionals who work with Microsoft Azure. It demonstrates your expertise in managing and monitoring Azure resources, as well as your ability to implement and manage security controls.

How to Obtain the Certification?

To obtain the Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate certification, you must pass the AZ-104 exam.

Microsoft Certified: Azure Developer Associate

This certification is ideal for professionals who develop and deploy Azure solutions. It demonstrates your expertise in building and deploying Azure solutions, as well as your ability to implement Azure security.

How to Obtain the Certification?

To obtain the Microsoft Certified: Azure Developer Associate certification, you must pass the AZ-204 exam.

Microsoft Certified: Azure Solutions Architect Expert

This certification is ideal for professionals who design and implement solutions on Microsoft Azure. It demonstrates your expertise in designing and implementing solutions that run on Azure, as well as your ability to manage Azure resources.

How to Obtain the Certification?

To obtain the Microsoft Certified: Azure Solutions Architect Expert certification, you must pass both the AZ-303 and AZ-304 exams.
