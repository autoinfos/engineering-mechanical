Vehicle technician jobs in Leeds involve diagnosing and repairing issues with cars, performing routine maintenance, and ensuring that vehicles are safe and roadworthy. This can include tasks such as changing oil and filters, replacing brakes and tires, and repairing engines and transmissions.
