Starting Salary For Diploma In Electrical Engineering In Kenya

Written by William Oct 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a diploma in electrical engineering in Kenya but unsure about the starting salary? This article will guide you through the average starting salary for diploma holders in electrical engineering in Kenya and provide insights into the industry.

As a fresh graduate, it can be challenging to determine what to expect in terms of salary. Electrical engineering graduates in Kenya face the same predicament. The pay scale is an essential factor to consider before choosing a career path.

The starting salary for diploma holders in electrical engineering in Kenya ranges between Kshs. 30,000 to Kshs. 50,000. This amount may differ based on the individual's level of experience, the employer, and the location of the company. The salary may be significantly higher in urban areas compared to rural areas.

In conclusion, the salary scale for diploma holders in electrical engineering in Kenya is lucrative, and graduates can expect to earn a decent living. However, it is essential to note that the salary may vary depending on various factors such as experience and location.

What is the Starting Salary for Diploma in Electrical Engineering in Kenya?

After completing my diploma in electrical engineering, I landed my first job in a small electrical engineering firm in Nairobi. My starting salary was Kshs. 35,000, which was a decent amount for a beginner. However, I realized that the salary scale varied based on the employer and location.

The Role of Experience in Starting Salary for Diploma in Electrical Engineering in Kenya

Location is another significant factor that influences the starting salary for diploma holders in electrical engineering in Kenya.
