Becoming a Certified Automotive Technician in Georgia

If you're interested in becoming a certified automotive technician in Georgia, it's important to understand the requirements for certification. The National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) offers certification for technicians in a variety of areas, including engine repair, brakes, and electrical systems.

To become certified, technicians must pass an ASE certification exam and have a minimum of two years of on-the-job training or one year of on-the-job training and a two-year degree in automotive repair. Many automotive technician schools in Georgia offer programs that can help students prepare for these exams and meet the requirements for certification.
