How Much Does A Mechanic Earn A Year In Australia

Written by Robby Oct 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a mechanic in Australia? If so, one of the most pressing questions on your mind is likely to be how much you can expect to earn. While there are many factors that can influence a mechanic's salary, such as location, experience, and qualifications, we'll explore the average earnings for mechanics in Australia.

As a mechanic, you'll be responsible for diagnosing and repairing vehicles, performing maintenance tasks, and ensuring that vehicles comply with safety standards. While this can be a rewarding and fulfilling career, it's also one that requires hard work and dedication.

The average salary for a mechanic in Australia is around $60,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on the type of mechanic you are, the location you work in, and your level of experience. For example, a diesel mechanic can earn up to $80,000 per year, while an automotive technician can earn around $50,000 per year.

In summary, if you're looking to become a mechanic in Australia, you can expect to earn an average salary of $60,000 per year, with the potential to earn more as you gain experience and specialize in a particular area.

What Do Mechanics Do?

As mentioned earlier, mechanics are responsible for diagnosing and repairing vehicles, performing maintenance tasks, and ensuring that vehicles comply with safety standards. This can include tasks such as:

  • Diagnosing and repairing mechanical and electrical faults in vehicles
  • Performing scheduled maintenance tasks, such as oil changes and brake replacements
  • Testing vehicles to ensure they comply with safety standards
  • Using diagnostic equipment to identify faults in vehicles
  • Providing advice to customers on how to maintain their vehicles

As a mechanic, you'll need to have a high level of technical knowledge and be able to work with a range of tools and equipment. You'll also need to have good communication skills, as you'll often need to explain complex technical information to customers.

How to Become a Mechanic in Australia

If you're interested in becoming a mechanic in Australia, there are a few steps you'll need to take. Firstly, you'll need to complete a qualification in automotive engineering or a related field. This can be done through a TAFE or vocational training program.

Once you've completed your qualification, you'll need to gain some work experience. This can be done through an apprenticeship or by working as a trainee mechanic. As you gain experience, you'll be able to specialize in a particular area, such as diesel mechanics or automotive electronics.

Finally, you'll need to obtain a license to work as a mechanic in Australia. This can be done by completing a Certificate III in Light Vehicle Mechanical Technology or a Certificate III in Heavy Commercial Vehicle Mechanical Technology.

What Are the Job Prospects for Mechanics in Australia?

The job prospects for mechanics in Australia are generally good. According to the Australian Government's Job Outlook website, there is strong demand for qualified mechanics, with around 25,000 job openings expected over the next five years.

However, competition for jobs can be high, especially in urban areas. To increase your chances of finding work, it's a good idea to specialize in a particular area, such as diesel mechanics or automotive electronics.

Conclusion of How Much Does a Mechanic Earn a Year in Australia

In conclusion, becoming a mechanic in Australia can be a rewarding and fulfilling career. While the average salary for a mechanic is around $60,000 per year, this can vary depending on your location, experience, and qualifications. If you're interested in pursuing a career as a mechanic, it's important to obtain the necessary qualifications and gain some work experience. With the right skills and knowledge, you'll be well placed to take advantage of the strong demand for qualified mechanics in Australia.
