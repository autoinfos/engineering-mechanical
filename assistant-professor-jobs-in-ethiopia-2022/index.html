Job Requirements for Assistant Professor Positions in Ethiopia

As an academician seeking an assistant professor job in Ethiopia, you must meet certain requirements to be considered for the position. The requirements include:

Education and Experience

You must have a Ph.D. in your field of study from a recognized university. Additionally, you must have at least three years of teaching experience at the university level. The experience should be in a relevant field of study.

How to Apply for Assistant Professor Jobs in Ethiopia

To apply for assistant professor jobs in Ethiopia, you need to follow the following steps:

  1. Check for job vacancies in your field of study in different universities and colleges in Ethiopia.
  2. Prepare your curriculum vitae (CV) and application letter. Your CV should highlight your education, experience, research, and publications.
  3. Submit your application online or through the address provided in the job advertisement.
  4. Attend the interview if shortlisted.
  5. Wait for feedback from the employer.

Salary and Benefits for Assistant Professor Positions in Ethiopia

The salary and benefits for assistant professor positions in Ethiopia vary depending on the employer and field of study. However, most universities and colleges offer competitive salaries and benefits to their employees. The benefits include health insurance, retirement benefits, housing allowance, research grants, and paid leave days.
