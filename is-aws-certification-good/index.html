Is Aws Certification Good Exploring The Benefits And Advantages

Written by Bardi Oct 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering pursuing an AWS certification but wondering if it's worth the investment? With the increasing demand for cloud computing skills, AWS certification has become a hot topic in the tech industry. In this blog post, we'll explore the benefits and advantages of getting AWS certified, so you can make an informed decision.

The Pain Points of AWS Certification

One of the main concerns for individuals considering AWS certification is the time and cost involved. The certification process can be a significant investment, with fees ranging from $100 to $300 per exam, and preparation time averaging around three to six months. Additionally, some people may worry that the certification may not lead to an immediate career boost or pay increase.

The Target of AWS Certification

Despite the potential challenges, AWS certification remains an incredibly valuable asset in the tech industry. AWS currently dominates the cloud computing market, with a 32% share, and is projected to continue growing in the coming years. This means that AWS certification holders are in high demand and can command higher salaries and better job opportunities.

Benefits of AWS Certification

So, what are the specific benefits of getting AWS certified? First and foremost, AWS certification demonstrates your expertise and competency in cloud computing. It shows that you have a deep understanding of AWS services, architecture, and best practices. This can help you stand out in a competitive job market and increase your chances of landing your dream job.

Additionally, AWS certification can lead to higher salaries. According to a survey by Global Knowledge, AWS-certified professionals earn an average salary of $129,868, significantly higher than the average salary of non-certified professionals in the same field.

Furthermore, AWS certification can provide you with access to exclusive resources, such as the AWS Certified community, events, and training opportunities. These resources can help you stay up-to-date on the latest trends and developments in the industry and further enhance your skills and knowledge.

Personal Experience with AWS Certification

As someone who has pursued AWS certification, I can attest to the many benefits it has brought me. Not only did it help me develop a deeper understanding of AWS services, but it also opened up new job opportunities and led to a significant pay increase. Additionally, being part of the AWS Certified community has allowed me to connect with other professionals in the industry and learn from their experiences.

Advantages of AWS Certification

Another advantage of AWS certification is that it is recognized globally. AWS has a presence in over 190 countries, and AWS certification is recognized and valued by employers worldwide. This means that obtaining an AWS certification can significantly enhance your career prospects, whether you're looking for a job in your current location or planning to move abroad.

How to Get AWS Certified

The AWS certification process consists of multiple levels and tracks, depending on your experience and expertise. The foundational level is the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, followed by associate-level certifications in various areas, such as Solutions Architect, Developer, and SysOps Administrator. There are also professional and specialty-level certifications for more advanced professionals.

To get AWS certified, you need to pass a certification exam, which consists of multiple-choice questions and simulations. You can prepare for the exam by taking online training courses, attending AWS events, and using study materials provided by AWS.

Tips for Preparing for the AWS Certification Exam

Preparing for the AWS certification exam can be a daunting task, but there are some tips that can help you succeed. First, make sure you have a solid understanding of AWS services and architecture. This can be achieved by taking online courses, reading AWS documentation, and practicing with AWS services.

Additionally, it's essential to take practice exams to get a feel for the types of questions you'll encounter on the actual exam. This can help you identify areas where you need to improve and develop a study plan.

FAQs about AWS Certification

Here are some frequently asked questions about AWS certification:

1. Is AWS certification worth it?

Yes, AWS certification is worth it. It demonstrates your expertise and competency in cloud computing, which is in high demand in the tech industry. Additionally, AWS certification can lead to higher salaries and better job opportunities.

2. How long does it take to get AWS certified?

The time it takes to get AWS certified depends on your experience and the level of certification you're pursuing. On average, it takes three to six months to prepare and pass an AWS certification exam.

3. What is the cost of AWS certification?

The cost of AWS certification varies depending on the level of certification. The fees range from $100 to $300 per exam.

4. How long is AWS certification valid?

AWS certification is valid for three years. After three years, you'll need to renew your certification by passing a recertification exam or earning a higher-level certification.

Conclusion of Is AWS Certification Good

In conclusion, AWS certification is an excellent investment for anyone looking to advance their career in cloud computing. It demonstrates your expertise and competency in AWS services, which is in high demand in the tech industry. Additionally, AWS certification can lead to higher salaries, better job opportunities, and access to exclusive resources. While the certification process can be challenging, the benefits are well worth the investment.
