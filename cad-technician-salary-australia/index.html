It can be challenging to find accurate information about the salary of a CAD technician in Australia. Many factors can influence the salary, such as location, years of experience, and industry. The average salary for a CAD technician in Australia is around AU$57,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on factors such as location, years of experience, and industry. Similarly, a CAD technician with several years of experience may earn a higher salary than a graduate starting in the industry.

In conclusion, the salary of a CAD technician in Australia can vary depending on several factors. However, on average, you can expect to earn around AU$57,000 per year. Factors Influencing CAD Technician Salary Australia

Location is a significant factor that can influence your salary as a CAD technician in Australia. For instance, a CAD technician working in Sydney or Melbourne may earn a higher salary than one working in a regional area. This is because the cost of living in Sydney and Melbourne is generally higher than in regional areas.

Years of experience is another factor that can influence your salary as a CAD technician. If you are just starting in the industry, you may earn a lower salary than someone with several years of experience. As you gain more experience and develop your skills, your salary is likely to increase.

Industry

The industry you work in can also influence your salary as a CAD technician. For instance, a CAD technician working in the construction industry may earn a different salary than one working in the manufacturing industry. This is because each industry has different requirements and demands, which can affect the salary of its workers.
