The Truth About Mechanical Draughtsman Salary In Dubai

Written by William Jun 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a mechanical draughtsman in Dubai and wondering if you are earning what you are worth? Are you curious about the average salary for your profession in this city? If so, keep reading to learn more about mechanical draughtsman salary in Dubai.

As a mechanical draughtsman in Dubai, you may be facing challenges related to salary. You may feel that you are not being compensated fairly for your skills and experience, or you may be unsure of what the appropriate salary range is for your position. These challenges are not unique to Dubai, but they can be particularly difficult to navigate in this city due to the high cost of living and the competitive job market.

The average salary for a mechanical draughtsman in Dubai is around AED 61,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on a number of factors, such as your level of experience, the industry you work in, and the company you work for. It is important to do your research and negotiate your salary based on your qualifications and the job market.

In summary, if you are a mechanical draughtsman in Dubai, it is important to understand the average salary range for your position and negotiate based on your qualifications and the job market. By doing so, you can ensure that you are being compensated fairly for your skills and experience.
