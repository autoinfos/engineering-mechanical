Is Pharmacy Tech Certification Worth It Exploring The Pros And Cons

Written by Christine Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Working as a pharmacy technician can be a rewarding career choice for those who enjoy helping others and have a strong attention to detail. However, many aspiring pharmacy techs wonder if obtaining certification is worth the time and money investment. In this blog post, we'll explore the pros and cons of pharmacy tech certification and help you determine if it's the right choice for you.

The Pain Points of Not Having Pharmacy Tech Certification

While it's possible to work as a pharmacy technician without certification, there are several reasons why it may be worth pursuing. For one, many employers prefer to hire certified technicians as they have demonstrated a certain level of knowledge and competency in the field. Additionally, some states require certification in order to work as a pharmacy tech, so not having it could limit your job opportunities.

Answering the Question: Is Pharmacy Tech Certification Worth It?

Ultimately, whether or not pharmacy tech certification is worth it depends on your personal and professional goals. If you're looking to advance your career, increase your earning potential, and stand out to potential employers, then certification is likely worth pursuing. Additionally, if you live in a state that requires certification, then it's a no-brainer.

On the other hand, if you're content with your current job and don't see yourself pursuing higher positions within the pharmacy field, then certification may not be worth the investment.

Pros and Cons of Pharmacy Tech Certification

Here's a quick rundown of some of the pros and cons of pursuing pharmacy tech certification:

Pros:

- Increased job opportunities

- Higher earning potential

- Demonstrated competency in the field

- Potential for career advancement

Cons:

- Time and money investment

- Continuing education requirements to maintain certification

- Not necessary for all pharmacy tech positions

Personal Experience with Pharmacy Tech Certification

As someone who has worked as a pharmacy technician for several years, I can attest to the benefits of obtaining certification. Not only did it open up more job opportunities for me, but it also gave me a sense of pride and accomplishment in my work. Additionally, I found that my knowledge and skills improved as a result of studying for the certification exam.

How to Obtain Pharmacy Tech Certification

If you've decided that pharmacy tech certification is worth pursuing, then the next step is to research the requirements and process for obtaining it. Generally, you'll need to complete an accredited pharmacy technician training program and pass a certification exam. Some programs also require completion of a certain number of hours of work experience.

Continuing Education Requirements

Once you've obtained certification, it's important to note that you'll need to complete continuing education requirements in order to maintain it. This typically involves completing a certain number of CE hours every two years.

Job Opportunities with Pharmacy Tech Certification

Having pharmacy tech certification can open up a wide range of job opportunities within the field. Some potential positions include:

- Retail pharmacy technician

- Hospital pharmacy technician

- Compounding pharmacy technician

- Pharmacy automation technician

- Pharmacy informatics specialist

Question and Answer Section

Q: Is pharmacy tech certification required in all states?

A: No, requirements vary by state. Some require certification, while others do not.

Q: How much does pharmacy tech certification cost?

A: The cost varies by program and location, but you can generally expect to pay a few hundred dollars for the exam and any required coursework.

Q: Can I work as a pharmacy technician without certification?

A: Yes, it's possible to work without certification, but it may limit your job opportunities and earning potential.

Q: How long does it take to obtain pharmacy tech certification?

A: The timeline varies depending on the program you choose and your personal schedule. Some programs can be completed in a matter of months, while others may take a year or more.

Conclusion of Is Pharmacy Tech Certification Worth It

In conclusion, pharmacy tech certification can be a valuable investment for those looking to advance their careers and increase their earning potential. However, it's important to weigh the pros and cons and consider your personal goals before making the commitment. With the right mindset and dedication, obtaining certification can be a rewarding and fulfilling experience.
