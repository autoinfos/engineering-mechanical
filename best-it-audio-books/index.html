Discover The Best It Audio Books In Relaxed English Language

Written by Bardi Jul 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a busy IT professional who wants to stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in the industry, but struggling to find time to read books? If so, you're not alone. Many IT experts face a similar challenge, which is why audiobooks have become increasingly popular. With audiobooks, you can listen to the latest IT titles on the go, during your commute or while doing other tasks.

However, not all audiobooks are created equal. Some are poorly narrated, while others may not cover the topics you're interested in. That's why it's essential to choose the best IT audio books that meet your needs and preferences.

If you're like most IT professionals, you're looking for audio books that provide practical tips, insights, and strategies that you can apply to your work immediately. You want audiobooks that are easy to understand, engaging, and informative, without being too technical or dry.

In this post, we'll introduce you to some of the best IT audio books that meet these criteria and more. We'll cover a range of topics, from cybersecurity and cloud computing to agile development and data analysis. By the end of this post, you'll have a better understanding of the best IT audio books to add to your listening list.

Best IT Audio Books for Cybersecurity Enthusiasts

If you're interested in cybersecurity and want to learn more about this critical field, check out the following audiobooks:

1. "The Art of Invisibility" by Kevin Mitnick

In this audiobook, famed hacker Kevin Mitnick shares his insights on how to protect your digital privacy and security. He covers a range of topics, from social engineering and phishing to encryption and VPNs.

2. "Countdown to Zero Day" by Kim Zetter

Journalist Kim Zetter takes listeners behind the scenes of the Stuxnet virus, one of the most significant cyberattacks in history. She explains how the virus was created, how it was discovered, and its implications for the future of cybersecurity.

The Best IT Audio Books for Agile Development

If you're interested in agile development, check out these audiobooks:

1. "Scrum: The Art of Doing Twice the Work in Half the Time" by Jeff Sutherland

Scrum is a popular agile methodology used by many IT teams. In this audiobook, Jeff Sutherland, the co-creator of Scrum, explains how this approach can help teams work more efficiently and productively.

2. "The Lean Startup" by Eric Ries

The Lean Startup is a classic book that explains how to create a successful startup using agile principles. In this audiobook, Eric Ries shares his insights on how to test your ideas, iterate quickly, and create a sustainable business.

The Best IT Audio Books for Cloud Computing

If you're interested in cloud computing, check out these audiobooks:

1. "The Big Switch" by Nicholas Carr

In this audiobook, Nicholas Carr explores the impact of cloud computing on the IT industry and society as a whole. He discusses the benefits and risks of cloud computing and how it's changing the way we work and live.

2. "Cloud Computing: Concepts, Technology & Architecture" by Thomas Erl

This audiobook provides a comprehensive overview of cloud computing, from the basics to the latest trends and technologies. It covers topics such as cloud architecture, security, and governance.

How to Choose the Best IT Audio Books for You

Now that you know some of the best IT audio books available, how do you choose which ones to listen to? Here are some tips:

1. Start with your interests: Choose audiobooks that cover topics you're passionate about or want to learn more about.

2. Read reviews: Check out online reviews and ratings of audiobooks to see what other listeners are saying.

3. Listen to samples: Many audiobook services offer free samples of their books. Listen to a sample to get a feel for the narrator's voice and style.

4. Consider the narrator: The narrator can make or break an audiobook. Choose a narrator whose voice and style resonate with you.

Question and Answer

Q: Are audiobooks as effective as reading books?

A: Yes, studies have shown that audiobooks are just as effective as reading books in terms of comprehension and retention.

Q: Can I listen to audiobooks on my phone?

A: Yes, most audiobook services offer apps that allow you to listen to audiobooks on your phone or tablet.

Q: How long are audiobooks?

A: The length of audiobooks varies, but most range from 6 to 12 hours.

Q: How much do audiobooks cost?

A: The cost of audiobooks varies depending on the service and the book. Some services offer monthly subscriptions that give you access to a certain number of audiobooks per month, while others charge per book.

Conclusion of Best IT Audio Books

Choosing the best IT audio books can be challenging, but with our recommendations, you can find audiobooks that meet your needs and preferences. Whether you're interested in cybersecurity, cloud computing, agile development, or other IT topics, there's an audiobook out there for you. So, start listening and enhance your knowledge and skills today!
