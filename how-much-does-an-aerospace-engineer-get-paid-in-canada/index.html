How Much Does An Aerospace Engineer Get Paid In Canada

Written by Robby Nov 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as an aerospace engineer in Canada but wondering about the salary prospects? If yes, then you have stumbled upon the right blog post. In this article, we will discuss the salary range for aerospace engineers in Canada and factors that can affect their earning potential.

As an aspiring aerospace engineer, you may have several questions regarding the salary prospects in Canada. You might be concerned if the salary is enough to meet your living expenses, pay off student loans, or save for retirement. Additionally, you might be wondering if your experience, education, or location can affect your earning potential.

The average salary for an aerospace engineer in Canada is around CAD 80,000 to CAD 120,000 per year. However, the salary range can vary based on several factors such as education, experience, job location, and industry. Aerospace engineers with a Master's or Ph.D. degree can earn higher salaries than those with a Bachelor's degree. Similarly, engineers with several years of experience can earn more than entry-level engineers.

In conclusion, aerospace engineering is a lucrative career option in Canada, with a salary range of CAD 80,000 to CAD 120,000 per year. However, several factors can impact your earning potential, such as education, experience, job location, and industry.

Factors That Affect the Salary of an Aerospace Engineer in Canada

Location: The salary of an aerospace engineer can vary depending on the location of the job. For instance, aerospace engineers working in Toronto, Vancouver, or Montreal can earn higher salaries than those working in smaller towns or cities.

Education: Aerospace engineers with a Master's or Ph.D. degree can earn higher salaries than those with a Bachelor's degree. Pursuing higher education can boost your earning potential in this field.

Experience: The salary of an aerospace engineer can increase with experience. Entry-level engineers can expect a lower starting salary than those with several years of experience in the field.

Industry: Aerospace engineers can work in different industries, such as military, aviation, or space exploration. The salary range can vary depending on the industry and job role.

Job Prospects for Aerospace Engineers in Canada

Aerospace engineering is a growing field in Canada, with several job opportunities available for professionals. The Canadian aerospace industry is the fifth-largest in the world and employs around 213,000 people. According to the Government of Canada's Job Bank, there is a high demand for aerospace engineers in Ontario, Quebec, and British Columbia. The job outlook for aerospace engineers in Canada is positive, with a stable growth rate projected for the next few years.

How to Become an Aerospace Engineer in Canada

To become an aerospace engineer in Canada, you need to have a Bachelor's degree in Aerospace, Mechanical, or Electrical Engineering. Pursuing a Master's or Ph.D. degree can enhance your job prospects and earning potential in this field. You also need to obtain a Professional Engineering (P.Eng.) license from a provincial or territorial engineering association.

Personal Experience of an Aerospace Engineer in Canada

"I have been working as an aerospace engineer in Canada for the past five years. Initially, I started as an entry-level engineer with a Bachelor's degree and earned around CAD 60,000 per year. However, with experience and a Master's degree, I was able to negotiate a higher salary and currently earn around CAD 100,000 per year. I work for a private aviation company in Toronto and enjoy the work-life balance and job security in this field."

Conclusion of How Much Does an Aerospace Engineer Get Paid in Canada

In conclusion, aerospace engineering is a growing field in Canada, with a positive job outlook and a lucrative salary range of CAD 80,000 to CAD 120,000 per year. Pursuing higher education, obtaining a Professional Engineering (P.Eng.) license, and gaining experience can increase your earning potential in this field. Additionally, the location and industry of the job can impact your salary range. Overall, if you are passionate about aerospace engineering, then this field can offer a fulfilling career with excellent salary prospects.
