Question and Answer

Q: How can I highlight my technical skills on my resume?

A: You can highlight your technical skills by creating a separate skills section on your resume. This section should include specific technical skills and software programs that you are proficient in.

Q: Should I include my GPA on my resume?

A: It's not necessary to include your GPA on your resume, but if it's above a 3.0, it can be beneficial to include it. Additionally, if you're a recent graduate, including your GPA can show potential employers your academic achievements.

Q: Can I include non-engineering related work experience on my resume?

A: Yes, you can include non-engineering related work experience on your resume. However, make sure to highlight how the skills you gained in that experience can be applied to the engineering field.

Q: How long should my resume be?

A: Your resume should be one to two pages in length. It's important to keep it concise and only include relevant information.
