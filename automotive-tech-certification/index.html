Types of Automotive Tech Certification

There are several types of automotive tech certification available, including ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certification, manufacturer-specific certification, and dealership certification. ASE certification is the most widely recognized certification, and it covers all aspects of automotive repair and maintenance. Manufacturer-specific certification is offered by car manufacturers and covers the specific systems and components of their vehicles. Dealership certification is offered by car dealerships and covers the specific systems and components of the vehicles they sell.

How to Earn Automotive Tech Certification

To earn automotive tech certification, you must complete a certification program and pass an exam. Certification programs are offered by community colleges, vocational schools, and industry organizations. The length of the program varies depending on the type of certification, but most programs take between six months and two years to complete. Once you have completed the program, you must pass an exam to earn your certification.
