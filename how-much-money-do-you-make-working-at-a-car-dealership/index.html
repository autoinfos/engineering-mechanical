How Much Money Do You Make Working At A Car Dealership

Written by Petter Oct 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Have you ever wondered how much money you can make working at a car dealership? If you're considering a career in the automotive industry or currently work in one, this is a question that you've likely asked yourself. In this post, we'll dive into the topic and provide you with some insights on how much you can earn working at a car dealership.

Working at a car dealership can be a rewarding career choice, but it can also come with its own set of challenges. One of the biggest pain points for many people is the uncertainty of how much money they can make. Many factors can affect your earnings, such as your role, experience, and location, among others. This can make it difficult to know what to expect when it comes to your salary.

The answer to how much money do you make working at a car dealership can vary greatly. According to Glassdoor, the average salary for a salesperson at a car dealership is around $50,000 per year, with the potential to earn more through commissions. However, this can vary depending on the dealership's location and the salesperson's experience and performance. Other roles, such as service technicians and finance managers, can also earn a good salary, with technicians earning an average of $52,000 per year and finance managers earning an average of $97,000 per year.

In summary, the amount of money you can make working at a car dealership will depend on various factors, including your role, experience, and location. While some roles may have a higher earning potential than others, the automotive industry can offer a rewarding career with good earning potential.

What's the Earning Potential for Salespeople?

As a former salesperson at a car dealership, I can speak from personal experience about the earning potential. While the base salary may not be very high, the commissions can be substantial, especially if you're a top performer. I've seen colleagues make six figures in a year through commissions alone. However, this requires a lot of hard work and dedication to the job. You need to be willing to put in the time and effort to build relationships with customers and close deals.

What About Service Technicians?

Service technicians can also earn a good salary in the automotive industry. As cars become increasingly complex, skilled technicians are in high demand. This means that experienced technicians with the right certifications and training can earn a good salary. However, this is a physically demanding job that requires a lot of technical knowledge and expertise.

How Can You Maximize Your Earnings?

If you want to maximize your earnings working at a car dealership, there are a few things you can do. First, focus on building strong relationships with your customers. This can help you build a loyal customer base that will come back to you for their future automotive needs. Second, continually improve your skills and knowledge. This can help you advance in your career and earn a higher salary. Finally, look for opportunities to earn commissions or bonuses, such as selling add-ons or hitting sales targets.

How to Negotiate Your Salary

If you're starting a new job at a car dealership, it's important to negotiate your salary. This can help you earn a higher salary and set yourself up for success in the long term. When negotiating your salary, do your research and come prepared with data on industry salaries and your own experience and skills. Be confident and assertive in your negotiations, but also be willing to listen to your employer's perspective.

My Experience Working at a Car Dealership

As someone who has worked in the automotive industry for several years, I can attest to the fact that it can be a challenging but rewarding career choice. I started as a salesperson and worked my way up to a management role. While the job can be demanding at times, the satisfaction of helping customers find their dream car and the potential to earn a good salary made it all worth it for me.

Question and Answer

Q: How much can you earn as a finance manager at a car dealership?

A: According to Glassdoor, the average salary for a finance manager in the automotive industry is around $97,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on the dealership's location and the manager's experience and performance.

Q: What skills do you need to work at a car dealership?

A: Some important skills for working in the automotive industry include communication skills, sales skills, technical knowledge, and the ability to build relationships with customers.

Q: How can you advance in your career at a car dealership?

A: To advance in your career at a car dealership, focus on building your skills and knowledge, taking on additional responsibilities, and demonstrating a strong work ethic and dedication to your job.

Q: Is it possible to earn a good salary working at a car dealership?

A: Yes, it's possible to earn a good salary working at a car dealership. While some roles may have a higher earning potential than others, there are opportunities to earn a good salary in the automotive industry.

Conclusion of How Much Money Do You Make Working at a Car Dealership?

Working at a car dealership can be a rewarding career choice with good earning potential. While the amount of money you can make will depend on various factors, such as your role, experience, and location, there are opportunities to earn a good salary in the automotive industry. By building strong relationships with your customers, continually improving your skills and knowledge, and looking for opportunities to earn commissions or bonuses, you can maximize your earnings and set yourself up for long-term success.
