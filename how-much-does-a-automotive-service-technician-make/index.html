How Much Does A Automotive Service Technician Make

Written by Frank Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you love cars and enjoy fixing them? Becoming an automotive service technician can be a great career choice for you. However, before you pursue this path, you might be wondering how much does a automotive service technician make. In this blog post, we will discuss the salary of an automotive service technician and related keywords.

While fixing cars can be a fulfilling job, it comes with its own set of challenges. One of the most common problems that automotive service technicians face is a low salary. Many people assume that the pay is not enough to support their lifestyle, which can be a major deterrent for those considering a career in this field.

The salary of an automotive service technician varies depending on several factors, including experience, location, and the employer. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for automotive service technicians and mechanics was $42,090 as of May 2020. However, the top 10 percent of earners made more than $68,880, while the bottom 10 percent made less than $24,090.

In conclusion, automotive service technicians can make a decent living, but the salary varies depending on several factors. If you're passionate about cars and enjoy fixing them, this can be a great career choice for you.

What is the Average Salary of an Automotive Service Technician?

When I started my career as an automotive service technician, I was curious about the salary of this profession. After researching, I found out that the average annual salary of an automotive service technician is $42,090. However, this number can vary depending on where you work and how much experience you have.

How Can You Increase Your Salary as an Automotive Service Technician?

One way to increase your salary as an automotive service technician is to gain more experience. As you become more experienced, you can take on more complex projects and be more efficient, which can lead to higher pay. Another way to increase your salary is to specialize in a particular area, such as diesel engines or high-performance cars.

Factors Affecting the Salary of an Automotive Service Technician

As mentioned earlier, the salary of an automotive service technician depends on several factors. Some of the most important factors include:

  • Experience
  • Location
  • Employer
  • Specialization

How to Negotiate Your Salary as an Automotive Service Technician

If you feel that your salary as an automotive service technician is not enough, you can negotiate with your employer. Before you do this, research the market rate for your position and experience level. Then, schedule a meeting with your supervisor and present your case for a salary increase.

My Personal Experience as an Automotive Service Technician

As an automotive service technician, I can say that this is a fulfilling career choice for those who love cars. However, the salary can be a bit low, especially for those who are just starting out. I recommend gaining as much experience as possible and specializing in a particular area to increase your pay.
