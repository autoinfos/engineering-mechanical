How Much Do New Grad Software Engineers Make

Written by Luffy Nov 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever wondered how much new grad software engineers make? It's a question that many people have, especially those who are interested in pursuing a career in software engineering. Software engineering is a field that is constantly growing and changing, and it's important to understand what kind of salary you can expect as a new grad.

When it comes to how much new grad software engineers make, there are several factors to consider. The first is the location where you'll be working. Salaries can vary widely depending on where you live and work. The second factor is the company you'll be working for. Some companies offer higher salaries than others, depending on the industry and the size of the company.

According to Glassdoor, the average base pay for a new grad software engineer is $83,000 per year. However, this can vary widely depending on the factors mentioned above. For example, a new grad software engineer working for a large tech company in Silicon Valley could make upwards of $100,000 per year, while a new grad software engineer working for a smaller company in a less tech-focused city could make closer to $60,000 per year.

In summary, when it comes to how much new grad software engineers make, there is no one-size-fits-all answer. Salaries can vary widely depending on location, company, and other factors. However, on average, new grad software engineers can expect to make around $83,000 per year.

My Experience with New Grad Software Engineer Salaries

When I graduated from college with a degree in computer science, I was excited to start my career as a software engineer. However, I was also a bit nervous about what kind of salary I could expect. I had heard that software engineering salaries could be quite high, but I wasn't sure if that was true for new grads.

After doing some research and applying to several companies, I ended up accepting a job offer from a large tech company in San Francisco. My starting salary was $90,000 per year, which was higher than I had expected. I was thrilled to be making such a good salary right out of college.

Over the next few years, I saw my salary increase steadily as I gained more experience and took on more responsibilities. I also saw that my colleagues who worked for smaller companies in less tech-focused cities were making less than I was. However, I also knew that living in San Francisco was expensive, and that my high salary was necessary to cover the high cost of living in the city.

Factors That Affect New Grad Software Engineer Salaries

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can affect how much new grad software engineers make. These include:

Location

Salaries can vary widely depending on where you live and work. Typically, salaries are higher in tech-focused cities like San Francisco, New York, and Seattle. However, the cost of living in these cities is also higher, so it's important to factor that into your decision.

Company Size and Industry

Large tech companies like Google, Amazon, and Microsoft typically offer higher salaries than smaller companies. This is often because they have more resources and can afford to pay their employees more. However, smaller companies may offer other benefits like stock options, which can be valuable in the long run.

My Advice for New Grad Software Engineers

If you're a new grad software engineer, my advice is to do your research and apply to several companies to get a sense of what kind of salary you can expect. Don't be afraid to negotiate your salary if you receive a job offer. It's also important to consider other factors like location, company culture, and potential for growth when deciding on a job offer.

A: Location, company size, and industry can all affect how much new grad software engineers make.

A: New grad software engineers should also consider location, company culture, and potential for growth when deciding on a job offer.

Conclusion of How Much Do New Grad Software Engineers Make

In conclusion, new grad software engineer salaries can vary widely depending on location, company size, and industry. However, on average, new grad software engineers can expect to make around $83,000 per year. It's important to do your research and consider all factors when deciding on a job offer as a new grad software engineer.
