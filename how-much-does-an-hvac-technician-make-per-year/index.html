How Much Does An Hvac Technician Make Per Year

Written by Luffy Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering pursuing a career as an HVAC technician? One of the most important factors when choosing a career path is salary. So, how much does an HVAC technician make per year?

Many people are unaware of the earning potential of HVAC technicians and may not consider it as a viable career option. However, the truth is that HVAC technicians can earn a good salary with opportunities for growth.

Answering the Question: How Much Does an HVAC Technician Make Per Year?

The average salary for an HVAC technician in the United States is around $50,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on several factors such as experience, location, and the type of HVAC work being performed.

Entry-level HVAC technicians can expect to make around $30,000 per year, while more experienced technicians can earn upwards of $75,000 per year. HVAC technicians who specialize in installation and repair tend to earn higher salaries than those who focus on maintenance.

Location is also an important factor when it comes to salary. HVAC technicians working in urban areas tend to earn more than those in rural areas due to higher demand for their services.

Earning Potential and Career Growth

As mentioned earlier, HVAC technicians have opportunities for career growth and increased earning potential. Experienced technicians can become supervisors or managers, while others may choose to start their own HVAC businesses.

Specializing in a particular area of HVAC work, such as refrigeration or heating, can also lead to higher salaries and more job opportunities.

Job Outlook for HVAC Technicians

The job outlook for HVAC technicians is positive, with a projected job growth of 4% from 2019 to 2029. This is due to the increasing demand for HVAC services as more buildings are constructed and older buildings require maintenance and repair.

How to Become an HVAC Technician

To become an HVAC technician, you will need to complete a training program or apprenticeship. These programs can range from six months to two years and provide hands-on training in HVAC systems and equipment.

After completing a training program, you will also need to obtain a certification or license to work as an HVAC technician. Requirements vary by state, but typically involve passing an exam and meeting certain education and experience requirements.

Personal Experience: Earning Potential as an HVAC Technician

As someone who has worked in the HVAC industry for over a decade, I can attest to the earning potential of HVAC technicians. When I first started out, I was making around $35,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and specialized in refrigeration, my salary increased to over $70,000 per year.

One of the best things about working as an HVAC technician is the job security and opportunities for growth. As long as buildings require heating, cooling, and ventilation, there will be a need for HVAC technicians.

Conclusion of How Much Does an HVAC Technician Make Per Year

If you are considering a career as an HVAC technician, you can expect to earn a good salary with opportunities for growth.
