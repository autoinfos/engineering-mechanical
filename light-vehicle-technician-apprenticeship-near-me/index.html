What is Light Vehicle Technician Apprenticeship?

Light Vehicle Technician Apprenticeship is a training program designed for individuals interested in becoming a professional technician. It is a combination of classroom instruction and on-the-job training, where apprentices learn from experienced technicians.

The program typically lasts for three years, and upon completion, apprentices receive a certificate of qualification. Additionally, it provides a pathway to becoming a licensed technician.

During the apprenticeship, trainees learn various skills, including how to diagnose and repair vehicle components, how to maintain vehicles, and how to use diagnostic equipment. They also gain valuable experience working with customers and colleagues, which helps them develop soft skills that are essential in the industry.
