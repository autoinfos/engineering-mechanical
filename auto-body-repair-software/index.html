Features of Auto Body Repair Software

The primary features of auto body repair software include:

  • Appointment scheduling
  • Customer management
  • Inventory tracking
  • Invoicing
  • Estimating
  • Reporting and analytics

Appointment scheduling allows you to manage your schedule and book appointments with ease. Customer management enables you to store customer data, including their contact information, vehicle details, and service history. Inventory tracking helps you manage your parts and supplies, so you never run out of stock. Invoicing allows you to generate and send invoices to customers, while estimating helps you provide accurate quotes for repairs. Reporting and analytics provide you with insights into your business performance, so you can make informed decisions.
