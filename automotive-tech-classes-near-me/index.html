What Are the Different Types of Automotive Tech Classes Near Me?

There are several different types of automotive tech classes near me, including certificate programs, associate degree programs, and bachelor's degree programs. Associate degree programs typically take two years to complete and provide a more comprehensive education in automotive technology. Bachelor's degree programs are four-year programs that provide a more in-depth education in automotive technology and may also include courses in business and management.
