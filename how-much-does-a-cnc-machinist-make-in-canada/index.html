How Much Does A Cnc Machinist Make In Canada

Written by Oliver Oct 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you considering a career as a CNC Machinist in Canada? If so, you may be wondering how much you can expect to earn in this field. As with any occupation, CNC Machinists can face a number of challenges when it comes to salary and job satisfaction. In this article, we will explore the topic of how much does a CNC Machinist make in Canada, and provide you with some insights into what you can expect in terms of compensation and career advancement.

CNC Machinists are skilled tradespeople who work with computer-controlled machines to produce a wide range of metal parts and components. They are responsible for programming, setting up, and operating these machines to produce precision parts that meet exact specifications. While this can be a rewarding and challenging career, CNC Machinists may face some challenges when it comes to salary and job satisfaction.

According to the Canadian government's Job Bank, the median hourly wage for CNC Machinists in Canada is $25.00 per hour. This means that half of all CNC Machinists in Canada earn more than this amount, while the other half earn less. The highest-paid CNC Machinists can earn upwards of $35.00 per hour, while the lowest-paid can earn as little as $15.00 per hour.

In terms of career advancement, CNC Machinists may have the opportunity to move into supervisory roles, such as Production or Shop Supervisor, which can earn higher salaries. They may also have the opportunity to specialize in a particular industry, such as aerospace or automotive, which can lead to higher-paying positions.

In summary, while CNC Machinists in Canada may face some challenges in terms of salary and job satisfaction, this can be a rewarding and challenging career for those who are interested in working with computer-controlled machines to produce precision parts. With opportunities for career advancement and specialization, CNC Machinists can earn a competitive salary and build a successful career in this industry.

How Much Does a CNC Machinist Make in Canada: Specialization

Specializing in a particular industry can be a great way to increase your earning potential as a CNC Machinist in Canada. For example, CNC Machinists who work in the aerospace industry may work on complex and high-precision parts, which can result in higher salaries. Similarly, those who work in the automotive industry may work on high-volume production runs, which can lead to higher salaries as well.
