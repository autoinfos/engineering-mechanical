Question and Answer

Q: What is the job outlook for mechanics?

A: According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of automotive service technicians and mechanics is projected to grow 6 percent from 2016 to 2026, about as fast as the average for all occupations.

Q: How long does it take to complete auto tech mechanic school?

A: The length of auto tech mechanic school varies depending on the program. Some programs can be completed in as little as 6 months, while others may take up to 2 years.

Q: What kind of salary can I expect as a mechanic?

A: According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for automotive service technicians and mechanics was $44,050 in May 2019.

Q: What kind of jobs can I get with a degree in auto tech mechanics?

A: With a degree in auto tech mechanics, you can pursue a variety of careers in the automotive industry. Some common job titles include mechanic, automotive technician, and service advisor.
