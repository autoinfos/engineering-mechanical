What Are Maintenance Technician Jobs in Orlando FL?

Maintenance technician jobs in Orlando FL are positions that involve repairing, maintaining, and upgrading buildings, facilities, and equipment. These jobs can be found in a wide range of industries, including healthcare, hospitality, manufacturing, and more.

Some common tasks that maintenance technicians might perform include fixing plumbing or electrical problems, repairing HVAC systems, painting and cleaning, and conducting safety inspections. These jobs require a combination of technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and attention to detail.

Qualifications and Certifications for Maintenance Technician Jobs in Orlando FL

Most maintenance technician jobs in Orlando FL require a high school diploma or equivalent, as well as some level of technical training or experience. Depending on the specific position, you may also need to have certifications in areas such as HVAC, electrical work, or plumbing.
