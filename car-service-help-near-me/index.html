Question and Answer

Q: How often should I have my car serviced?

A: It's recommended to have your car serviced every 6 months or 10,000 miles, whichever comes first.

Q: What should I do if I suspect my mechanic is overcharging me?

A: If you suspect your mechanic is overcharging you, get a second opinion from another mechanic. Additionally, you can ask for a detailed breakdown of services and costs to ensure that you're not being taken advantage of.

Q: What certifications should I look for in a mechanic?

A: Look for certifications such as Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) or National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (NIASE). These certifications indicate that the mechanic has passed rigorous testing and has the knowledge and experience to provide quality service.

Q: What should I do if my car breaks down on the road?

A: If your car breaks down on the road, pull over to a safe location and turn on your hazard lights. Call for roadside assistance or a tow truck and wait for help to arrive.
