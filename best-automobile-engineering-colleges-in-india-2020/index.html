Question and Answer

Q: What is the eligibility criteria for admission to automobile engineering colleges in India?
A: The eligibility criteria vary from college to college. However, most colleges require a minimum of 50% marks in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.

Q: What is the duration of the automobile engineering course?
A: The duration of the automobile engineering course is four years.

Q: What are the job opportunities after completing automobile engineering?
A: After completing automobile engineering, you can work as an automobile engineer, design engineer, research and development engineer, quality control engineer, and many more.

Q: How much can I earn after completing automobile engineering?
A: The salary after completing automobile engineering varies based on the company and job profile. However, the average salary ranges from 4-6 lakhs per annum.
