Do You Use Periods In Bullet Points On A Resume

Written by Oliver Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to creating a resume, there are many questions that come to mind. One common question is whether or not to use periods in bullet points. This seemingly small detail can have a big impact on your overall presentation and can even affect your chances of getting hired.

Employers want to see a clear and concise summary of your experience and qualifications. If your bullet points are inconsistent or difficult to read, it can be a turn-off for potential employers. This is why it's important to pay attention to the details, including whether or not to use periods.

The answer to the question of whether or not to use periods in bullet points on a resume is not a simple one. It ultimately depends on your personal preference and the style guide you are following. However, there are some general guidelines that can help you decide.

In general, it is recommended to use periods at the end of complete sentences in bullet points. This helps to make your resume look more professional and consistent. However, if you are using sentence fragments in your bullet points, it is not necessary to use periods.

When to Use Periods in Bullet Points

How to Use Periods in Bullet Points for Skills
