How Much Money Does A Vet Tech Make An Hour A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Working as a veterinary technician is a rewarding career that involves taking care of animals and ensuring their well-being. However, one of the most common questions asked by aspiring vet techs is, “How much money does a vet tech make an hour?” This question can be a cause of concern, especially for those who are looking to pursue this career path. In this post, we will delve into the topic of how much money does a vet tech make an hour, and provide a comprehensive guide to help you understand the salary structure of this profession.

There are several factors that determine how much money a vet tech can earn per hour. These factors include location, experience, and education level. It is important to note that while the salary of a vet tech may not be as high as other medical professionals, it is a respectable income that can provide a comfortable living.

On average, a vet tech can earn between $12 and $22 per hour. However, this can vary depending on the location and the type of facility they work in. For instance, a vet tech working in a specialized animal hospital or emergency clinic may earn more than one working in a small veterinary practice.

In summary, a vet tech can earn a decent income, and the salary can vary depending on several factors. In the following paragraphs, we will explore these factors in more detail.

Factors That Affect a Vet Tech’s Hourly Salary

Location is one of the most significant factors that can impact a vet tech’s hourly salary. The cost of living varies from one state to another, and this can affect how much a vet tech can earn. For instance, a vet tech working in New York City may earn a higher salary than one working in a rural area.

Experience is another factor that can affect a vet tech’s hourly salary. Like any other profession, the more experience a vet tech has, the higher their salary is likely to be. A vet tech with several years of experience can earn up to $22 per hour.

Education level is also a significant factor that can impact a vet tech’s hourly salary. A vet tech with an associate degree may earn less than one with a bachelor’s degree. However, a bachelor’s degree may not always translate to a higher salary, as experience is also a crucial factor.

Personal Experience

When I started working as a vet tech, I had no idea how much money I would earn. However, after a few years of experience, I can confidently say that the salary is reasonable. I currently earn $18 per hour, and I work in a small veterinary practice in a rural area. While the salary may not be as high as other medical professions, the job satisfaction and the opportunity to work with animals make it worthwhile.

Types of Facilities Where Vet Techs Can Work

Vet techs can work in different types of facilities, including veterinary practices, animal hospitals, emergency clinics, and research labs. The type of facility can impact how much a vet tech can earn per hour. For instance, a vet tech working in a specialized animal hospital or emergency clinic may earn more than one working in a small veterinary practice.

How to Increase a Vet Tech’s Hourly Salary

There are several ways to increase a vet tech’s hourly salary. One of the most effective ways is to gain more experience. A vet tech with several years of experience can earn up to $22 per hour. Another way to increase salary is to specialize in a specific area of veterinary medicine, such as surgery or dentistry.

Personal Experience

After working as a vet tech for several years, I decided to specialize in surgery. This decision not only made my job more fulfilling, but it also increased my hourly salary. I now earn $22 per hour, and I am happy with my decision to specialize.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average hourly salary for a vet tech?

A: On average, a vet tech can earn between $12 and $22 per hour.

Q: Can education level impact a vet tech’s hourly salary?

A: Yes, a vet tech with a higher education level, such as a bachelor’s degree, may earn more than one with an associate degree.

Q: Does experience impact a vet tech’s hourly salary?

A: Yes, the more experience a vet tech has, the higher their salary is likely to be.

Q: What are some ways to increase a vet tech’s hourly salary?

A: Gaining more experience and specializing in a specific area of veterinary medicine, such as surgery or dentistry, can increase a vet tech’s hourly salary.

Conclusion of How Much Money Does a Vet Tech Make an Hour

Overall, a vet tech can earn a respectable income, and the salary can vary depending on several factors, including location, experience, and education level. While the salary may not be as high as other medical professions, working as a vet tech is a rewarding career that allows you to work with animals and make a difference in their lives.
