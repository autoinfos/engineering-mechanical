Is Radiology Tech A Good Career In 2022

Written by Christine Jun 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering a career in radiology technology? Radiology tech is a rapidly growing field that offers many opportunities for growth and development. As technology continues to advance, the demand for skilled radiology techs is increasing. In this blog post, we will explore the benefits of pursuing a career in radiology tech and why it can be a great career choice in 2022.

The Pain Points of Choosing a Career in Radiology Tech

One of the biggest concerns for those considering a career in radiology tech is the level of education and training required. It can take several years of education and hands-on training to become a certified radiology tech. Additionally, the job can be physically demanding, as radiology techs often spend long hours on their feet and may need to lift heavy equipment. Finally, some may worry about the potential exposure to radiation. However, with proper training and safety precautions, the risk of radiation exposure is minimal.

Why Radiology Tech is a Good Career Choice in 2022

Despite the potential challenges, there are many reasons why pursuing a career in radiology tech can be a great choice in 2022. First and foremost, the job outlook for radiology techs is excellent. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of radiologic technologists is projected to grow 7 percent from 2019 to 2029, faster than the average for all occupations. This is due in part to the aging population and the need for diagnostic imaging.

Another benefit of choosing a career in radiology tech is the variety of work settings available. Radiology techs can work in hospitals, clinics, imaging centers, and other medical facilities. They may also have the opportunity to specialize in areas such as mammography, computed tomography (CT), or magnetic resonance imaging (MRI).

Finally, radiology techs are an essential part of the healthcare team. They play a critical role in diagnosing and treating illnesses and injuries, and their work is vital to patient care.

Personal Experience with Radiology Tech as a Career

As a radiology tech, I have found the job to be both challenging and rewarding. The work can be physically demanding, but it is also intellectually stimulating. I enjoy the variety of work settings available and the opportunity to work with a diverse group of patients. Overall, I have found radiology tech to be a fulfilling and satisfying career choice.

The Benefits of Radiology Tech Certification

One of the most significant benefits of becoming a certified radiology tech is the increased job opportunities and earning potential. Certification shows potential employers that you have the knowledge and skills needed to perform the job effectively. Additionally, many employers require certification as a condition of employment. Finally, certification can lead to opportunities for advancement and specialization within the field.

How to Become a Certified Radiology Tech

To become a certified radiology tech, you will need to complete an accredited radiologic technology program. These programs typically take two to four years to complete and include both classroom instruction and hands-on training. Once you have completed your education, you will need to pass a certification exam to become certified. Continuing education is also required to maintain your certification.

Job Opportunities for Radiology Techs

As previously mentioned, the job outlook for radiology techs is excellent. In addition to traditional medical facilities, radiology techs can also find employment in research and development, education, and even government agencies. The average salary for a radiologic technologist is $63,120 per year, with the top 10 percent earning more than $90,760 per year.

Conclusion: Radiology Tech is a Promising Career Choice in 2022

Overall, pursuing a career in radiology tech can be an excellent choice in 2022. With a growing demand for skilled professionals, a variety of work settings available, and opportunities for advancement and specialization, radiology techs are an essential part of the healthcare industry. If you are considering a career in healthcare, radiology tech is definitely worth considering.
