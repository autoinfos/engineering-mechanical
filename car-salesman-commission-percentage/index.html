Understanding Car Salesman Commission Percentage

Written by Frank Aug 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you planning to buy a car? Have you ever wondered how much commission the car salesman makes on the sale? If you're curious about car salesman commission percentage, keep reading to find out more.

Buying a car can be a stressful experience, and one of the biggest stressors is not knowing how much commission the salesperson is making. This lack of transparency can lead to a feeling of distrust and can make buyers feel like they're being taken advantage of.

The commission percentage for car salespeople varies depending on the dealership and the type of car being sold. However, the average commission percentage for car salespeople is between 20% and 30% of the profit made on the sale of the car.

In summary, car salesman commission percentage is the percentage of the profit made on the sale of a car that goes to the salesperson. It can vary depending on the dealership and the type of car being sold.

How Car Salesman Commission Percentage Works

When a car is sold, the dealership makes a profit on the sale. The commission percentage for the salesperson is then calculated based on this profit. For example, if the dealership makes a profit of $5,000 on the sale of a car and the commission percentage for the salesperson is 25%, the salesperson would make $1,250 in commission on the sale.

Personally, I have experienced the commission percentage system when buying a car. During the negotiation process, the salesperson tried to increase the price of the car, which I later found out was to increase their commission percentage. This experience made me realize the importance of understanding commission percentage when buying a car.

The Pros and Cons of Commission Percentage for Car Salespeople

One advantage of commission percentage for car salespeople is that it can provide a strong incentive for them to make more sales. This can lead to a more motivated sales force and better customer service. However, the downside is that it can lead to salespeople pressuring customers into buying more expensive cars or add-ons in order to increase their commission.

The Impact of Commission Percentage on Car Prices

Commission percentage can also have an impact on the price of cars. Dealerships may increase the price of cars in order to make more profit and therefore increase the commission percentage for salespeople. This is why it's important to do your research and negotiate the price of the car before making a purchase.

How to Negotiate Commission Percentage When Buying a Car

If you're buying a car, it's important to negotiate the commission percentage with the salesperson. You can do this by asking for a breakdown of the price and the commission percentage. You can also try to negotiate a lower price for the car in exchange for a lower commission percentage for the salesperson.

Conclusion of Car Salesman Commission Percentage

Car salesman commission percentage is an important factor to consider when buying a car. It can impact the price of the car and the behavior of the salesperson. By understanding how commission percentage works and negotiating with the salesperson, you can ensure that you get the best deal possible on your car purchase.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it possible to negotiate the commission percentage with the dealership?

A: Yes, it's possible to negotiate the commission percentage with the salesperson. This can be done by asking for a breakdown of the price and the commission percentage and negotiating a lower commission percentage in exchange for a lower price for the car.

Q: Can commission percentage lead to salespeople pressuring customers into buying more expensive cars?

A: Yes, commission percentage can lead to salespeople pressuring customers into buying more expensive cars in order to increase their commission. This is why it's important to do your research and negotiate the price of the car before making a purchase.

Q: Is commission percentage the same for all car salespeople?

A: No, commission percentage can vary depending on the dealership and the type of car being sold.

Q: How can I ensure that I get the best deal on my car purchase?

A: By understanding how commission percentage works and negotiating with the salesperson, you can ensure that you get the best deal possible on your car purchase.
