What Is The Average Mechanical Engineer Salary In Dubai Per Month

Written by Christine Oct 26, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to working as a mechanical engineer in Dubai, one of the biggest concerns is undoubtedly the salary. Dubai is known for its high living costs and it can be challenging to make ends meet if you're not earning enough. This blog post will explore the average mechanical engineer salary in Dubai per month and provide you with some insight into what you can expect to earn in this field.

According to recent statistics, the average mechanical engineer salary in Dubai per month is around AED 15,000 to AED 20,000. Of course, this figure can vary depending on a number of factors such as your level of experience, the company you work for, and the industry you're in. Generally speaking, mechanical engineers who work in the oil and gas industry tend to earn higher salaries than those in other industries.

In summary, mechanical engineers in Dubai can expect to earn a decent salary that is in line with the high cost of living in the city. However, it's important to keep in mind that your salary may vary depending on your experience and the industry you work in.

How to Negotiate a Higher Mechanical Engineer Salary in Dubai Per Month

If you're not satisfied with the salary being offered to you, there are a few things you can do to negotiate a higher salary. Firstly, do your research and find out what the average salary is for your level of experience and industry. This will give you a good idea of what you should be earning.

Next, be prepared to justify why you're worth the salary you're asking for. This could be because of your experience, qualifications, or the skills you bring to the table. It's important to be confident and assertive when negotiating your salary, but also be willing to compromise if necessary.

Factors That Affect Mechanical Engineer Salary in Dubai Per Month

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can affect your mechanical engineer salary in Dubai per month. One of the biggest factors is your level of experience. Generally speaking, the more experience you have, the higher your salary will be.

The industry you work in can also have a big impact on your salary. As previously mentioned, mechanical engineers who work in the oil and gas industry tend to earn higher salaries than those in other industries. Other factors that can affect your salary include the size of the company you work for, your qualifications, and your job responsibilities.

How to Find Mechanical Engineer Jobs with High Salaries in Dubai

If you're looking for mechanical engineer jobs with high salaries in Dubai, there are a few things you can do to increase your chances of finding them. Firstly, make sure your resume is up to date and tailored to the job you're applying for. This will help you stand out from other applicants.

Networking is also important when it comes to finding high-paying mechanical engineer jobs in Dubai. Attend industry events and conferences, connect with other professionals on LinkedIn, and reach out to recruiters who specialize in your field.

FAQs About Mechanical Engineer Salary in Dubai Per Month

Q: What is the average starting salary for mechanical engineers in Dubai?
A: The average starting salary for mechanical engineers in Dubai is around AED 8,000 to AED 12,000 per month.

Q: Do mechanical engineers in Dubai receive bonuses?
A: Yes, it's common for mechanical engineers in Dubai to receive bonuses. The amount of the bonus will depend on the company you work for and your performance.

Q: Is it possible to negotiate a higher salary as a mechanical engineer in Dubai?
A: Yes, it's possible to negotiate a higher salary as a mechanical engineer in Dubai. However, it's important to do your research and be prepared to justify why you're worth the salary you're asking for.

Q: What is the highest paying industry for mechanical engineers in Dubai?
A: The oil and gas industry tends to be the highest paying industry for mechanical engineers in Dubai.

Conclusion of Mechanical Engineer Salary in Dubai Per Month

While the average mechanical engineer salary in Dubai per month is around AED 15,000 to AED 20,000, it's important to keep in mind that your salary may vary depending on your level of experience, industry, and qualifications. By doing your research and being prepared to negotiate, you can increase your chances of earning a higher salary in this field.
