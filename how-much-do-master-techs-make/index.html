How Much Do Master Techs Make

Written by Petter Jul 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a master tech and wondering how much you can expect to earn? If so, you're not alone. Many people are interested in becoming a master technician, but want to know more about the salary potential before making a commitment. In this post, we'll explore the earning potential for master techs and what factors can impact their salary.

For many people, salary is a major consideration when choosing a career. It's important to understand that the amount a master tech can earn can vary greatly depending on a number of factors. These factors can include location, experience, certifications, and the type of employer they work for. For this reason, it's difficult to give a straightforward answer to the question of how much do master techs make.

However, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for automotive service technicians and mechanics, which includes master techs, was $44,050 as of May 2020. This means that half of all workers in this category earned more than this amount, while the other half earned less. The lowest 10 percent earned less than $25,560, while the highest 10 percent earned more than $70,820.

In summary, master techs can earn a good living, with many factors impacting their potential salary. The following sections will explore this topic in more detail and provide additional information on how much do master techs make.

Factors That Can Impact Master Tech Salary

As mentioned earlier, there are many factors that can impact how much a master tech can earn. Here are some of the most important:

Location: The geographic location where a master tech works can have a big impact on their earning potential. For example, master techs who work in large cities or affluent areas may be able to command higher salaries than those who work in rural areas or economically depressed regions.

Experience: As with many professions, experience can play a big role in determining a master tech's salary. Those who have been working in the industry for many years and have built up a strong reputation may be able to earn more than those who are just starting out.

Certifications: Master techs who hold additional certifications beyond their basic training may also be able to earn more. Certifications can demonstrate a higher level of expertise and knowledge, which can make an employee more valuable to an employer.

Type of Employer: Whether a master tech works for a dealership, an independent repair shop, or another type of employer can also impact their salary. Generally, those who work for larger, more established companies may be able to earn more than those who work for smaller, less established businesses.

My Personal Experience With Master Tech Salary

As someone who has worked in the automotive industry for many years, I have seen firsthand how much master techs can earn. In my experience, those who work for reputable dealerships or large repair shops can earn upwards of $60,000 or more per year, depending on their experience and certifications. However, those who work for smaller, independent shops may earn less, with salaries ranging from $30,000 to $50,000 per year.

It's important to note that other factors, such as location, can also impact salary. For example, master techs who work in urban areas or affluent regions may be able to command higher salaries than those who work in rural or economically depressed areas.

Master Tech Salary FAQs

Q: Are there any education requirements to become a master tech?

A: While there are no formal education requirements to become a master tech, most employers prefer to hire candidates who have completed a formal training program in automotive technology. Additionally, many master techs hold certifications from organizations such as the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE).

Q: Can master techs earn more by working overtime?

A: Yes, many master techs are paid hourly and can earn overtime pay for working more than 40 hours per week. This can significantly increase their earning potential.

Q: Are there any benefits that come with being a master tech?

A: Yes, many employers offer benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off to their master techs.

Q: Is it possible to make a six-figure salary as a master tech?

A: While it is possible for some master techs to earn six-figure salaries, this is relatively uncommon. Most master techs earn between $40,000 and $70,000 per year.

Conclusion of How Much Do Master Techs Make

As you can see, there is no one-size-fits-all answer to the question of how much do master techs make. However, with the right experience, certifications, and employer, master techs can earn a good living and enjoy a fulfilling career in the automotive industry.
