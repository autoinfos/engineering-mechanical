What Skills and Qualifications are Required for an Automotive Service Advisor Job?

To become an automotive service advisor, you'll need to possess several key skills and qualifications. These include:

  • Excellent communication skills
  • Strong customer service skills
  • Ability to multitask and prioritize
  • Knowledge of automotive services and products
  • Experience in a customer-facing role
  • High school diploma or equivalent

Additionally, some employers may require a post-secondary education in a related field, such as automotive technology or business administration.

If you're interested in pursuing a career as an automotive service advisor, there are several steps you can take to prepare for the role.
