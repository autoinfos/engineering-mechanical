Automotive Exterior Design Jobs Germany A World Of Opportunities

Written by Frank Jul 13, 2023 · 5 min read

When it comes to designing cars, the exterior is just as important as the interior. In fact, the exterior is what catches a potential buyer's eye and draws them in. That's why automotive exterior design jobs in Germany are in high demand. But, finding the right job isn't always easy.

Designing a car's exterior is more than just making it look good. Automotive exterior design jobs in Germany require a keen eye for detail, knowledge of the latest design trends, and the ability to work within a team. It can also be a challenging job, with long hours and tight deadlines. But for those who are passionate about cars and design, it can be a dream career.

If you're interested in automotive exterior design jobs in Germany, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll need to have a degree in industrial design or a related field. You'll also need to be fluent in German and English, as most automotive companies in Germany require both languages. Additionally, having experience in 3D modeling and computer-aided design (CAD) is a must.

In summary, automotive exterior design jobs in Germany are a world of opportunities for those who are passionate about cars and design. But, it's not an easy job, and it requires a lot of hard work and dedication. If you're up for the challenge, it can be a rewarding career.

What is Automotive Exterior Design Jobs Germany?

Automotive exterior design jobs in Germany are focused on designing the exterior of cars. This includes everything from the body shape to the headlights and taillights. The goal is to create a car that is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and safe. Jobs in this field can range from designing concept cars to working on production models.

As someone who has always been interested in cars and design, I knew that automotive exterior design jobs in Germany were the perfect fit for me. After completing my degree in industrial design, I landed a job at a top German automotive company. I've been working there for five years now, and it's been an incredible experience.

Challenges of Automotive Exterior Design Jobs Germany

As mentioned earlier, automotive exterior design jobs in Germany aren't easy. There are a few challenges that come with the job. First, it can be a high-pressure environment with tight deadlines. Additionally, you'll need to be able to work within a team, which can be challenging at times. Finally, the job requires a lot of attention to detail, as even the smallest mistake can have a big impact on the final product.

One of the biggest challenges I've faced in my job is working on concept cars. These are cars that are designed to showcase the latest technology and design trends, but they aren't meant for production. The challenge with these cars is that you have a lot of creative freedom, but there's also a lot of pressure to create something that is truly unique and innovative.

Skills Required for Automotive Exterior Design Jobs Germany

As mentioned earlier, having a degree in industrial design or a related field is a must for automotive exterior design jobs in Germany. Additionally, you'll need to be fluent in German and English, as most automotive companies require both languages. Having experience in 3D modeling and computer-aided design (CAD) is also important.

But, there are a few other skills that are essential for success in this field. First, you'll need to have a strong sense of design and aesthetics. Additionally, you'll need to be able to work well under pressure and within a team. Finally, having good communication skills is essential, as you'll need to be able to communicate your ideas effectively to other members of the team.

How to Get Started in Automotive Exterior Design Jobs Germany

If you're interested in automotive exterior design jobs in Germany, the first step is to get your degree in industrial design or a related field. You'll also need to gain experience in 3D modeling and CAD. Additionally, it's a good idea to start building a portfolio of your work, which you can showcase to potential employers.

Once you have your degree and experience, you can start looking for jobs at automotive companies in Germany. Some of the top companies to look out for include BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and Porsche. You can also look for internships or apprenticeships, which can help you gain valuable experience and connections in the industry.

Conclusion of Automotive Exterior Design Jobs Germany

Automotive exterior design jobs in Germany are a world of opportunities for those who are passionate about cars and design. But, it's not an easy job, and it requires a lot of hard work and dedication. If you're up for the challenge, it can be a rewarding career.
