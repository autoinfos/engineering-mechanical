How Much Do Hvac Tech Make A Year

Written by Frank Jun 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering becoming an HVAC technician? Or are you already in the industry and wondering about your earning potential? Either way, you're in the right place. Today, we're going to answer the question: How much do HVAC tech make a year?

When it comes to HVAC tech, there are several pain points surrounding compensation. Technicians who are paid by the hour may struggle with inconsistent workloads, while those who are salaried may feel as though they are underpaid. Additionally, some HVAC techs may work in high-stress, dangerous environments, which can make them feel as though they should be earning more money.

So, how much do HVAC tech make a year? The answer varies depending on a variety of factors, including experience, location, and the type of work being performed. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for HVAC technicians is $50,590. However, the top 10% of earners in the field make more than $77,920 per year.

In summary, HVAC techs can earn a decent living, with the top earners in the field making a salary that is well above the national average. However, compensation can vary widely depending on several factors, so it's important to do your research and negotiate for fair pay.

How Much Do Entry-Level HVAC Tech Make a Year?

When I started my career as an HVAC technician, I was making around $25,000 per year. This was an entry-level position, and I knew that I would need to work my way up to earn more money. Over time, I gained experience and certifications, which allowed me to negotiate for higher pay.

Entry-level HVAC techs can expect to make around $30,000 to $40,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on the company you work for, the location of the job, and your level of experience.

How Much Do HVAC Tech Make a Year in Different States?

The amount that HVAC tech make a year can vary widely depending on the state in which they work. For example, HVAC technicians in Alaska earn an average of $65,220 per year, while those in Mississippi earn an average of $38,910 per year.

Other states where HVAC techs earn high salaries include California, Massachusetts, and New York. However, it's important to keep in mind that the cost of living in these states may be higher than in other areas, which can offset the higher salary.

Factors That Affect How Much Do HVAC Tech Make a Year

There are several factors that can affect how much HVAC techs make each year. These include:

  • Experience
  • Certifications
  • Location
  • Type of work being performed (residential vs. commercial, for example)
  • Company size and type

By taking these factors into account, HVAC techs can get a better understanding of their earning potential and negotiate for fair pay.

How to Negotiate for a Higher Salary as an HVAC Tech

If you feel as though you are underpaid as an HVAC technician, there are several strategies you can use to negotiate for a higher salary. These include:

  • Researching industry standards for your position
  • Gathering evidence of your skills and accomplishments
  • Presenting a clear case for why you deserve a raise
  • Being willing to compromise and find a solution that works for both you and your employer

How Much Do HVAC Tech Make a Year in the Future?

The job outlook for HVAC technicians is positive, with the Bureau of Labor Statistics projecting that employment in the field will grow 4% from 2019 to 2029. This growth is due in part to the increasing need for energy-efficient HVAC systems and the growing number of buildings that require climate control.

As the demand for HVAC technicians grows, it's likely that salaries will continue to rise as well. However, it's important for technicians to stay up-to-date with industry trends and continue to develop their skills in order to remain competitive in the job market.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average hourly wage for HVAC technicians?

A: According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the average hourly wage for HVAC technicians is $24.31.

Q: How much do HVAC technicians make in the highest-paying state?

A: HVAC technicians in Alaska earn an average of $65,220 per year.

Q: How much do experienced HVAC technicians make?

A: Experienced HVAC technicians can make upwards of $77,920 per year.

Q: Do HVAC technicians get benefits?

A: Many HVAC technicians receive benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off.

Conclusion of How Much Do HVAC Tech Make a Year

Overall, HVAC technicians can earn a decent living with the potential to earn a high salary. However, compensation can vary widely depending on a variety of factors, including location, experience, and certifications. By staying up-to-date with industry trends and negotiating for fair pay, HVAC techs can ensure that they are compensated fairly for their hard work.
