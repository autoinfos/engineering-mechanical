What Does a Deck Cadet Do?

As a deck cadet, your responsibilities will vary depending on the ship you are working on and the specific needs of the crew. Some of the most common duties include:

1. Monitoring Weather Conditions

One of the most important tasks of a deck cadet is monitoring weather conditions. This involves keeping a close eye on the forecast and making adjustments to the ship's course as needed to avoid storms or other hazards.

2. Maintaining Equipment

Deck cadets are also responsible for maintaining the ship's equipment, including lifeboats, winches, and other machinery. This involves regular inspections and repairs as needed to keep everything in working order.

3. Navigation

Deck cadets assist with navigation by plotting courses, using charts and other tools to determine the ship's position, and communicating with other crew members to ensure a safe and efficient journey.

2. Gain Experience

Once you have completed your training, you will need to gain experience working on a ship. This can involve working as an ordinary seaman or in another entry-level position before moving up to the role of deck cadet.

3. Apply for Deck Cadet Jobs
