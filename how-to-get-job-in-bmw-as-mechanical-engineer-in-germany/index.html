How To Get A Job In Bmw As A Mechanical Engineer In Germany

Written by Robby Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a mechanical engineer looking for a job in Germany, BMW is one of the best companies to work for. However, the process of getting a job there can be daunting. In this blog post, we will explore the steps you can take to increase your chances of landing a job at BMW as a mechanical engineer in Germany.

The first step in getting a job at BMW is to ensure that your education and skills are up to par. BMW is known for its high-quality engineering, so having a strong educational background in mechanical engineering is essential. Additionally, having experience in the automotive industry can be a big plus.

Next, you should research the different job opportunities available at BMW. Check their website and job portals for open positions that match your skills and experience. Make sure to tailor your CV and cover letter to the specific job you are applying for.

Networking is also crucial when trying to get a job at BMW. Attend job fairs and industry events to meet people who work at the company. You can also reach out to alumni from your university who work at BMW and ask for advice or referrals.

Personal Experience

When I was looking for a job at BMW, I attended a job fair and was able to meet a few engineers who worked at the company. We exchanged contact information and I followed up with them after the event. One of them was able to refer me for a job opening in my field, which ultimately led to me getting the job.

Additional Qualifications

BMW values engineers who have additional qualifications such as language skills and international experience. If you speak German or another language and have experience working in other countries, make sure to highlight that in your CV and cover letter.

Online Presence

In today's digital age, having a strong online presence can also help you stand out to potential employers. Make sure your LinkedIn profile is up-to-date and includes relevant keywords for the job you are applying for. You can also create a personal website or blog to showcase your work and skills.

Internship Opportunities

One way to increase your chances of getting a job at BMW is to start with an internship. BMW offers a variety of internships for mechanical engineers, which can be a great way to gain experience and learn more about the company culture. Internships can also lead to full-time job offers.

Personal Experience

When I was in college, I did an internship at BMW and it was a great experience. I was able to work on real projects and gained valuable skills. After the internship, I was offered a full-time job at the company.

Conclusion of How to Get a Job in BMW as a Mechanical Engineer in Germany

Getting a job at BMW as a mechanical engineer in Germany requires a strong educational background, relevant skills and experience, networking, and a strong online presence. Additionally, considering internships and highlighting additional qualifications such as language skills and international experience can increase your chances of landing a job at BMW. By following these steps, you can increase your chances of landing your dream job at BMW.
