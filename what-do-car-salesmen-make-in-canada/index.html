What Do Car Salesmen Make In Canada

Written by Robby Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a car salesman in Canada? If so, you may be wondering about the potential earnings and what you can expect to make. It's a common question, and one that deserves a thorough answer. So, let's dive into the details of what car salesmen make in Canada.

As a car salesman, there are a few factors that can impact your earnings. One of the biggest factors is the dealership where you work. Some dealerships pay a straight commission, while others offer a base salary plus commission. Additionally, the type of vehicles you sell can also play a role. High-end luxury vehicles typically have a higher commission rate than more affordable options.

On average, a car salesman in Canada can expect to make around $40,000 per year. However, this number can vary greatly depending on the factors mentioned above. Some car salesmen can make upwards of $100,000 per year, while others may only make around $20,000.

In summary, what car salesmen make in Canada can vary greatly depending on the dealership and type of vehicles they sell. However, on average, car salesmen can expect to make around $40,000 per year.

My Personal Experience

When I first started as a car salesman, I was making a base salary of $30,000 per year plus commission. However, I quickly learned that the real money came from selling high-end luxury vehicles. I worked hard to establish myself as the go-to salesman for luxury cars at my dealership, and it paid off. Within a year, I was making over $80,000 per year.

The Importance of Negotiation

One of the key skills for any car salesman is negotiation. Being able to negotiate effectively can mean the difference between making a sale and losing a customer. It's important to understand the value of the vehicle you're selling and be able to articulate that value to potential buyers. Additionally, being able to read customers and understand their needs can help you negotiate a deal that works for both parties.

Understanding Commission Structures

As mentioned earlier, commission structures can vary greatly between dealerships. It's important to understand the commission structure at your dealership and how it impacts your earnings. Some dealerships may offer a higher commission rate for selling certain types of vehicles, while others may offer a higher base salary. Understanding these details can help you make informed decisions about your career as a car salesman.

Tips for Success

If you're looking to succeed as a car salesman, there are a few key tips to keep in mind. First, establish yourself as an expert in the vehicles you sell. Know the features and benefits of each model, and be able to answer any questions potential buyers may have. Additionally, focus on building relationships with your customers. Repeat business and referrals can be a major source of income for car salesmen.

Conclusion of What Do Car Salesmen Make in Canada

Overall, what car salesmen make in Canada can vary greatly depending on a number of factors. However, with hard work and a focus on building relationships, it's possible to earn a comfortable living as a car salesman. Remember to negotiate effectively and understand the commission structure at your dealership, and you'll be on your way to success in this exciting career.

Question and Answer

A: The average salary for a car salesman in Canada is around $40,000 per year.

A: Yes, some car salesmen can make upwards of $100,000 per year.

A: Negotiation skills, product knowledge, and relationship building are all important for success as a car salesman.

A: The type of dealership, commission structure, and type of vehicles sold can all impact a car salesman's earnings.
