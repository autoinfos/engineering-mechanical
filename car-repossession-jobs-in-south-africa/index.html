The Laws and Regulations of Car Repossession in South Africa

In South Africa, car repossession is governed by the National Credit Act. This act sets out the procedures that must be followed when repossessing a vehicle, including the notice that must be given to the debtor before repossession can take place. Additionally, the act sets out the requirements for the sale of repossessed vehicles.
