Theatre Technician Salary In Kenya What You Need To Know

Written by Frank Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever wondered how much a theatre technician makes in Kenya? Are you considering a career in theatre and want to know what your earning potential could be? Look no further, because in this blog post we will be discussing everything you need to know about theatre technician salary in Kenya.

Theatre technicians are responsible for the technical aspects of a theatrical production. This can include lighting, sound, stage management, and set design. While these roles are crucial to the success of any production, they often do not receive the recognition or compensation they deserve.

So, how much can you expect to make as a theatre technician in Kenya? The average salary for a theatre technician in Kenya is around Ksh. 50,000 per month. However, this can vary depending on your level of experience, the production company you work for, and the location of the theatre.

In summary, theatre technician salary in Kenya can range from Ksh. 30,000 to Ksh. 100,000 per month, depending on various factors. However, it is important to note that theatre technicians often work irregular hours, including evenings and weekends, and may have to travel for productions.

Personal Experience with Theatre Technician Salary in Kenya

As someone who has worked as a theatre technician in Kenya for several years, I can attest to the fact that the salary can be quite modest. However, the job is incredibly rewarding in terms of the experience gained and the opportunity to work on exciting productions.

It is also worth noting that networking and building relationships within the theatre community can lead to more job opportunities and potentially higher salaries in the long run.

Advancement Opportunities for Theatre Technicians in Kenya

While theatre technician salaries may not be high, there are opportunities for advancement within the industry. For example, experienced theatre technicians may be able to take on supervisory roles or move into stage management positions, which can come with higher salaries.

Skills Needed for a Career in Theatre Technician

In order to succeed as a theatre technician in Kenya, there are several skills that are essential. These include:

  • Technical knowledge of lighting, sound, and stage management
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Attention to detail
  • Ability to work under pressure
  • Flexibility and adaptability

How to Get Started in a Career as a Theatre Technician in Kenya

Starting a career as a theatre technician in Kenya can be challenging, but there are several steps you can take to get started. These include:

  • Getting a degree or diploma in theatre or a related field
  • Volunteering at local theatres to gain experience and make connections
  • Building a portfolio of work to showcase your skills
  • Networking within the theatre community to find job opportunities

Is a Career as a Theatre Technician in Kenya Right for You?

Ultimately, whether or not a career as a theatre technician is right for you will depend on your interests, skills, and priorities. While the salary may not be high, the job can be incredibly fulfilling for those who are passionate about theatre and the arts.
