Exploring Mechanical Engineer Jobs In Dubai 2021

Written by Aprilia Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a mechanical engineer looking for job opportunities in Dubai? Look no further! Dubai has been a hub of opportunities for engineers from all over the world, and 2021 is no exception. With numerous projects in the pipeline, the city is in constant need of skilled mechanical engineers who can help bring these projects to life. In this blog post, we'll explore the current state of mechanical engineer jobs in Dubai and how you can make the most of the opportunities available.

Mechanical engineers in Dubai face unique challenges and opportunities. On one hand, there is a high demand for skilled professionals, but on the other hand, there is also intense competition for jobs. Additionally, many mechanical engineers may find it difficult to navigate the job market in a foreign country, especially when it comes to finding the right job that matches their skills and experience.

So, what should you know about mechanical engineer jobs in Dubai in 2021? Firstly, it's important to be aware of the skills and experience that companies are looking for. Most companies are looking for mechanical engineers with experience in project management, design, and analysis. Additionally, knowledge of industry-specific software and tools is highly valued.

To increase your chances of success in finding a mechanical engineer job in Dubai in 2021, it's important to highlight your experience and skills in your resume and cover letter. Additionally, networking can be a crucial part of the job search process in Dubai. Attend industry events and connect with professionals through social media platforms like LinkedIn to expand your network and learn about job opportunities.

Job Market and Salary Expectations

The job market for mechanical engineers in Dubai is highly competitive, but there are numerous job opportunities available in various industries, including construction, manufacturing, and energy. The average salary for a mechanical engineer in Dubai is around AED 95,000 per year, but this can vary depending on experience, skills, and industry.

Visa Requirements and Legal Considerations

To work as a mechanical engineer in Dubai, you will need a valid work visa. Most companies in Dubai sponsor work visas for their employees, but it's important to be aware of the legal requirements and restrictions. Additionally, it's important to be aware of the cultural and social norms in Dubai to ensure a smooth transition to living and working in the city.

Top Companies Hiring Mechanical Engineers in Dubai 2021

Some of the top companies hiring mechanical engineers in Dubai in 2021 include:

  • Siemens
  • GE
  • Emirates Global Aluminium
  • Al Futtaim Group
  • ABB

How to Stand Out in the Job Market

To stand out in the competitive job market in Dubai, it's important to showcase your unique skills and experience. Highlight your experience in project management, design, and analysis, and include any industry-specific software and tools you are proficient in. Additionally, consider obtaining certifications or additional training to expand your skillset and make yourself a more attractive candidate to potential employers.

Personal Experience

As a mechanical engineer who has worked in Dubai for over five years, I can attest to the numerous job opportunities available in the city. However, it's important to be prepared for the competitive job market and to showcase your skills and experience in your job search. Networking has also been a crucial part of my job search process and has helped me expand my professional network and learn about job opportunities.
