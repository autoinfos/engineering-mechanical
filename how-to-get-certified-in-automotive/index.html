How To Get Certified In Automotive A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 15, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you interested in working in the automotive industry but don’t know where to start? Perhaps you’re already working in the industry and want to take the next step in your career. Whatever your situation may be, getting certified in automotive is a great way to gain the knowledge and skills you need to succeed. However, the process can be overwhelming for many. In this post, we’ll break down how to get certified in automotive in an easy-to-understand way.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to getting certified in automotive is knowing where to start. With so many options available, it can be difficult to know which certifications are necessary for your career path. Additionally, the process of obtaining these certifications can be time-consuming and expensive. However, the benefits of becoming certified far outweigh the challenges.

The first step to getting certified in automotive is to research the different types of certifications available. There are several organizations that offer certifications in the automotive industry, including ASE (Automotive Service Excellence), I-CAR (Inter-Industry Conference on Auto Collision Repair), and NATEF (National Automotive Technicians Education Foundation). Each organization offers different types of certifications depending on your specific career goals.

To obtain a certification, you must meet certain requirements, such as completing a certain number of hours of training or passing an exam. The requirements vary depending on the certification you are pursuing. It’s important to carefully review the requirements to ensure that you meet them before applying for certification.

In summary, getting certified in automotive can be a valuable investment in your career. By researching the different types of certifications available and meeting the necessary requirements, you can gain the knowledge and skills you need to succeed in the industry. Additionally, becoming certified can increase your earning potential and job opportunities.

ASE Certification

ASE Certification is one of the most recognized certifications in the automotive industry. This certification is offered by the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) and is designed for automotive professionals who want to demonstrate their knowledge and skills in the field. ASE Certification is available in several different areas, including engine repair, brakes, electrical systems, and more.

Personally, I obtained ASE Certification in engine repair early in my career. It was a challenging process, but ultimately worth it. I found that the knowledge and skills I gained through the certification process helped me become a better technician and advance in my career.

To obtain ASE Certification, you must meet certain requirements, such as having at least two years of work experience in the field and passing an exam. The exam is computer-based and consists of multiple-choice questions. The questions are designed to test your knowledge and skills in the specific area of certification you are pursuing.

I-CAR Certification

I-CAR Certification is designed for automotive professionals who specialize in collision repair. This certification is offered by the Inter-Industry Conference on Auto Collision Repair (I-CAR) and is designed to ensure that technicians have the knowledge and skills necessary to properly repair vehicles that have been in accidents.

I personally do not have I-CAR Certification, as I have focused my career on engine repair rather than collision repair. However, I have worked with many technicians who have obtained this certification and have seen firsthand the benefits it can bring to their career.

To obtain I-CAR Certification, you must complete a series of training courses and pass an exam. The courses cover topics such as collision repair fundamentals, structural analysis and repair, and painting and refinishing. The exam is designed to test your knowledge and skills in these areas.

NATEF Certification

NATEF Certification is designed for automotive professionals who work in education and training. This certification is offered by the National Automotive Technicians Education Foundation (NATEF) and is designed to ensure that automotive instructors have the knowledge and skills necessary to properly train future technicians.

While I do not have NATEF Certification myself, I have worked with many instructors who have obtained this certification. I have found that instructors who are NATEF Certified are often more knowledgeable and skilled in their teaching methods.

To obtain NATEF Certification, you must meet certain requirements, such as having at least two years of experience as an automotive instructor and passing an exam. The exam is designed to test your knowledge and skills in areas such as teaching methods, curriculum development, and student assessment.

Manufacturer-Specific Certification

Manufacturer-Specific Certification is designed for automotive professionals who work with a specific brand of vehicle, such as Ford or Toyota. This certification is offered by the manufacturer and is designed to ensure that technicians have the knowledge and skills necessary to properly repair vehicles made by that manufacturer.

Personally, I have obtained manufacturer-specific certification for several different brands of vehicles throughout my career. I have found that this type of certification can be especially valuable if you work for a dealership or independent repair shop that specializes in a particular brand of vehicle.

To obtain manufacturer-specific certification, you must meet certain requirements, such as completing a certain number of hours of training and passing an exam. The requirements vary depending on the manufacturer and the type of certification you are pursuing.

Question and Answer

Q: How long does it take to get certified in automotive?

A: The length of time it takes to get certified in automotive varies depending on the type of certification you are pursuing. Some certifications can be obtained in a matter of weeks, while others may take several months or even years to complete.

Q: How much does it cost to get certified in automotive?

A: The cost of getting certified in automotive varies depending on the type of certification you are pursuing. Some certifications may be relatively inexpensive, while others may cost thousands of dollars in training and exam fees.

Q: Do I need to be certified to work in the automotive industry?

A: While certification is not always required to work in the automotive industry, it can be a valuable asset to have. Many employers prefer to hire technicians who are certified, and certification can also increase your earning potential and job opportunities.

Q: Can I obtain certification online?

A: While some aspects of the certification process may be available online, such as training courses and practice exams, most certifications require in-person testing at a designated testing center.

Conclusion of How to Get Certified in Automotive

Getting certified in automotive can be a valuable investment in your career. By researching the different types of certifications available and meeting the necessary requirements, you can gain the knowledge and skills you need to succeed in the industry. Whether you’re just starting out in the field or looking to take the next step in your career, obtaining certification can help you achieve your goals.
