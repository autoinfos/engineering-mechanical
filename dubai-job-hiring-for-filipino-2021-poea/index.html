Are you a Filipino looking for job opportunities abroad? Have you considered working in Dubai, United Arab Emirates? Dubai is a bustling city known for its luxurious lifestyle, towering skyscrapers, and diverse culture. With the recent job openings and hiring trends, Dubai has become a popular destination for overseas workers, especially for Filipinos. In this article, we will discuss the current job market in Dubai and the opportunities available for Filipino job seekers.

Looking for a job abroad can be a daunting task, especially for those who have no prior experience or connections in the country. The process of searching for job openings, applying for visas, and preparing for interviews can be time-consuming and stressful. Moreover, the language barrier and cultural differences can pose challenges for foreign workers.

If you are a Filipino looking for job opportunities in Dubai, you can apply through the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA), which is the government agency responsible for regulating and monitoring overseas employment of Filipino workers. The POEA has a list of licensed recruitment agencies that offer job openings in Dubai and other countries. You can also visit the POEA website for the latest job postings and requirements.

In summary, Dubai offers various job opportunities for Filipino workers in different industries such as healthcare, hospitality, construction, and finance. With the help of the POEA and licensed recruitment agencies, Filipino job seekers can apply for jobs in Dubai with ease and confidence. Here are some tips to increase your chances of getting hired:

Tip #1: Update your resume and cover letter

Make sure that your resume and cover letter are tailored to the job you are applying for. Highlight your skills, experiences, and achievements that are relevant to the position. Use keywords and phrases that are commonly used in the job description.

Tip #2: Research the company and industry

Before applying for a job, research the company and industry to gain insights on their values, culture, and business practices. This will help you prepare for the interview and demonstrate your knowledge and interest in the job.

Tip #3: Network with people in Dubai

Networking is a valuable tool in job hunting. Try to connect with people who are working in your desired industry or company in Dubai. You can join online groups or attend job fairs and events to expand your network.

Tip #4: Be prepared for the interview

Practice common interview questions and prepare your responses. Dress appropriately and arrive on time. Show confidence and enthusiasm during the interview.

What are the common job openings for Filipinos in Dubai?

Filipino workers are in demand in Dubai for their skills, work ethic, and adaptability. The most common job openings for Filipinos in Dubai are in the healthcare, hospitality, construction, and finance industries. Some of the job positions available are nurses, hotel staff, engineers, accountants, and sales representatives.

What are the requirements for working in Dubai?

To work in Dubai, you need to have a valid passport, work visa, and residency permit. You also need to pass a medical examination and obtain a police clearance certificate. Your employer will provide you with the necessary documents and assist you in the visa application process.

How much can you earn working in Dubai?

The salary range in Dubai varies depending on the industry, job position, and experience level. The minimum wage for workers in Dubai is 1,000 AED (approximately 13,600 PHP) per month. However, some skilled workers can earn up to 20,000 AED (approximately 272,000 PHP) per month.

Conclusion of Dubai Job Hiring for Filipino 2021 POEA

Working in Dubai can be a rewarding experience for Filipino workers who are looking for job opportunities abroad. With the help of the POEA and licensed recruitment agencies, Filipino job seekers can have access to various job openings in Dubai. By following the tips mentioned above and preparing well for the job application process, you can increase your chances of getting hired and starting a new chapter in your career.
