Signs That Your Car's Brakes Need Repair

It's essential to pay attention to any signs that indicate your car's brakes need repair. Some of the signs include:

  • Screeching or grinding noise when you apply the brakes
  • Difficulty in stopping your car
  • A vibrating brake pedal
  • A warning light on your dashboard
