Certified Electronics Technician Salary What To Expect

Written by William Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering a career as a certified electronics technician, you may be wondering about the potential salary you could earn. After all, compensation is a major factor in any career decision. In this blog post, we'll explore the topic of certified electronics technician salary and what you can expect in terms of earnings.

Before we dive into the details, it's important to acknowledge that salary can vary greatly based on a number of factors. These can include your level of experience, location, and the specific industry you work in. With that said, there are some general trends that can give you a sense of what a typical certified electronics technician salary might look like.

According to the United States Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for electrical and electronics engineering technicians (which includes certified electronics technicians) was $65,260 as of May 2020. The lowest 10% of earners in this field made less than $39,810, while the highest 10% earned more than $97,870.

My Experience with Certified Electronics Technician Salary

As someone who has worked as a certified electronics technician for several years, I can attest to the fact that salary can vary widely. In my early days on the job, I was making around $40,000 annually. However, as I gained more experience and took on more responsibilities, my salary increased to around $75,000.

It's worth noting that I work in the telecommunications industry, which tends to pay higher salaries for certified electronics technicians compared to other industries. Additionally, I live in a major metropolitan area where salaries tend to be higher across the board.

Factors That Impact Certified Electronics Technician Salary

As mentioned earlier, there are a number of factors that can influence how much money you can expect to make as a certified electronics technician. Here are a few to keep in mind:

Experience

As with any career, experience can play a big role in determining your salary. Generally speaking, the more years of experience you have as a certified electronics technician, the higher your earning potential.

Industry

As I mentioned earlier, the industry you work in can have a big impact on your salary. Some industries, such as telecommunications or aerospace, tend to pay higher salaries for certified electronics technicians than others.

The Future of Certified Electronics Technician Salary

So, what can we expect in terms of certified electronics technician salary in the future? While it's impossible to predict with certainty, there are some trends that suggest salaries may continue to rise. For example, the demand for electronics technicians is expected to grow in the coming years as new technologies continue to emerge.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it possible to make six figures as a certified electronics technician?

A: While it's not common, it is possible to earn a six-figure salary as a certified electronics technician. This typically requires many years of experience and a high level of expertise in a specialized area.

Q: Do certified electronics technicians typically receive benefits?

A: Yes, many certified electronics technicians receive benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off.

Q: Are there opportunities for advancement as a certified electronics technician?

A: Yes, there are opportunities for advancement in this field. Some certified electronics technicians go on to become supervisors or managers, while others become specialists in a particular area of electronics.

Q: Are there any downsides to working as a certified electronics technician?

A: Like any job, there are pros and cons to working as a certified electronics technician. Some potential downsides include the need to keep up with rapidly changing technology, the potential for physical strain from standing or working in awkward positions, and the possibility of working on call or overnight shifts.

Conclusion of Certified Electronics Technician Salary

Overall, certified electronics technician salary can vary widely based on a number of factors. However, with the right experience, expertise, and industry knowledge, it is possible to earn a comfortable living in this field. If you're interested in pursuing a career as a certified electronics technician, it's worth doing your research to understand the potential salary and career growth opportunities that may be available to you.
