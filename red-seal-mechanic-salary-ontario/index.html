What is a Red Seal Mechanic?

A Red Seal Mechanic is a certified tradesperson who has completed their apprenticeship and passed a standardized exam. This certification is recognized across Canada and allows mechanics to work in any province or territory without having to complete additional certification requirements.

As a Red Seal Mechanic, you will have a wide range of knowledge and skills in areas such as engine repair, electrical systems, brakes, and suspension. These skills are in high demand and can lead to a rewarding career.

How to Become a Red Seal Mechanic in Ontario

To become a Red Seal Mechanic in Ontario, you will need to complete an apprenticeship program and pass a standardized exam. The apprenticeship program typically takes four years to complete and involves a combination of on-the-job training and in-class instruction.

Once you have completed your apprenticeship, you will need to pass the Red Seal exam. This exam is standardized across Canada and covers a wide range of topics related to the trade.
