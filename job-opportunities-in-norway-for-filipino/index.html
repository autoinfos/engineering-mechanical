Job Opportunities In Norway For Filipino A Guide

Written by William Aug 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Have you been dreaming of working abroad but don't know where to start? Norway might be the perfect destination for you. With its strong economy, high standard of living, and beautiful scenery, Norway offers a lot of opportunities for Filipinos looking for a better life. In this post, we'll explore the job opportunities in Norway for Filipino and what you need to know to start your journey.

Challenges of Job Hunting in Norway for Filipinos

As a Filipino, you might face some challenges when looking for a job in Norway. One of the biggest challenges is the language barrier. Norwegian is the official language in Norway, and most employers require their employees to speak Norwegian. Another challenge is the competition. Norway has a small population, but it's a highly educated one. You'll be competing with not only Norwegians but also other foreigners for job positions. Lastly, you might face some cultural differences that might make it hard to adjust to the work environment.

Target: Job Opportunities in Norway for Filipino

If you're a Filipino looking for a job in Norway, you'll be happy to know that there are many opportunities available. Norway has a shortage of skilled workers in various industries, including IT, engineering, healthcare, and construction. If you have experience in any of these fields, you might have a higher chance of finding a job in Norway.

Another option is to apply for a job in the shipping or oil and gas industry. Norway is one of the largest exporters of oil and gas in the world and has a thriving shipping industry. These industries offer a lot of job opportunities for Filipinos with the right skills and experience.

Personal Experience: Job Hunting in Norway

I moved to Norway from the Philippines a few years ago to start a new life. At first, it was challenging to find a job because of the language barrier. But I decided to enroll in Norwegian language classes to improve my language skills, and it paid off. I found a job in the healthcare industry, and I'm now living my dream life in Norway. My advice to other Filipinos looking for a job in Norway is to keep an open mind, learn the language, and never give up.

Visa Requirements and Work Permits

If you're a Filipino citizen, you'll need a work permit to work in Norway. To apply for a work permit, you'll need to have a job offer from a Norwegian employer. You'll also need to meet certain qualifications, such as having the required education and work experience. You can find more information about the visa requirements and work permits on the Norwegian Directorate of Immigration website.

Cost of Living in Norway

Norway is known for its high cost of living. The prices of goods and services, including housing, food, and transportation, can be quite expensive. However, salaries in Norway are also high, and the country has a strong welfare system that provides a lot of benefits to its citizens and residents. It's important to do your research and plan accordingly before moving to Norway.

Tips for Job Hunting in Norway

If you're looking for a job in Norway, here are some tips to help you increase your chances of success:

  • Learn Norwegian: As mentioned earlier, most employers require their employees to speak Norwegian. Taking Norwegian language classes will not only improve your language skills but also show your commitment to learning about the Norwegian culture.
  • Network: Attend job fairs and networking events to meet people in your industry. You can also join online forums and groups to connect with other professionals.
  • Be flexible: Be open to different job opportunities and locations. Sometimes, the job you're looking for might not be available in the city you want to live in. Consider different locations and industries to increase your chances of success.

Question and Answer

Q: What are the most in-demand jobs in Norway for Filipinos?

A: Some of the most in-demand jobs in Norway for Filipinos are in the healthcare, IT, engineering, construction, shipping, and oil and gas industries.

Q: How long does it take to get a work permit in Norway?

A: The processing time for a work permit in Norway can vary depending on your situation. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months.

Q: Can I bring my family with me to Norway if I get a job there?

A: Yes, you can bring your spouse and children with you to Norway if you have a job and a work permit. They can also apply for family reunification visas.

Q: Can I apply for a work permit in Norway without a job offer?

A: No, you need a job offer from a Norwegian employer to apply for a work permit in Norway.

Conclusion of Job Opportunities in Norway for Filipino

Job hunting in a new country can be challenging, but with the right skills and mindset, it's possible to find a job in Norway as a Filipino. Remember to keep an open mind, learn the language, and be flexible in your job search. With its strong economy and high standard of living, Norway offers a lot of opportunities for Filipinos looking for a better life.
