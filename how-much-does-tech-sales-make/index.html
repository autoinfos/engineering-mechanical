How Much Does Tech Sales Make

Written by Petter Jul 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you interested in a career in tech sales? Or are you already in the industry and wondering how much you can expect to make? Either way, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll explore the world of tech sales salaries and give you a better understanding of what you can expect to earn. So, let's dive in!

As with any industry, there are certain pain points that come with a career in tech sales. One of the biggest concerns for sales professionals is their earnings potential. With so many variables at play, it can be difficult to determine how much you can realistically expect to make. Additionally, the commission-based nature of many sales roles can create a lot of uncertainty around earnings from month to month.

So, how much does tech sales make? The answer, as with many things in life, is: it depends. There are a number of factors that can influence a tech sales professional's earnings, including their experience level, the company they work for, the products they sell, and the region they work in.

That being said, there are some general trends that we can observe when it comes to tech sales salaries. According to Glassdoor, the average base salary for a tech sales professional in the United States is around $68,000 per year. However, when you factor in commissions and bonuses, that number can easily reach six figures.

Personal Experience with Tech Sales Salaries

When I first started my career in tech sales, I was making around $50,000 per year in base salary. However, as I gained more experience and built up my client base, my earnings increased significantly. Now, after several years in the industry, I'm making well over six figures in total compensation.

One thing to keep in mind is that your earnings potential in tech sales is largely tied to your ability to sell. If you're able to consistently exceed your sales quotas and bring in new business, you'll be in a good position to negotiate for higher compensation.

Tech Sales Salaries by Region

Another factor that can impact your earnings in tech sales is your location. Generally speaking, sales professionals in major tech hubs like San Francisco and New York City can expect to earn more than those in smaller cities or less tech-focused regions.

For example, according to Glassdoor, the average base salary for a tech sales professional in San Francisco is around $77,000 per year, compared to $62,000 per year in Dallas, Texas. Of course, these numbers can vary widely based on a number of other factors, but they do give us a general idea of how location can impact earnings.

Factors that Impact Tech Sales Salaries

As we mentioned earlier, there are a number of factors that can influence a tech sales professional's earnings. Here are a few of the most important:

  • Experience level: Generally speaking, more experienced sales professionals will earn more than those who are just starting out.
  • Company size: Larger companies may offer higher compensation packages than smaller ones, although this can vary widely.
  • Product type: Selling high-margin products or services can lead to higher earnings potential.
  • Location: As we mentioned earlier, sales professionals in major tech hubs can generally expect to earn more than those in smaller cities or less tech-focused regions.

Tips for Maximizing Your Earnings in Tech Sales

If you're looking to boost your earnings in tech sales, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Focus on building strong relationships with your clients and prospects.
  • Stay up-to-date on industry trends and developments.
  • Be willing to take risks and try new approaches to selling.
  • Continuously work to improve your sales skills and techniques.

Conclusion of How Much Does Tech Sales Make

So, how much does tech sales make? As we've seen, the answer can vary widely based on a number of factors. However, with the right skills, experience, and location, you can expect to earn a comfortable living in this exciting and dynamic industry. By focusing on building strong relationships with your clients, staying up-to-date on industry trends, and continuously improving your sales skills, you can maximize your earnings potential and build a successful career in tech sales.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average base salary for a tech sales professional?

A: According to Glassdoor, the average base salary for a tech sales professional in the United States is around $68,000 per year. However, this can vary widely based on a number of factors.

Q: What factors can impact a tech sales professional's earnings?

A: Some of the most important factors include experience level, company size, product type, and location.

Q: How can I maximize my earnings potential in tech sales?

A: By building strong relationships with clients, staying up-to-date on industry trends, and continuously improving your sales skills, you can position yourself for higher earnings in tech sales.

Q: Is it possible to earn six figures in tech sales?

A: Yes, it is definitely possible to earn six figures in tech sales. However, this will typically require a combination of experience, skill, and a willingness to take risks and try new approaches to selling.
