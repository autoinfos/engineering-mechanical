FAQs About Auto Repair Near Me Billings Missouri

Q: How often should I get my oil changed?

A: It is recommended to get your oil changed every 3,000 miles or every three months, whichever comes first.

Q: How do I know if my brakes need repair?

A: Signs that your brakes may need repair include squeaking or grinding noises, shaking or vibration when braking, and a longer stopping distance.

Q: How often should I get my tires rotated?

A: It is recommended to get your tires rotated every 6,000 miles or every six months, whichever comes first.

Q: What should I do if my check engine light comes on?

A: If your check engine light comes on, it is important to have it checked by a professional mechanic as soon as possible. Ignoring the problem could lead to more significant issues and costly repairs.
